1. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Prefrontal cortex (PFC)
    - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
        - Left superior OFC
        - Right medial OFC
        - Left medial OFC
    - Superior frontal gyrus

2. **Striatum**
    - Putamen
        - Bilateral putamen
        - Left putamen
    - Caudate nucleus

3. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**
    - Bilateral SMA

4. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Left postcentral gyrus

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Left anterior cingulate (ACC)

6. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

7. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: cognitive control, inhibitory control, impulsivity, decision making, habitual behaviors, and goal-directed actions.
