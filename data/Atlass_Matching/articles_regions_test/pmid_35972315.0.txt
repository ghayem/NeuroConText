## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Anterior Insula**

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**

3. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
    - Subgenual ACC
    - Pregenual ACC
    - Supracallosal ACC

4. **Temporoparietal Junction**

5. **Supplementary Motor Area**
    - Left supplementary motor area
    - Right supplementary motor area

6. **Occipital Gyrus**
    - Middle occipital gyrus (MOG)
    - Superior occipital gyrus (SOG)
    - Cuneus

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as empathy, emotional regulation, and visuospatial information processing.
