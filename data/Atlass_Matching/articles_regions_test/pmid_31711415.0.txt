1. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Frontal motor cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Precentral gyrus

2. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Precuneus (Pcu)
   - Postcentral gyrus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus (PHG)

3. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Lingual gyrus (LG)
   - Cuneus (CU)

   - Visual association cortex

4. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Parahippocampal gyrus (PHG)

5. **Limbic Lobe**
   - Posterior cingulate

6. **Corpus Callosum**

7. **Cerebellum**
   - Left cerebellar anterior lobe
   - Left cerebellar posterior lobe

8. **White Matter (WM)**
   - Cingulum bundle
   - Frontal lobe WM
   - Deep gray matter

9. **Other**
   - Basal ganglia
   - Sensorimotor areas
   - Premotor areas
   - Primary somatosensory area

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including executive functions, visuospatial abilities, visual memory, reasoning, attention, and memory.
