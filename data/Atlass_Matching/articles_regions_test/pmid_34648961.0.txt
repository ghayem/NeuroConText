1. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
   - Paracingulate sulcus (PCS)
   - Anterior paracingulate cortex
   - Ventral medial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)

2. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex / precuneus
   - Lateral parietal cortices
   - Ventral medial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)
   - Anterior paracingulate cortex
   - Right posterior cingulate cortex

3. **Fronto-Parietal Network (FPN)**
   - Lateral prefrontal cortices (especially middle frontal gyrus)
   - Anterior inferior parietal lobule
   - Anterior insula
   - Caudate
   - Thalamus
   - Right lateralised FPN
   - Posterior regions of paracingulate cortex and precuneus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Dorsal regions of the lateral and medial prefrontal cortex
   - Right paracingulate gyrus
   - Left superior and inferior frontal gyrus
   - Bilateral middle frontal gyrus
   - Right middle frontal gyrus
   - Bilateral inferior frontal gyrus
   - Left basal ganglia
   - Amygdala
   - Left thalamus
   - Bilateral occipital cortex
   - Right superior parietal lobule

4. **External Attention System**
   - Dorsal Attention component

5. **Other Brain Regions**
   - Left fusiform gyrus
   - Left superior temporal gyrus

- These regions are involved in cognitive processes such as reality monitoring, self-referential judgments, mental state inferences, memory recall, and cognitive control.
