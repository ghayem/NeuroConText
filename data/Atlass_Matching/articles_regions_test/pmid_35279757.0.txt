## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Bilateral prefrontal cortices

2. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Bilateral occipital cortices

3. **Thalamus**
    - Right thalamus

4. **Cerebellum**
    - Left cerebellum
    - Posterior cerebellum

5. **Superior Frontal Gyrus**
    - Bilateral superior frontal gyrus

6. **Medial Frontal Gyrus**
    - Bilateral medial frontal gyrus

7. **Anterior Cingulate**
    - Right anterior cingulate

8. **Insular Cortex**
    - Right insular cortex

9. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
    - Right inferior frontal gyrus

10. **Frontal Orbital Cortex**
    - Right frontal orbital cortex

11. **Caudate**
    - Right caudate

12. **Fronto-Striatal-Limbic Network**
    - Fronto-striatal-limbic system
    - Fronto-striatal connectivity
    - Fronto-limbic regions
    - Striatal structures
    - Limbic structures

13. **Temporo-parieto-occipital Junction (TPO)**

14. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal cortex

15. **Putamen**
    - Striatal (including the putamen)

16. **Corticospinal Fluid Probability Maps**
    - Grey matter
    - White matter
    - Corticospinal fluid

17. **Montreal Neurological Institute (MNI) Space**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and psychiatric functions, including language, memory, executive functions, and psychotic symptoms such as hallucinations, delusions, and schizotypal traits.
