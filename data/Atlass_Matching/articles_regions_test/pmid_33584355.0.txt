## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

2. **Cerebellar Crus I**

3. **Supramarginal Gyrus (SMG)**

4. **Angular Gyrus (AG)**

5. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**

6. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**

7. **Frontoparietal Network (FPN)**

8. **Salience Network (SN)**

9. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex**

10. **Temporal Cortex**

11. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**

12. **Cerebellum**
    - Posterior lobe of cerebellum
    - Cerebellar lobule VI

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: executive functions, memory, attention, social processing, language, and emotion regulation.
