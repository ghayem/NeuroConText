Based on the provided article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Default Network (DN)**
   - **Core Subsystem**
     - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
     - Medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
       - Anterior MPFC (aMPFC)
   - **Other Regions**
     - Left and right angular gyrus (lAG and rAG)

2. **Dorsal Attention Network (DAN)**
   - Frontal eye field (FEF)
     - Right FEF (rFEF)
     - Left FEF (lFEF)
   - Inferior parietal sulcus (IPS)
   - Left and right inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Right and left superior parietal lobes (rSPL/lSPL)

3. **Salience Network (SN)**
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
   - Anterior insula (AI)
     - Right AI (rAI)
     - Left AI (lAI)
   - Left and right anterior prefrontal cortex (aPFC)

4. **Other Regions**
   - Anterior prefrontal cortex (aPFC)
   - Von Economo neurons (VEN)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:

- **Default Network (DN)**: Self-referential mental activities, internal mentation, and self-projection.
- **Dorsal Attention Network (DAN)**: Directed attention and working memory.
- **Salience Network (SN)**: Detection and mapping of external salient inputs and task control.
