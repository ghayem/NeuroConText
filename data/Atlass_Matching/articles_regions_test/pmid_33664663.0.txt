## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Striatum**
    - Caudate nucleus
    - Anterior putamen (APU)
    - Posterior putamen (PPU)

2. **Telencephalon**
    - Claustrum

3. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (opercular part)
    - Medial frontal gyrus [supplementary motor area (SMA)]

4. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal gyrus

5. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Paracentral lobule

6. **Limbic Cortex**
    - Cingulate gyrus

7. **Insula**

8. **Occipital Cortex**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: executive function, memory, visuospatial function, and language.
