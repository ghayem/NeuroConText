## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Parahippocampal Gyrus (PHG)**
   - Parahippocampal Place Area (PPA)
   - Collateral Sulcus (CoS)

2. **Retrosplenial Cortex (RSC)**

3. **Occipital Place Area (OPA)**

4. **Fusiform Gyrus**
   - Fusiform Face Area (FFA)

5. **Lateral Occipital Complex (LOC)**
   - Object-selective Lateral Occipital Cortex (LO)
   - Human Middle Temporal Complex (hMT+)

6. **Ventral Occipitotemporal Cortex (VOT)**

7. **Middle Occipital Gyrus (MOG)**

8. **Middle Temporal Gyrus (MTG)**
   - Posterior Middle Temporal Gyrus (pMTG)

9. **Middle Occipital Sulcus (MOS)**

10. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**
    - Lateral OFC (lOFC)
    - Medial OFC (mOFC)
    - Mid Anterior OFC (maOFC)

11. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
    - Anterior Medial Prefrontal Cortex (aMPFC)
    - Dorsal Medial Prefrontal Cortex (dMPFC)
    - Ventral Medial Prefrontal Cortex (vMPFC)

12. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

13. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**

14. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Nucleus Accumbens (NAc)
    - Caudate
    - Putamen
    - Pallidum

15. **Pericalcarine Cortex**

16. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Anterior Medial Prefrontal Cortex (aMPFC)
    - Dorsal Medial Prefrontal Cortex (dMPFC)
    - Ventral Medial Prefrontal Cortex (vMPFC)
    - Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)
    - Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)

17. **Superior Rostral Sulcus (SRS)**

18. **Anterior Insula**

These brain regions and sub-regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including perception, memory, emotion, valuation, and aesthetic evaluation.
