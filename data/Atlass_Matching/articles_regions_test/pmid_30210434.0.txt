## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Left Ventro-dorsal Stream**
    - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
        - Supramarginal gyrus
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
        - Ventral premotor cortex
        - Opercular region
        - Triangular region
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Rolandic operculum
    - Insula

2. **Left Frontal Cortex**
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Superior frontal gyrus
    - Middle frontal gyrus
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
        - Opercular region
        - Triangular region
        - Orbital region
    - Rolandic operculum

3. **Left Parietal Cortex**
    - Postcentral gyrus
    - Superior parietal lobule
    - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
        - Supramarginal gyrus
        - Angular gyrus

4. **Left Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal gyrus
    - Superior temporal pole
    - Middle temporal gyrus

5. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Putamen
    - Pallidum
    - Thalamus

6. **Other Regions**
    - Left insula
    - Left angular gyrus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including motor control, motor learning, visuo-motor integration, and the generation of motor predictions.
