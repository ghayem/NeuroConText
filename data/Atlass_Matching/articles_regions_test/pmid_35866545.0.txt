## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Cerebral Cortex**
    - **Neocortex**
    - **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
        - Ventral mPFC (vmPFC)
        - Dorsal mPFC (dmPFC)
    - **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**
    - **Precuneus (PCun)**
    - **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**
    - **Parahippocampal Gyrus (PHG)**
    - **Posterior Superior Temporal Sulcus (pSTS)**
    - **Anterior Intraparietal Sulcus (aIPS)**
    - **Premotor Cortex (PMC)**

2. **Cerebellum**
    - **Posterior Cerebellum**
        - Lobules Crus 1
        - Lobules Crus 2
    - **Cerebellar Crus**

3. **Default Mode Network**
    - **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
    - **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**

4. **Action Observation Network**
    - **Posterior Superior Temporal Sulcus (pSTS)**
    - **Anterior Intraparietal Sulcus (aIPS)**
    - **Premotor Cortex (PMC)**
    - **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

5. **Mentalizing Network**
    - **Ventral mPFC (vmPFC)**
    - **Dorsal mPFC (dmPFC)**
    - **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**
    - **Precuneus (PCun)**

6. **Spatial Navigation Network**
    - **Hippocampus**
    - **Parahippocampal Gyrus (PHG)**
    - **Precuneus (PCun)**
    - **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including social cognition, mentalizing, action observation, spatial navigation, and sequence learning.
