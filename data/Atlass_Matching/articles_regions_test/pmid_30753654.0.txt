Sure, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Medial hypothalamus**

3. **Periaqueductal gray matter**

4. **Septo-hippocampal system**

5. **Frontostriatal system**
    - Ventral striatum
        - Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)
    - Dorsal striatum
        - Caudate nucleus
    - Prefrontal lobe

6. **Prefrontal cortex**
    - Medial prefrontal cortex
        - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex
        - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex
    - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex
    - Lateral prefrontal cortex
    - Middle frontal cortex
    - Inferior frontal cortex
    - Superior temporal regions

7. **Anterior hippocampus**

8. **Supramarginal gyrus**

9. **Striatum**
    - Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)
    - Caudate nucleus

10. **Gray matter (GM)**

11. **White matter**

12. **Cerebrospinal fluid**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including fear perception, behavioral response, goal conflict resolution, defensive approach behavior, and reward sensitivity.
