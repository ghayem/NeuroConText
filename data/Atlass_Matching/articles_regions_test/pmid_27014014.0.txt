1. **Cortex**
    - Posterior parietal cortex
    - Right posterior parietal cortex
    - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
    - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
    - Temporal parietal junction (TPJ)
    - Premotor cortex (BA6)
    - Precuneus
    - Postcentral gyrus
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Supplementary motor area (SMA)

2. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Substantia nigra
    - Red nucleus
    - Subthalamic nuclei
    - Thalamus

3. **Cerebellum**
    - Cerebellar lobule VIIIa Vermis
    - Cerebellar lobule VIIa Crus I
    - Cerebellar lobule VI vermis and hemisphere

4. **Other Regions**
    - Insula

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including attention, visual search, learning, memory, motor function, and cognitive processing.
