## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Supragenual ACC (BA24/32)

2. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (MPFC)**

3. **Insula**
   - Posterior insula/primary sensory cortex (pIns/S1)
   - Left middle insular cortex
   - Bilateral middle to posterior insular cortex
   - Left middle to posterior insula cortex

4. **Primary Sensory Cortex**
   - Posterior insula/primary sensory cortex (pIns/S1)

5. **Supplementary Motor Area**

6. **Somatosensory Cortex**
   - Left postcentral/middle frontal gyrus (BA2, 3, 4, 6, 40)
   - Right pre/postcentral gyrus (BA2, 3, 4, 6)
   - Right somatosensory cortex (BA 7, 39, 40)

7. **Amygdala**

8. **Middle Frontal Gyrus**
   - Left postcentral/middle frontal gyrus (BA2, 3, 4, 6, 40)

9. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Frontal operculum

10. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Middle/inferior temporal gyrus (BA37)

11. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Right inferior/superior parietal cortex (BA 7, 39, 40)

12. **Auditory Areas**
    - Bilateral posterior superior temporal gyri

13. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Left middle insular cortex and the medial frontal cortex (supplementary motor area)
    - Left postcentral/middle frontal gyrus (BA2, 3, 4, 6, 40)

14. **Brodmann Areas (BA)**
    - BA24/32
    - BA2, 3, 4, 6, 40
    - BA 7, 39, 40
    - BA37

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as interoception, emotional awareness, somatosensory processing, and the integration of bodily sensations with emotional experiences.
