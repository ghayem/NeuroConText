## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**

2. **Cingulate**
    - Anterior cingulate

3. **Striatum**
    - Ventral striatum
        - Nucleus accumbens
    - Dorsal striatum
    - Putamen

4. **Thalamus**

5. **Precuneus**

6. **Amygdala**

7. **Insula**

8. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**

9. **Posterior Cingulate**

10. **Temporo-parietal Junction (TPJ)**

11. **Occipital Lobe**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions related to emotional arousal, affect regulation, empathy, mentalizing skills, motivation, pleasure, and approach behavior, as well as social cognition and visual processing.
