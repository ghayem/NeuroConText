## The brain regions:

1. **Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)**

2. **Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)**

3. **Insula**

4. **Basal ganglia**
    - Caudate nucleus
    - Putamen
    - Nucleus accumbens

5. **Cingulate cortex**

6. **Prefrontal cortex**

7. **Striatum**

8. **Mediotemporal regions**

9. **Frontostriatal circuit**
    - Frontal cortex
    - Striatum

10. **Medial prefrontal cortex**

11. **Inferior OFC**

12. **Superior OFC**

13. **Ventral striatum**

14. **Medial OFC**

15. **Insular cortex**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: reward processing, motivation, hedonic response, emotion processing, sensory integration, and salience network.
