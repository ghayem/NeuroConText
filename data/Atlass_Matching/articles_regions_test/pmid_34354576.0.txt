## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)
   - Inferior parietal lobe (IPL)
   - Supramarginal gyrus

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Anterior prefrontal cortex
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OPFC)
   - Premotor cortex

3. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Primary visual cortex
   - Secondary visual cortex
   - Peristriate cortex

4. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Fusiform gyrus
   - Occipitotemporal cortex

5. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Frontal eye field
   - Supplementary motor area

6. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex

7. **Insular Cortex**
   - Bilateral insular cortex

8. **Hippocampus**

9. **Cerebellum**

10. **Other Subcortical Structures**
    - Hypothalamus
    - Thalamus
    - Basal ganglia

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including numerical processing, attention, memory, executive control, and sensory processing.
