1. **Amygdala**
    - Left amygdala
    - Right amygdala

2. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**
    - Orbital parts of the superior frontal gyrus (OSFG)
        - Left orbital part of the superior frontal gyrus
        - Right orbital part of the superior frontal gyrus
    - Orbital parts of the middle frontal gyrus (OMFG)
        - Left orbital part of the middle frontal gyrus
        - Right orbital part of the middle frontal gyrus
    - Orbital parts of the inferior frontal gyrus (OIFG)
        - Left orbital part of the inferior frontal gyrus
        - Right orbital part of the inferior frontal gyrus
    - Medial orbital part of the superior frontal gyrus (MOSFG)
        - Left medial orbital part of the superior frontal gyrus
        - Right medial orbital part of the superior frontal gyrus

3. **Hippocampus**
    - Left hippocampus
    - Right hippocampus

4. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**
    - Left parahippocampal gyrus
    - Right parahippocampal gyrus

5. **Insula**
    - Left insula
    - Right insula

6. **Olfactory Cortex**
    - Left olfactory cortex
    - Right olfactory cortex

7. **Temporal Pole of Middle Temporal Gyrus (TPMTG)**
    - Left temporal pole of middle temporal gyrus

8. **Caudate**
    - Bilateral caudate

9. **Putamen**
    - Left putamen

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including olfaction, memory, emotion, and higher brain functions.
