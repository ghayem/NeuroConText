Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Anterior cingulated gyrus
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (BA13)
    - Superior frontal gyrus (BA9)
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex

2. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Precuneus (BAs 7 and 31)
    - Postcentral gyrus (BA40)
    - Inferior parietal lobule (BAs 40 and 2)
    - Supramarginal gyrus (BA40)

3. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Lingual gyrus (BAs 18 and 3)
    - Visual cortex center

4. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Hippocampus

5. **Other Regions**
    - Insula
    - Thalamus
    - Cerebellum
    - Default mode network
    - Pain modulating system
    - Limbic system
    - Wernicke's area

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:
- Emotional processing and recognition
- Cognition
- Somatic sensation
- Spatial orientation
- Semantic representation and language production
- Vision
- Memory
- Executive functions
- Sensory and motor processing
