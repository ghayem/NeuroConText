## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex (DMPFC)**

2. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**
    - Superior frontal gyrus (SFG)

3. **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex (VLPFC)**
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
    - Left IFG
    - Right IFG

4. **Parietal Lobes**
    - Superior parietal lobe (SPL)
    - Inferior parietal lobe (IPL)

5. **Temporal Gyrus**
    - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
    - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
    - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)

6. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
    - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
    - Middle cingulate cortex (MCC)

7. **Motor Cortex**
    - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
    - Postcentral/precentral gyrus

8. **Rolandic Operculum**

9. **Cerebellum**

10. **Fusiform Gyrus**

11. **Occipital Gyrus**

12. **Insula**
    - Right insula

13. **Precuneus**

14. **Supramarginal Gyrus**

15. **Amygdala**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including emotion regulation, cognitive control, semantic processing, attention control, and performance monitoring.
