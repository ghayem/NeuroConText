## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Superior Frontal Gyrus (SFG)
    - Middle Frontal Gyrus (MFG)
    - Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)

2. **Parietal Lobule**
    - Superior Parietal Lobule (SPL)
    - Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)

3. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)
    - Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)

4. **Insula Cortex**

5. **Thalamic Radiation**

6. **Corona Radiata**

7. **Parahippocampal Cortex**

8. **Precuneus**

9. **Hippocampus**
    - Parahippocampus

10. **Occipital Lobes**
    - Bilateral lingual lobes
    - Bilateral calcarine

11. **Temporal Lobes**
    - Bilateral fusiform
    - Right temporal lobe
    - Bilateral superior temporal gyri
    - Bilateral temporal pole
    - Left uncus
    - Right amygdala

12. **Corpus Callosum**
    - Genu
    - Splenium

13. **Cuneus**

14. **Anterior-Posterior Commissure Line**

- These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including working memory, executive function, language, spatial information processing, and cognitive slowing.
