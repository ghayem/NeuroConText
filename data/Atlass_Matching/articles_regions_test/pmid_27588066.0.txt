## The brain regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Left deep prefrontal cortex

2. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Left temporal lobe
   - Right deep temporal lobe

3. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - White matter of the inferior frontal gyrus

4. **Superior Frontal Gyrus**

5. **Hippocampus**
   - Hippocampal sulcus

6. **Amygdala**

7. **Entorhinal Cortex**

8. **Corpus Callosum**
   - Part of the corpus callosum

9. **Cingulate Gyrus**

10. **Frontal Lobe**

11. **Middle Frontal Lobe**

- These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including executive functions, emotion regulation, memory, and higher-order processing.
