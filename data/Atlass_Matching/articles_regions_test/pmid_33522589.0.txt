These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including but not limited to:

- **ACC**: Error detection, conflict monitoring, reward anticipation, and emotion regulation.
- **PCC**: Internally directed cognition, self-reflection, and memory.
- **Caudate**: Motor control, learning, memory, reward, and emotion.
- **IFG**: Language processing, response inhibition, and attention.
- **Orbitofrontal Cortices**: Decision-making, expectation, and reward processing.
- **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortices**: Executive functions, working memory, and attention.
- **Premotor Cortices**: Motor planning and execution.
- **FFG**: Face and object recognition, visual processing.
- **Corpus Callosum**: Interhemispheric communication.
- **Cingulum**: Part of the limbic system, involved in emotion regulation and cognitive control.
