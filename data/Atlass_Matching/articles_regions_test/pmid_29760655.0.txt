## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Hippocampus (HC)**
   - Anterior hippocampus (aHC)
   - Posterior hippocampus (pHC)

2. **Parahippocampal Place Area (PPA)**
   - Posterior parahippocampal cortex

3. **Retrosplenial Complex (RSC)**
   - Retrosplenial cortex proper (Brodmann areas 29–30)
   - Posterior cingulate (Brodmann areas 23–31)
   - Ventral parieto-occipital sulcus
   - Anterior calcarine sulcus

4. **Occipital Place Area (OPA)**
   - Transverse occipital sulcus

5. **Early Visual Cortex (EVC)**

## Cognitive Functions:
These regions are involved in cognitive functions related to spatial navigation, spatial memory, and spatial orientation.
