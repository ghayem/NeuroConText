## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Olfactory Cortex**
   - Olfactory bulb
   - Piriform cortex
   - Agranular insular region
   - Entorhinal cortex
   - Hippocampus

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Medial frontal gyrus
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
     - Orbital gyrus
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

3. **Limbic System**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Piriform cortex
   - Agranular insular region

4. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Cuneus (Cu)

5. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Lingual gyrus (LG)
   - Parahippocampal gyrus (PHG)

6. **Cerebellum**
   - Declive (DoC)
   - Lobule VI

7. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Visual cortex

8. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (BA 10)

9. **Somatosensory Cortex**

10. **Brodmann Areas (BAs)**
    - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (BA 10)
