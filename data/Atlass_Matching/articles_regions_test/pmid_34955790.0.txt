## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Medial Temporal Lobe**
   - Hippocampus

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Right inferior/middle orbital frontal gyrus (OFG)

3. **Anterior Cingulum**

4. **Cerebellum**
   - Posterior lobe of cerebellum
   - Right cerebellum crus1/crus2/6

5. **Insular Cortex**
   - Right insular

6. **Sensorimotor Cortex**

7. **Visual Cortex**

8. **Auditory Cortex**

9. **Salience Network (SN)**
   - Insula

10. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**

11. **Central Executive Network (CEN)**

12. **Affective Network (AN)**
   - Insular cortex
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)

13. **Prefrontal-Cerebellar Circuits**
   - Posterior cerebellum (Crus I/II)

14. **Hippocampal Subregions**
   - CA1
   - Dentate gyrus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including memory, information processing, emotional regulation, and higher-order cognitive processes.
