## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Pregenual sub-region

2. **Putamen**
   - Part of the dorsal striatum

3. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**

4. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Dorsal prefrontal cortex

5. **Hippocampus**

6. **Amygdala**

7. **Caudate**
   - Part of the dorsal striatum

8. **Ventral Striatum**

9. **Cerebellum**

10. **Thalamus**

11. **Midbrain**

12. **Middle Frontal Gyri**
   - Bilateral middle frontal gyri

13. **Cuneus**

14. **Midbrain/Thalamus**
   - Associated with arousal

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including learning, attention, emotion regulation, and cognitive flexibility.
