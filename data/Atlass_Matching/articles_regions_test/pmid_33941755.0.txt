## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Brainstem**
   - Rostral ventromedial medulla (RVM)
   - Locus coeruleus (LC)
   - Periaqueductal gray (PAG)

2. **Forebrain**
   - Dorsal posterior insula (dpIns)
   - Primary somatosensory cortex (S1)
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Frontopolar cortex (Brodmann area 10, BA10)
   - Lateral occipital cortex (LOC)
   - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
   - Anterior insula (aIns)
   - Precuneus (Pcu)
   - Fronto-medial cortex (FMC)
   - Operculum (OPC)

These regions are involved in pain modulation, attentional processes, and cognitive functions.
