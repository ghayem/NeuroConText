## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Medial Temporal Lobes (MTL)**
   - Hippocampus
     - Hippocampal head
     - Hippocampal body
     - Hippocampal tail
   - Amygdala
   - Perirhinal cortex
   - Entorhinal cortex
   - Parahippocampal cortex
   - Temporopolar cortex

2. **Thalamus**
   - Mediodorsal thalamus

3. **Posterior Cingulate**
   - Precuneus-posterior cingulate

4. **Posterior Medial Cortex**
   - Posterior cingulate-retrosplenial cortex-precuneus

5. **Neocortical Regions**
   - Posteromedial cortex

6. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Paracingulate cortex

7. **Default-Mode Network**
   - Posterior cingulate
   - Precuneus

8. **Limbic Circuitry**
   - Thalamus
   - Posterior cingulate

9. **Brainstem**

10. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Caudate nucleus
    - Putamen
    - Pallidum

11. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Medial prefrontal cortex

12. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Posterior cingulate cortex
    - Precuneus

13. **Hippocampal-Thalamic-Neocortical Network**

14. **Hippocampal-Diencephalic-Cingulate Circuitry**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: memory processing, episodic memory, and other cognitive functions.
