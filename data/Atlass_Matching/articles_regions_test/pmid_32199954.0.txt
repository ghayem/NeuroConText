## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Midcingulate Cortex (MCC)**
   - Anterior midcingulate cortex (aMCC)
   - Posterior midcingulate cortex (pMCC)

2. **Cingulate and Peri-Cingulate Cortex**
   - Right cingulate cortex
   - Peri-cingulate cortex

3. **Occipitotemporal Regions**
   - Bilateral inferior/middle occipital gyrus
   - Bilateral lingual gyrus
   - Left inferior temporal/occipital gyrus
   - Right middle occipital/temporal gyrus

4. **Frontal Areas**
   - Right parietal cortex
   - Bilateral frontal areas

5. **Subcortical Regions**
   - Left cerebellum
   - Left amygdala
   - Bilateral putamen

6. **Precentral Gyrus**
   - Right precentral gyrus

7. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**
   - Right supplementary motor area

8. **Insula**
   - Left insula
   - Left anterior insula

9. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**
   - Bilateral middle temporal gyrus
   - Extrastriate body area (EBA)

10. **Other Regions**
   - Left inferior frontal gyrus (pars Opercularis)
   - Right precuneus
   - Right superior parietal lobule

   - These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: emotion processing, motor control, threat perception, and adaptive responses.
