Sure, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Auditory Cortex**
   - Primary auditory cortex
   - Secondary auditory cortex
   - Heschl gyrus
   - Planum temporale

2. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Left temporal pole
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Inferior temporal gyrus
   - Medial temporal gyrus

3. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyrus

4. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal gyrus
   - Precuneus

5. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Fusiform
   - Cuneus

6. **Visual Cortex**
   - Primary visual cortex
   - Secondary visual cortex

7. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Anterior DMN (aDMN)
     - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Posterior DMN (pDMN)
     - Precuneus

8. **Frontoparietal Network**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus
   - Inferior parietal gyrus

9. **Dorsal Attention Network**
   - Frontoparietal network

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including language, memory, executive functions, sensory processing, and attention.
