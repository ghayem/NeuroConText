## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
    - Mesial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
    - Left inferior parietal lobe (LIPL)
    - Right inferior parietal lobe (RIPL)
    - Left temporal lobe
    - Right temporal lobe (RTL)

2. **Frontal Regions**
    - Mesial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)

3. **Parietal Regions**
    - Left inferior parietal lobe (LIPL)
    - Right inferior parietal lobe (RIPL)

4. **Temporal Regions**
    - Left temporal lobe
    - Right temporal lobe (RTL)

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)

6. **Thalamus**

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as memory, executive functions, and consciousness.

## Additional Sub-Regions and Structures:

- **Electrocardiogram**
- **Electrooculogram**
- **Electroencephalogram (EEG)**
    - MR-compatible EEG electrodes
    - EEG data
    - EEG clock
    - Gradient artifact
    - EEG-defined sleep stage
    - EEG montage

- **Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)**
- **Superior temporal plane (STP)**
- **Planum temporale (PT)**
- **Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)**
- **Anterior portion of the pSTG/S**

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as language, memory, and executive functions.

## Other Neuroanatomical Structures:

- **White matter**
- **Cerebrospinal fluid**

- These structures are involved in the neural and physiological processes that support brain function.
