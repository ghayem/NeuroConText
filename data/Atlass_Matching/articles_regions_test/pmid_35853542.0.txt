## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Ventral Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

2. **Amygdala**

3. **Medial Frontal Gyrus**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Medial frontal cortex

4. **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
     - Brodmann area 47 (BA 47)

5. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
   - Precuneus
   - Mid cingulate cortex (MCC)

6. **Lateral Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (VLPFC)

7. **Insula**

8. **Inferior Parietal Cortex**

9. **Left Temporal Cortex**

10. **Left Parietal Cortex**
    - Lateral parietal cortex

11. **Precentral Sulcus**

12. **Medial Frontal Regions**
    - Medial frontal gyrus/anterior cingulate

13. **Posterior Regions**
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
    - Precuneus

14. **Parietal Midline Regions**
    - Right dorsal PCC
    - Right precuneus
    - Right postcentral sulcus

15. **Medial Frontal Cortex**

16. **Parietal Midline Regions**

17. **Lateral Parietal Cortex**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotional regulation, executive control, self-referential processing, memory encoding, and working memory tasks.
