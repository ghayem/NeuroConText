Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**

2. **Hippocampus**

3. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Lingual gyrus
   - Fusiform gyrus

4. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)

5. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Superior parietal cortex
   - Inferior parietal cortex

6. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

7. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Visual cortex
     - Calcarine gyrus
     - Middle occipital cortex
     - Inferior occipital cortex

8. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

9. **Ventral Striatum**

10. **Insula**
    - Posterior insular cortex

11. **Hypothalamus**

12. **Thalamus**

13. **Amygdala**

14. **Precentral Gyrus (PG)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including reward processing, memory, imagery, attention, semantic decoding, word comprehension, aesthetic judgments, emotional responses, and visuo-spatial processes.
