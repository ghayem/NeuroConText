## The brain regions:

1. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
   - Cognitive control network
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
   - Right mPFC

2. **Rostrolateral Prefrontal Cortex (rlPFC)**
   - Right rlPFC

3. **Lateral Prefrontal Cortex (lPFC)**

4. **Temporal Pole**
   - Bilateral temporal pole

5. **Amygdala**

6. **Hippocampus**
   - Bilateral hippocampus

7. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: decision-making, uncertainty processing, emotional regulation, and threat avoidance.
