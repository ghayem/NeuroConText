1. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Ventro-medial PFC
   - Dorsomedial PFC (dmPFC)

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Dorsal ACC (dACC)

3. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

4. **Precuneus**

5. **Anterior Insula**

6. **Temporo-Parietal Junction (TPJ)**
   - Posterior superior temporal gyrus
   - Ventral areas of the inferior parietal lobule
   - Angular gyrus (ANG)

7. **Thalamus (THA)**
   - Mediodorsal thalamus

8. **Cerebellum**
   - Left cerebellum (crus I & hemispheric lobule VI)

9. **Inferior Temporal Cortex**
   - Left inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)

10. **Fusiform Gyrus**
    - Left fusiform gyrus

11. **Median Cingulate Cortex**
    - Bilateral median cingulate cortex

12. **Superior Frontal Gyrus**
    - Left medial superior frontal gyrus
    - Right superior frontal gyrus

13. **Temporal Cortex**

14. **Default Mode Network**
    - Includes mPFC, ACC, PCC, and precuneus

15. **Salience Network**
    - Includes dACC and anterior insula

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including self-processing, self-referencing, cognitive control, attention, theory of mind, social cognition, decision-making, and emotional processing.
