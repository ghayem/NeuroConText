## The brain regions:

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Insula**

3. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
    - Left PFC
    - Right inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
    - Right inferior frontal cortex
    - Left inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
    - Left periamygdalar cortex
    - Right periamygdalar cortex

4. **Thalamus**

5. **Posterior Inferior Frontal Cortex**

6. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Left parietal cortex

7. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

8. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**

9. **Inferior Parietal Lobe**

10. **Putamen**

11. **Brainstem**

12. **Anterior Insular/Opercular Cortex**

13. **Pre-supplementary Motor Area**

14. **Basal Ganglia**

15. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Ventrolateral
    - Dorsolateral

16. **Hippocampus**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as emotional processing, fear response, attention, inhibitory control, and interoceptive awareness.
