## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Prefrontal Gyrus (PFG)**
    - Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)
    - Dorsal Anterior Cingulate Gyrus (ACG)
    - Dorsolateral Prefrontal Gyrus (DLPFG)
    - Right Inferior Frontal Gyrus (rIFG)

2. **Subthalamic Nucleus (STN)**
    - Part of the Basal Ganglia

3. **Pre-Supplementary Motor Area (preSMA)**

4. **Primary Motor Gyrus (PMG)**

5. **Right Temporal-Parietal Junction (rTPJ)**

6. **Left Inferior Parietal Gyrus (IPG)**

7. **Right Middle Occipital Gyrus (rMOG)**
    - Left Middle Occipital Gyrus (lMOG)

8. **Right Middle Frontal Gyrus (rMFG)**
    - Left Inferior Frontal Gyrus (lIFG)

9. **Right Inferior Occipital Gyrus (IOG)**
    - Left Inferior Occipital Gyrus (IOG)

10. **Right Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**

11. **Fusiform Gyrus**

12. **Right Precuneus**

13. **Right Postcentral Gyrus**

14. **Left Insula**

15. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**

16. **Amygdala**

### These regions are involved in these cognitive functions:
- Inhibitory control
- Attentional control
- Executive control
- Visual form perception and recognition
- Spatial attention
- Motor control
- Error detection
- Conflict monitoring
- Performance monitoring
- Face recognition
- Emotional processing
- Theory-of-mind (ToM) network
- Moral judgments
- Attentional reorienting
