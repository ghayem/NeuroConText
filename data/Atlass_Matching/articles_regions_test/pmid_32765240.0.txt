1. **Parietal Cortex**
   - **Bilateral intraparietal sulcus (IPS)**
     - Horizontal segment of the intraparietal sulcus (hIPS)
   - **Left angular gyrus (AG)**
   - **Posterior superior parietal area**

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - **Right middle frontal gyrus**
   - **Left precentral gyrus**
   - **Left superior frontal gyrus**

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - **Right middle temporal gyrus**

4. **Occipital Cortex**
   - **Extrastriate visual areas**

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - **Middle cingulate cortex**

6. **Motor Cortex**
   - **Primary motor cortex**
     - Representation of the right abductor pollicis brevis (APB) muscle

7. **Other Regions**
   - **Retrosplenial cortex**
   - **Hippocampal system**
   - **Ventral pathway (via middle longitudinal fascicle)**
   - **Dorsal pathway (via superior longitudinal fascicle)**
   - **Cortical cingulate route**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: arithmetic processing, numerical knowledge, working memory, strategy selection, verbal processing of numbers, spatial and non-spatial attention, memory retrieval, and motor functions.
