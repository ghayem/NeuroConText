## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article

1. **Frontal Region**
   - Left middle frontal gyrus

2. **Temporal Region**
   - Right inferior temporal gyrus

3. **Parietal Region**
   - Bilateral postcentral gyrus

4. **Limbic Region**

5. **Striatal Region**

6. **Thalamus**
   - Anterior thalamic nuclei
   - Ventrolateral thalamus
   - Dorsomedial thalamic nucleus

7. **Cerebellum**

8. **Prefrontal Cortex**

9. **Orbital Frontal Cortex**

10. **Anterior Cingulate Gyrus**

11. **Accumbens Nucleus**

12. **Postcentral Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including motor control, emotion regulation, cognitive processing, memory, and sensory gating.
