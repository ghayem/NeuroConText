## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Cortical Midline Structures**
    - Medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
    - Precuneus (Prec)

2. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
    - Posterior cingulate cortex/precuneus (PCC/Prec)

3. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

4. **Precuneus**
    - Left precuneus

5. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**
    - Right temporoparietal junction (RTPJ)

6. **Intraparietal Sulcus (IPS)**
    - Left intraparietal sulcus (LIPS)

7. **Fusiform and Inferior Temporal Area**
    - Bilateral fusiform
    - Bilateral inferior temporal area

8. **Middle Frontal Region**
    - Bilateral middle frontal region

9. **Middle Cingulate Region**
    - Left middle cingulate region

10. **Medial Frontal Region**
    - Left medial frontal region

11. **Temporal Pole**
    - Right temporal pole

12. **Thalamus**
    - Right thalamus

13. **Calcarine Region**
    - Right calcarine region

14. **Precentral Region**
    - Right precentral region

15. **Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex (DMPFC)**

16. **Temporal Pole**

17. **Inferior Parietal Lobule**

18. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**

19. **Paracentral Lobule**

20. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

21. **Subgenual Anterior Cingulate Cortex (sgACC)**

22. **Middle and Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
    - Right middle and inferior frontal gyrus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including self-referential thinking, rumination, attention, social cognition, and memory.
