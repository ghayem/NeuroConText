## Brain regions and sub-regions:

1. **Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)**

2. **Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)**
   - Left OFC
   - Left medial OFC
   - Right NAcc

3. **Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (VMPFC)**

4. **Fronto-parietal (FP) control system**
   - Right inferior parietal lobule
   - Left inferior parietal lobule
   - Right precuneus
   - Left lingual gyrus
   - Right middle frontal gyrus
   - Right middle temporal cortex

5. **Left middle occipital gyrus**

6. **Left supramarginal gyrus**

7. **Right postcentral gyrus**

8. **Right inferior frontal gyrus**

9. **Right insula**

10. **Left occipitotemporal cortex**

11. **Left precentral gyrus**

12. **Left cuneus**

13. **Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: reward processing, cognitive control, self-regulation, decision-making, and food evaluation.
