### Cognitive Functions Associated with These Regions:
- **Language Processing**: Inferior frontal gyri, superior temporal gyrus, middle temporal gyrus
- **Memory**: Hippocampal and parahippocampal regions
- **Attention**: Dorsal-attentional network, including regions like the inferior frontal gyrus, superior temporal gyrus, and fusiform gyrus
- **Sensorimotor Functions**: Supplementary motor area (SMA), postcentral gyrus, precentral gyrus
- **Executive Functions**: Middle frontal gyrus, inferior frontal gyri
- **Emotional Processing**: Amygdala
- **Visual Processing**: Occipital cortex regions
- **Auditory Processing**: Superior temporal gyrus, heschl gyrus

These regions and their sub-regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including language, memory, attention, sensorimotor processing, executive functions, emotional processing, and visual and auditory processing.
