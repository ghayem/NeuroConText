## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Hippocampus**
    - Anterior hippocampus

2. **Entorhinal Cortex (EC)**

3. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**

4. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Anterior temporal lobe
    - Right anterior temporal lobe

5. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

6. **Cerebellum**

7. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Bilateral frontal superior gyri
    - Superior frontal gyrus

8. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Bilateral superior parietal lobule (SPL)
    - Left SPL
    - Right SPL
    - Posterior parietal cortex
    - Paracentral lobule

9. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Bilateral occipital lobe
    - Medioventral occipital cortex (MVOcC)
    - Right lateral occipital cortex (LOcC)
    - Left lateral occipital cortex (LOcC)

10. **Temporal Gyrus**
    - Rostral area of the right middle temporal gyrus (rMTG)

11. **Basal Ganglia**

12. **Other Mentioned Regions**
    - Bilateral hippocampi
    - Bilateral superior frontal gyrus
    - Bilateral SPLs
    - Bilateral occipital lobes
    - Ventral stream (or the “what” pathway)
    - Dorsal stream (or the “how” pathway)
    - Posterior regions of the parietal cortex
    - Medial portions of the SPL

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as spatial memory, spatial navigation, episodic memory, and visuospatial retrieval.
