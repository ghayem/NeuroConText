## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)**

2. **Ventral Premotor Cortex**

3. **Pre-Supplementary Motor Area (pre-SMA)**

4. **Anterior-Ventral Sector of the Insula**

5. **Temporal Pole**
   - Left antero-ventral insula
     - Anterior and middle short gyri

6. **Amygdala**
   - Right amygdala

7. **Periaqueductal Gray**

8. **Habenula/Medial Pulvinar**

9. **Anterior Thalamus/Hypothalamus**
   - Right thalamus

10. **Extrastriate Body Area (EBA)**

11. **Occipital Lobe**

12. **Mesial and Dorsal Premotor Regions**

13. **Medial Thalamus**

14. **Putamen**
   - Anterior and ventral part

15. **Caudate**
   - Posterior part

16. **Cerebellum**

17. **Raphe Nucleus**

18. **Superior Temporal Gyrus**

19. **Middle Temporal Area (MT)**

20. **Pregenual Anterior Cingulate Cortex (pACC)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions including the processing of facial expressions, emotional content, and vitality forms.
