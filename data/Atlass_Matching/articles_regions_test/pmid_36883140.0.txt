1. **Primary odour sensing regions**
   - Olfactory bulbs

2. **Secondary odour processing regions**
   - Piriform cortex (Pir)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
     - Left orbitofrontal cortex (L-OFC)
     - Right orbitofrontal cortex (R-OFC)
   - Insula cortex
     - Anterior insula (AI)
     - Left anterior insula (L-AI)
     - Left insula cortex
   - Fusiform gyrus
   - Visual association cortex
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
   - Ventral posterior cingulate cortex
   - Posterior hippocampus
   - Cerebellum
     - Left Crus I
     - Right IX
     - Right Crus I
   - Thalamus
     - Left thalamus

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: olfactory processing, sensory integration, cognitive processing, and memory recall/processing.
