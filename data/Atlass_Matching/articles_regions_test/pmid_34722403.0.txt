## The brain regions:

1. **Primary Motor Cortex (M1)**
2. **Premotor Cortex (PMC)**
3. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**
4. **Primary Somatosensory Cortex**
    - Brodmann Areas BA1
    - Brodmann Areas BA2
    - Brodmann Areas BA4
5. **Pre-supplementary Motor Area**
6. **Posterior Parietal Cortex**
7. **Superior Parietal Lobe**
8. **Intraparietal Sulcus**
9. **Dorsal Premotor Cortex**

- These regions are involved in motor functions, motor imagery, and motor planning.
