Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Primary motor cortex (M1)**

2. **Premotor cortex (PM)**
    - Ventral premotor cortex (PMv)
    - Dorsal premotor cortex (PMd)

3. **Supplementary motor area (SMA)**
    - SMA proper
    - Pre-SMA

4. **Parietal cortex**
    - Posterior parietal cortex (PPC)
    - Medial intraparietal area (MIP)
    - Anterior intraparietal area (AIP)
    - Lateral region in the posterior intraparietal sulcus (lpIPS)
    - Medial portion of the posterior intraparietal sulcus (mpIPS)
    - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
    - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
    - Angular gyrus (A39c)
    - Supramarginal gyrus

5. **Occipital cortex**
    - Primary visual cortex
    - Medial visual occipital cortex (MVOcC)
    - Right lingual gyrus (rLinG)
    - Fusiform (Occipitotemporal gyrus)

6. **Frontal cortex**
    - Superior frontal gyrus
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Frontal eye fields (FEF)
    - Handknob

7. **Other regions**
    - Precuneus
    - Insular cortex
    - Central sulcus
    - Pre-supplementary motor area (preSMA)

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including sensorimotor transformation, visually-guided movements, and motor control.
