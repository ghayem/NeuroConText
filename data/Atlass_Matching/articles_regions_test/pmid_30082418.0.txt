## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Hippocampus**
   - Anterior medial hippocampus
     - Pre/parasubiculum
   - Posterior hippocampus
   - Anterior lateral hippocampus
     - Prosubiculum/CA1

2. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
   - Hippocampus
   - Entorhinal cortex (ENT)
   - Perirhinal cortex (PRC)
   - Parahippocampal cortex (PHC)
   - Retrosplenial cortex (RSC)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)

3. **Early Visual Cortices (EVCs)**
   - Precuneus (inferior portions)
   - Calcarine sulcus
   - Lingual gyrus
   - Posterior fusiform cortex

4. **Other Regions**
   - Anterior medial hippocampus
     - Uncus
   - Posterior portions of the EVC
   - Anterior lateral hippocampus
     - Prosubiculum/CA1
   - Left anterior medial hippocampus

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: memory, spatial navigation, scene perception, short-term memory, constructing mental representations, imagining the future, decision-making, mind-wandering, object processing, spatial processing, and associative processing.
