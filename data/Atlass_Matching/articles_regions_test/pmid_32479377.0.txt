## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Orbital Frontal Cortex (OFC)**

2. **Ventral Striatum (VS)**

3. **Amygdala**
   - Central nucleus
   - Lateral/basolateral nucleus

4. **Insula**

5. **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex (vlPFC)**

6. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

7. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

8. **Nucleus Accumbens (NAcc)**

9. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

10. **Dorsal Striatum**

11. **Precuneus**

12. **Posterior Parietal Cortex**

13. **Primary Visual Cortex**

14. **Primary Motor Cortex**

15. **Cerebellum**

16. **Paracingulate Gyrus**

17. **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including reward processing, cognitive control, emotion regulation, and decision-making.
