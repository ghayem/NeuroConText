Certainly! Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
    - Precuneus

2. **Visual Cortex**
    - Primary visual cortex (V1)

3. **Somatosensory Processing Areas**
    - Somatosensory cortex

4. **Motor and Premotor Areas**
    - Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)

5. **Vestibular Processing Areas**
    - Vestibular cortex

6. **Motion Perception Areas**
    - Middle temporal (MT)/visual area 5 (V5)

7. **Ventral Visual Cortex**
    - Fusiform gyrus

8. **Occipitotemporal Cortex**
    - Lingual gyrus

9. **Other Cortical Areas**
    - Left hemisphere (LH)
    - Right hemisphere (RH)

10. **Subcortical Areas**
    - Ventricles
    - Cerebellum

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: self-awareness, consciousness, embodiment, motion perception, balance, vestibular processing, somatosensory processing, cognitive aspects of own-body representations, visual perception, space perception, locomotion, goal-directed tasks, and self-motion.
