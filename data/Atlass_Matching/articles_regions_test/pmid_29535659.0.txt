1. **Thalamus**
   - Left thalamic pulvinar

2. **Cerebellum**

3. **Middle Frontal Gyrus (MFG)**
   - Left middle frontal gyrus

4. **Superior Frontal Gyrus (SFG)**

5. **Posterior Cingulate Gyrus (PCG)**

6. **Middle Temporal Gyrus (MTG)**

7. **Inferior Temporal Gyrus (ITG)**

8. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**

9. **Middle Occipital Gyrus (MOG)**

10. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**

11. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**

12. **White Matter (WM)**

13. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

14. **Amygdala**

15. **Visual Cortex**
   - Primary visual cortex (V1)

16. **Auditory Cortex**

17. **Premotor Cortex**

18. **Superior Colliculus**

These regions are involved in a wide range of affective, perceptual, mnemotic, executive, and other cognitive functions, as well as sensory processing and emotional regulation.
