## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
    - Dorsal lateral PFC (dlPFC)
    - Ventral lateral PFC (vlPFC)
    - Medial PFC (mPFC)
    - Ventromedial PFC (vmPFC)

2. **Cingulate System**
    - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)

3. **Amygdala**

4. **Nucleus Accumbens (NAcc)**

5. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**

6. **Thalamus**

7. **Right Precentral Gyrus**

8. **Right Parietal Cortex**
    - Angular gyrus

9. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

10. **Posterior Cingulate (Precuneus)**

11. **Dorsal Executive System (DES)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including emotion regulation, cognitive reappraisal, and stress response modulation.
