## The brain regions:

1. **Orbitofrontal cortex**

2. **Anterior cingulate cortex**

3. **Thalamus**

4. **Striatum**

5. **Dorsolateral frontal regions**

6. **Parietal regions**

7. **Right putamen**

8. **Right frontal operculum**

9. **Left midcingulate cortex**

10. **Right temporal pole**

11. **Caudate nucleus**

12. **Medial frontal gyrus**

13. **Rolandic operculum**

14. **Medial prefrontal cortex**

15. **Insula**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: planning, execution, cognitive regulation of emotional behavior, resistance to compulsions, and emotional saliency.
