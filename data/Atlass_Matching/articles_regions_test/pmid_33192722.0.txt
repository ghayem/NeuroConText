    - Medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
    - Orbital PFC (OPFC)

    - Right inferior frontal gyrus/insula

2. **Basal Ganglia**

3. **Temporal Regions**

4. **Parietal Regions**

5. **Hippocampus**

6. **Default Mode Network**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as working memory, salience detection, attention, social cognition, cognitive dysfunction, and psychopathology.
