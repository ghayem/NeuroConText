### Cognitive Functions and Processes

- **Reward Prediction**: Involves VTA, NAcc, ventral pallidum, central amygdala opioid, OFC, amygdala, insula, thalamus, substantia nigra, red nucleus, hypothalamus, globus pallidus, precentral gyrus, inferior parietal lobule, dorso-lateral prefrontal cortex, medial prefrontal cortex, superior parietal lobule, claustrum.

- **Value Attribution**: Involves NAcc, ventral pallidum, OFC, amygdala, insula, ACC, caudate body, caudate head, caudate tail, putamen, caudal-ventral putamen, hippocampus, subcallosal area, uncus, mammillary body, parahippocampal gyrus, entorhinal cortex.

- **Interoception and Prediction Error**: Involves mid-posterior insula, anterior insula, ACC.

- **Emotional Representation**: Involves anterior insula.

- **Awareness of Wants and Needs**: Involves anterior insula.

- **Choice and Action Selection**: Involves ACC, caudate, ventral striatum, mid-posterior insula.

- **Homeostatic Emotion**: Involves ACC.

- **Long-Term Value Association**: Involves caudate body, caudate tail, putamen, caudal-ventral putamen.

- **Short-Term Value Encoding**: Involves ventromedial caudate, rostroventral putamen, ventral striatum, ventral pallidum.

- **Exploratory Behavior**: Involves ventromedial prefrontal cortex.

These regions and their functions provide a comprehensive understanding of the neural underpinnings of "Wanting" and "Needing" as described in the article.
