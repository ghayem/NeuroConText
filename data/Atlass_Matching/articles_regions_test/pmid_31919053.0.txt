1. **Frontoparietal Network**
- Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
- Posterior parietal cortex (PPC)
  - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)

2. **Right Hemisphere Regions**
- Right lateral prefrontal cortex (PFC)

3. **Premotor Cortex**
- Left premotor cortex (PMC)
- Primary motor area (MI)

4. **Frontal Lobe Regions**
- Left middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
- Left superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
- Bilateral supplementary motor areas (SMA)
- Right superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
- Right frontal pole
- Right middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
- Pars triangularis of the right inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

5. **Parietal Lobe Regions**
- Right posterior parietal cortex (PPC)

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: perception of approximate numerosities, working memory (WM), higher-order cognitive functions such as decision-making and reasoning, and supramodal coding of numerosity memoranda.
