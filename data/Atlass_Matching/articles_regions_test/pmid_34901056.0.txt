These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including but not limited to:
- **Default-Mode Network (DMN)**: Self-projection, self-awareness, episodic memory, autobiographical memory, and information integration.
- **Sensorimotor Network (SMN)**: Sensory input, motor coordination, and movement-demanding tasks.
- **Salience Network (SN)**: Responses to behaviorally salient events.
- **Hippocampal Network (HN)**: Memory disturbances and slow motor performances.

Additionally, some of these regions are implicated in cognitive impairment, dementia, and other neurological manifestations in patients with end-stage renal disease (ESRD).
