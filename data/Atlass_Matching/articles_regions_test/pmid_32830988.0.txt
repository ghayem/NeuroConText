Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Left Hemisphere**
   - Perilesional regions
   - Superior temporal cortex
   - Insula
   - Precuneus
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Lateral temporal cortex
   - Hippocampal formations

2. **Right Hemisphere**
   - Homotopic areas to left perisylvian language areas
   - Inferior frontal areas
   - Temporal areas
   - Precuneus
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Lateral temporal cortex
   - Hippocampal formations
   - Superior frontal cortex
   - Anterior cingulum

3. **Bilateral**
   - Perisylvian language areas
   - Frontal midline cortex
   - Presupplementary motor area
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate

4. **Networks**
   - Default mode network (DMN)
   - Salience network (SN)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including language processing, memory, attention, and domain-general cognitive processes.
