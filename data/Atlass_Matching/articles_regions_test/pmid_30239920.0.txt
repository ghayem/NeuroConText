## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Amygdala**
    - Basolateral part of the amygdala (BLA)

2. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
    - Medial PFC (mPFC)
    - Ventromedial PFC (vmPFC)
    - Ventrolateral PFC (vlPFC)

3. **Hippocampus**
    - No sub-regions specified

4. **Hypothalamus-Pituitary-Adrenal (HPA) Axis**
    - Hypothalamus
    - Pituitary gland
    - Adrenal glands

5. **Insula**
    - No sub-regions specified

6. **Occipital Inferior Gyrus**
    - No sub-regions specified

7. **Anterior Parietal Regions**
    - Post-central gyrus
    - Supramarginal gyrus

8. **Ventral Tegmental Area (VTA)**
    - No sub-regions specified

9. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**
    - No sub-regions specified

10. **Soft Tissue**
    - No sub-regions specified

11. **Air**
    - No sub-regions specified

12. **Bone**
    - No sub-regions specified

13. **Subcortical Brain Regions**
    - Amygdala
    - Insula
    - Hippocampus

14. **Posterior Parietal Cortex**
    - No sub-regions specified

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including affective processing, emotion regulation, memory formation, and stress response.
