Certainly! Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Precuneus
    - Thalamus
    - Frontal gyrus
    - Temporal gyrus

2. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

3. **Nucleus Accumbens (NAcc)**

4. **Supplementary Motor Areas (SMAs)**
    - Left supplementary motor area (SMA)
    - Right supplementary motor area (SMA)

5. **Insula**
    - Bilateral regions between the insula and inferior frontal gyrus (insula/IFG)

6. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
    - Ventral anterior cingulate cortex (vACC)
    - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)

7. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
    - Left inferior frontal gyrus
    - Right inferior frontal gyrus

8. **Secondary Somatosensory Cortex**
    - Mandibular angle
    - Cheek
    - Lip
    - Forehead
    - Foot

9. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**
    - Left inferior parietal lobule
    - Right inferior parietal lobule

10. **Posterior Inferior Gyrus**
    - Left posterior inferior gyrus
    - Right posterior inferior gyrus

11. **Ventral Premotor Area (VPC)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including reward, motivation, emotion, arousal, self-relevant thought, empathy, mirror neuron activation, and somatosensory activation.
