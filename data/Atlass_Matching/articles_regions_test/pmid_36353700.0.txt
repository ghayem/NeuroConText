Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Somatosensory cortices**
   - Primary somatosensory cortex (SI)
   - Secondary somatosensory cortex (SII)

2. **Insula**
   - Insula (Ins)
   - Anterior insula (aIns)
     - Left anterior insula (aInsL)
   - Posterior insula (pIns)
     - Left posterior insula (pInsL)
     - Right posterior insula (pInsR)

3. **Cingulate cortices**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
     - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex (rACC)
   - Mid-cingulate cortex (MCC)
   - Medial cingulate gyrus

4. **Prefrontal cortex (PFC)**
   - Dorsal prefrontal cortex
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Ventral prefrontal cortex

5. **Parietal lobe**
   - Region bordering the occipital lobe and the height of pInsL (used as a control region, UNR)

These regions are involved in the following cognitive and physiological functions: pain processing, pain perception, pain regulation, emotional processing, somatosensory integration, cognitive processes (such as attention and decision making), and emotion regulation.
