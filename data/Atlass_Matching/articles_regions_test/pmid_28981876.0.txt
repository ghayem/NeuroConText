## The brain regions:

1. **Insula**
   - Anterior insula

2. **Ventromedial prefrontal/medial orbitofrontal cortex (mOFC)**
   - Ventral striatum

3. **Substantia nigra/ventral tegmental area (SN/VTA) complex**
   - Substantia nigra (SN)
   - Ventral tegmental area (VTA)

4. **Anterior insula**
   - Anterior insula

5. **Human midbrain**
   - Substantia nigra/ventral tegmental area (SN/VTA) complex

6. **Red nucleus (RN)**
   - Red nucleus

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: social norm adaptation, norm updating, norm prediction errors (PEs), risk/variance PEs, and decision-making processes.
