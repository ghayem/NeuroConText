## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Lateral occipital cortex
    - Medial occipital cortex

2. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Occipito-parietal cortex
    - Lateral parietal cortex
    - Medial parietal cortex
    - Superior parietal cortex
    - Parieto-occipital cortex
    - Supramarginal gyrus

3. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Occipito-temporal cortex
    - Lateral temporal cortex
    - Medial temporal cortex
    - Superior temporal gyrus
    - Planum polare of the superior temporal gyrus
    - Inferior temporal gyrus

4. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Bilateral frontal eye field

5. **Cerebellum**
    - Cerebellum (vermis excluded)

6. **Ventricular System**
    - Vermis of the cerebellum

7. **Other Cortical Areas**
    - Temporo-parieto-occipital cortices
    - Parietal associative cortices
    - Posterior regions of the brain
    - Frontal areas
    - Frontal eye field
    - Posterior cortical atrophy (PCA)
