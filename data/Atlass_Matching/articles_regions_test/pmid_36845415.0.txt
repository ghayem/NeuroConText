## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Precuneus
    - Posterior cingulate
    - Entorhinal cortex

2. **Pain Network**
    - Insula
    - Primary somatosensory cortex
    - Secondary somatosensory cortex
    - Prefrontal cortex
        - Ventromedial region
    - Thalamus
    - Anterior cingulate
    - Posterior cingulate
    - Basal ganglia
    - Cerebellum

3. **Other Regions**
    - Periaqueductal gray matter
    - Brainstem
    - Primary somatosensory cortex
    - Cavum septum pellucidum
    - Arachnoid cysts

- These regions are involved in pain processing, sensitization, and cognitive modulation of pain.
