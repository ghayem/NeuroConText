## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex
   - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex

3. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Anterior medial prefrontal cortex (PFC)

   - Inferior PFC
   - Superior PFC

4. **Hippocampus**

5. **Ventral Striatum**

6. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

7. **Precuneus**

8. **Temporo-Parietal Junction (TPJ)**

9. **Temporal Pole**

10. **Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex (dmPFC)**

11. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Amygdala
    - Hippocampus
    - Nucleus accumbens
    - Insula
    - Orbitofrontal cortex

12. **Subgenual Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

13. **Cortical Regions**
    - Medial PFC
    - Dorsolateral PFC
    - Temporo-parietal junction (TPJ)

   - These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: social processing, emotional regulation, and decision-making.
