## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Primary visual cortex (V1)
    - Extrastriate visual-cortical areas (V2/3)
    - Cuneus
    - Precuneus
    - Middle occipital gyrus
    - Fusiform gyrus

2. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Precuneus
    - Sub-gyral
    - Paracentral superior- and inferior-lobules
    - Supramarginal- and postcentral-gyrus

3. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Frontal eye-field (FEF)
    - Middle-frontal gyrus
    - Medial-frontal gyrus
    - Superior-frontal gyrus
    - Paracentral- and precentral-lobules
    - Sub-gyral

4. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Middle temporal gyrus
    - Superior temporal gyrus

5. **Cerebellum**
    - Cerebellar tonsil
    - Pyramis
    - Inferior semi-lunar lobule
    - Posterior lobe
    - Declive
    - Culmen
    - Tuber

6. **Brainstem**
    - Midbrain

7. **Thalamus**

8. **Insula**

9. **Cingulate Gyrus**
    - Anterior cingulate
    - Posterior cingulate

10. **Limbic Lobe**
    - Parahippocampal gyrus
    - Amygdala

11. **Subcortical Occipital Area**
    - Cuneus

12. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Optic radiation

13. **Uvula**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and sensory functions, including visual processing, motor control, and higher-order cognitive functions.
