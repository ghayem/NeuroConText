## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Hippocampus**
    - Anterior hippocampus (ventral hippocampus in rodents)
    - Posterior hippocampus (dorsal hippocampus in rodents)
    - Left hippocampus
        - Anterior-most emotional cluster (HIPe)
        - Middle cognitive cluster (HIPc)
        - Posterior-most perceptual cluster (HIPp)

2. **Medial temporal gyrus**
    - Right medial temporal gyrus (RMTG)

3. **Middle occipital gyrus**
    - Left middle occipital gyrus (LMOG)

4. **Putamen**
    - Left putamen

5. **Angular gyrus**
    - Right angular gyrus (RAG)
    - Left angular gyrus (LAG)

6. **Postcentral gyrus**
    - Left postcentral gyrus

7. **Inferior parietal lobule**
    - Left inferior parietal lobule

8. **Medial frontal gyrus**
    - Medial frontal gyrus

9. **Temporal gyrus**
    - Medial/inferior temporal gyrus
    - Fusiform gyrus

10. **Occipital gyrus**
    - Occipital gyrus

11. **Parietal lobule**
    - Inferior parietal lobule

12. **Parahippocampal cortex**

13. **Cerebrospinal fluid**

14. **White matter**

15. **Grey matter**

16. **Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: memory, emotion regulation, facial emotion processing, and cognitive processes such as semantic processing, attention, and memory retrieval.
