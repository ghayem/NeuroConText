## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Hemispheres**
   - Lesioned hemisphere
   - Intact hemisphere
   - Ipsilateral hemisphere
   - Contralateral hemisphere

2. **Insular Cortex**
   - Anterior insular cortex
   - Posterior insular cortex
   - Lesioned insula
   - Contralesional insula

3. **Temporal Lobe**

4. **Parietal Lobe**

5. **Occipital Lobe**

6. **Frontal Lobe**

These regions are involved in multiple brain functions, including language processing, emotion, cognitive control, and decision making.
