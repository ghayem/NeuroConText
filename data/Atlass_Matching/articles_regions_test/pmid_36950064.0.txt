## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Frontoparietal Cortices**
   - Dorsal executive network
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex

2. **Emotion Processing Areas**
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Insula

3. **Limbic Emotion Response System**
   - Amygdala

4. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
   - Bilateral sensorimotor cortex
   - Central sulcus
   - Pre-central gyrus
   - Paracentral gyri
   - Supplementary motor area

5. **Temporal Cortices**
   - Bilateral temporal cortices
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Superior temporal plane (STP)
   - Planum temporale (PT)
   - Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)
   - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

6. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Bilateral superior frontal sulci (dorsolateral prefrontal cortex; dlPFC)
   - Inferior frontal gyri (ventrolateral prefrontal cortex; vlPFC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)
   - Orbital part of the right inferior frontal gyrus

7. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Bilateral anterior cingulate cortex

8. **Insula**
   - Right insula

9. **Intraparietal Gyrus**
   - Intraparietal gyrus

10. **Occipito-temporal and Occipito-temporo-parietal Junctions**
    - Occipito-temporal junction
    - Occipito-temporo-parietal junction

11. **Posterior Occipitoparietal Regions**
    - Posterior occipitoparietal regions

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotion regulation, executive functions, and sensorimotor processing.
