## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**

3. **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Ventral striatum

4. **Cingulate Cortex**

5. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**

6. **Limbic System**

7. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Temporal pole

8. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Orbital inferior frontal gyrus (OIFG)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

9. **Parietal Lobe**

10. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Occipital gyrus (OG)
    - Middle occipital gyrus
    - Superior occipital gyrus
    - Bilateral cuneus

11. **Insula**

12. **Cerebellum**
    - Cerebellum crus I
    - Cerebellum vermis
    - Vermis I
    - Vermis II
    - Parahippocampal gyrus

13. **Brainstem**

14. **Supplementary Motor Area**

15. **Precentral Gyrus**

16. **Fusiform Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotional regulation, mental flexibility, impulsivity, self-awareness, self-monitoring, auditory and visual processing, working memory, and emotional processing.
