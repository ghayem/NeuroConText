## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Amygdala**
   - Basolateral amygdala (BLA)
   - Centromedial amygdala (CMA)
   - Superficial amygdala (SFA)

2. **Hippocampus**
   - Anterior hippocampus
   - Posterior hippocampus

3. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
     - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
     - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsal medial prefrontal cortex
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (subgenual ACC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex

4. **Insula**
   - Anterior insula
   - Posterior insula

5. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior temporal gyrus

6. **Cerebellum**

7. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

8. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus
   - Frontal pre-ventromedial (vmPFC)

9. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Middle occipital gyrus
   - Fusiform gyrus
   - Lingual gyrus

10. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Angular gyrus

11. **Motor Cortex**
    - Supplementary motor area (SMA)

12. **Olfactory Cortex**

13. **Brainstem**
    - Autonomic and motor centers

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including emotion processing, memory, fear response, sensory integration, and executive functions.
