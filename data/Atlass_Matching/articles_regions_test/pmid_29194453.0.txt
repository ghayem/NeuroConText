## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Left Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
   - Frontal operculum
   - Pars triangularis
   - Pars opercularis
   - Orbitalis

2. **Temporal Poles (TP)**
   - Anterior part of the temporal lobe (aTL)
   - Bilateral temporal poles

3. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**
   - Posterior superior temporal gyrus (pSTG)
   - Anterior portion of the STG
   - Posterior superior temporal sulcus (pSTS)
   - Anterior superior temporal sulcus (aSTS)

4. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Superior frontal gyrus

5. **Parietal Lobes**
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Inferior parietal lobule

6. **Occipital Regions**
   - Ventral occipitotemporal regions

7. **Subcortical Regions**
   - Right globus pallidus
   - Left amygdala
   - Right rolandic operculum

8. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**
   - Left middle temporal gyrus
   - Posterior middle temporal gyrus

9. **Supplementary Motor Area**
   - Bilateral supplementary motor area

10. **Insula**
    - Bilateral insulae

11. **Visual Cortex**

12. **Temporo-Parietal Junction (TPJ)**

13. **Parietal Lobule**
    - Left inferior parietal lobule

14. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Bilateral amygdalae

15. **Ventral Medial Areas**

16. **Anterior Temporal Network**
    - Right hemisphere (associated with negative beta values)

17. **Left Posterior STS**

18. **Left IFG Opercularis**

19. **Left IFG Triangularis**

20. **Left Posterior Temporal Region**

21. **Right Parietal and Occipital Areas**

22. **Right Supplementary Motor Area**

23. **Left Parietal Lobule**

24. **Left IPL**

25. **Left Posterior Middle Temporal Gyrus**

26. **Temporo-Parieto-Occipital Junction (TPO)**

## Cognitive Functions
These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: language processing, syntactic and semantic integration, morphological processing, verbal working memory, structural hierarchy processing, and executive functions.
