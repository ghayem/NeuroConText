1. **Hypothalamus**

2. **Cingulo-opercular network**
    - Bilateral insula
    - Thalamus
    - Anterior cingulate cortex
    - Right middle frontal gyrus
    - Bilateral midcingulate cortex

    - Left inferior parietal gyrus

3. **Frontoparietal network**
    - Bilateral superior frontal gyrus

    - Angular gyrus

4. **Arousal system**
    - Bilateral red nucleus
    - Substantia nigra
    - Locus coeruleus

5. **Motor areas**
    - Left pre- and postcentral gyrus
    - Bilateral supplementary motor area
    - Putamen
    - Cerebellum

6. **Visual cortex**
    - Bilateral inferior orbitofrontal gyrus
    - Fusiform gyrus

7. **Other regions**
    - Bilateral temporal lobe
    - Right angular gyrus
    - Vermis
    - Bilateral inferior orbitofrontal gyrus
    - Subthalamic nucleus

- These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including sustained attention, response inhibition, motor control, arousal, and visual processing.
