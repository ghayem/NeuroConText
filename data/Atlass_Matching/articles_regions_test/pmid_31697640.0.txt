### Cognitive Functions

- **Motor Control**: Primary hand motor cortex, dorsal premotor cortex, ventral premotor cortex, supplementary motor area (SMA), cingulate motor area, parietal operculum
- **Auditory Processing**: Superior temporal cortex, auditory cortex, posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S), auditory association cortex
- **Executive Functions**: Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex, inferior frontal cortex
- **Memory and Language**: Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)
- **Timing and Rhythm Processing**: Auditory association cortex, supplementary motor area (SMA), cerebellum
- **Sensory Integration**: Putamen, thalamus, superior cerebellum
- **Bimanual Coordination**: Primary hand motor areas, cerebellum
- **Auditory-Motor Interactions**: Auditory association cortex, supplementary motor area (SMA), basal ganglia, thalamus
- **Cognitive Grouping and Internal Generation**: Fronto-mesial cortex, intraparietal sulcus

These regions and sub-regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including motor control, auditory processing, executive functions, memory, language, timing and rhythm processing, sensory integration, bimanual coordination, and auditory-motor interactions.
