## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Broca's Area**
   - Brodmann Area 44 (BA 44)
   - Brodmann Area 45 (BA 45)

2. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Caudate nucleus
   - Putamen
   - Striatum (includes caudate nucleus and putamen)

3. **Precentral Gyrus**
   - Brodmann Area 6 (BA 6)

4. **Visual Cortex**
   - Lingual gyrus (BA 30)

5. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Brodmann Area 46 (BA 46)
   - Brodmann Area 47 (BA 47)
   - Mesial supplementary motor area

### Cognitive Functions Involved:
- Language processing (syntax-related)
- Coordinated movements
- Executive functions
- Visual processing
