Based on the article, the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned are as follows:

1. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Left dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Left superior frontal gyrus
   - Left middle frontal gyrus
   - Left triangular part of the inferior frontal gyrus
   - Left orbitofrontal cortex
   - Right inferior temporal gyrus

2. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Left precuneus
   - Left cuneus
   - Right inferior parietal cortex
   - Right postcentral gyrus
   - Right superior temporal gyrus
   - Calcarine gyrus

3. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Right middle temporal gyrus
   - Left inferior temporal gyrus
   - Right inferior temporal gyrus

4. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Left middle occipital gyrus
   - Right inferior occipital gyrus

5. **Limbic System**
   - Insula
   - Cingulate gyrus
   - Right putamen
   - Right caudate nucleus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotion regulation, social function, self-awareness, negative emotion regulation, attention control, episodic memory, self-reflection, advanced cognition, autobiographical memory, emotion processing, and cognitive control.
