## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Hippocampus**
   - Bilateral hippocampus

3. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

4. **Perirhinal Cortex**
   - Bilateral perirhinal cortex

5. **Entorhinal Cortex**
   - Bilateral entorhinal cortex

6. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Prefrontal networks
   - Prefrontal-hippocampal interactions

7. **Middle Occipital Gyrus**

8. **Fusiform Gyrus**

9. **Basolateral Amygdala**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: memory recognition, emotional enhancement of memory (EEM), attention, and emotional memory processing.
