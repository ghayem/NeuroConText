2. **Other Regions**

   - **Left Basal Ganglia**
   - **Default Mode Network**
     - **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - **Bilateral Temporo-Opercular Regions**

   - **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
