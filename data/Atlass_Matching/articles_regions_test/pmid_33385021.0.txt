## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Limbic Systems**

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**

3. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Hippocampus
    - Parahippocampus (PHG)
    - Parahippocampal Gyrus
    - Precuneus
    - Orbital Gyrus

4. **Salience Network (SN)**
    - Amygdala
    - Insula
    - Thalamus

5. **Central Executive Network (CEN)**

6. **Temporal Lobe**

7. **Frontal Lobe**

8. **Parietal Lobe**

9. **Subcortical Lobe**

10. **Visual Network**

11. **Somatomotor Network**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: stress processes, cognitive and emotion regulation, self-referential processing, arousal regulation, orienting attention to salient information, and emotion regulation and salience attribution.
