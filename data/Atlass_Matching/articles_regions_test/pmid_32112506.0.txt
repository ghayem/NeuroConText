## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Posterior cingulate/precuneus
    - Medial prefrontal cortex
    - Bilateral inferior parietal lobules

2. **Precuneus/Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
    - Precuneus
    - Posterior cingulate

3. **Auditory Cortex**
    - Bilateral superior temporal gyrus

4. **Parietal Memory Network (PMN)**
    - Precuneus
    - Middle/posterior cingulate cortex
    - Bilateral inferior parietal lobules

5. **Auditory Network (AN)**
    - Bilateral superior temporal gyrus
    - Right Heschl's gyrus (rHG)
    - Right superior temporal gyrus (rSTG)
    - Right posterior superior temporal gyrus (rpSTG)

6. **Temporal Cortices**
    - Posterior cingulate/precuneus

7. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**

8. **Inferior Parietal Lobules**
    - Bilateral inferior parietal lobules

9. **Middle/Posterior Cingulate Cortex**

10. **Bilateral Superior Temporal Gyrus**

11. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
    - Left posterior cingulate cortex (lPCC)

12. **Precuneus**
    - Left precuneus (lPCU)
    - Right precuneus (rPCU)

13. **Heschl's Gyrus**
    - Right Heschl's gyrus (rHG)

14. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**
    - Right superior temporal gyrus (rSTG)

15. **Posterior Superior Temporal Gyrus (pSTG)**
    - Right posterior superior temporal gyrus (rpSTG)

16. **Medial Frontal Cortex**

17. **Temporoparietal Junction**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as self-referential processing, memory-based reality monitoring, source memory or judgment, familiarity labeling in memory and learning, and auditory or language processing.
