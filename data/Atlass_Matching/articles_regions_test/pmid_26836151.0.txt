## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Hippocampus**
   - Notable sub-regions: CA1, presubiculum

2. **Amygdala**
   - Notable sub-regions: basomedial, basolateral

3. **Thalamus**
   - Notable sub-regions: ventrolateral part

4. **Putamen**

5. **Pallidum**

6. **Caudate Nucleus**

7. **Accumbens**
   - Notable sub-regions: nucleus accumbens

8. **Parietal Cortex**

9. **Temporo-parietal Cortex**

10. **Pulvinar Nucleus**

11. **Dentate Nucleus**

12. **Cerebral Grey Matter**

13. **White Matter Bundles**

14. **Subcortical Structures**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including memory, emotion regulation, motor control, and sensory processing.
