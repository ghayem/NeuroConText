## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Middle Cerebral Artery (MCA) territory**

2. **Corticospinal Tract**

3. **Pontine Crossing Tract**

4. **Middle Cerebellar Peduncle**

5. **Superior Cerebellar Peduncle**

6. **Medial Lemniscus**

7. **Inferior Cerebellar Peduncle**

8. **Corpus Callosum**
   - Body of corpus callosum

9. **Cingulum**

10. **Posterior Thalamic Radiation**

11. **Posterior Corona Radiata**

12. **Internal Capsule**
   - Posterior limb of the internal capsule

13. **Primary Motor Cortex (M1)**

14. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

15. **Transcallosal Motor Tract**

These regions are involved in motor functions and structural connectivity relevant to stroke recovery and the effects of rTMS.
