## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Lateral occipital region
    - Temporo-occipital region
    - Superior pericentral region
        - Precentral gyrus
        - Central gyrus
        - Postcentral gyrus
    - Cuneus

2. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Middle temporal gyrus
    - Inferior temporal cortex
    - Fusiform gyrus
    - Parahippocampal gyrus
    - Superior temporal cortex
    - Temporal cortex (general)

3. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Orbitofrontal cortex
    - Medial prefrontal cortex

4. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Superior insula
    - Adjacent parietal operculum
    - Precuneus

5. **Limbic System**
    - Amygdala
    - Hippocampus

6. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Caudate nucleus
    - Putamen

7. **Cerebellum**

8. **Other Regions**
    - Anterior cingulate
    - Insula
    - Superior insula
    - Operculum

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including social abilities such as mindreading and empathy, visual processing, memory, and emotional regulation.
