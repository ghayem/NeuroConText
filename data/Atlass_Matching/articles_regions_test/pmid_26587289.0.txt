## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Gray Matter (GM)**
    - Thalamus
    - Primary motor cortex
    - Primary sensory cortex
    - Motor cortex
    - Temporal lobe
    - Parietal lobe
    - Premotor cortex (PMC)
    - Left premotor area (BA 6)
    - Occipital lobes
    - Temporooccipital junction
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Precuneus lobe
    - Inferior parietal lobule
    - Frontal orbital cortex
    - Primary somatosensory and motor areas

2. **White Matter (WM)**
    - Corpus callosum (CC)
    - Corticospinal tract
    - Superior corona radiata
    - Inferior frontooccipital fasciculus (IFOF)
    - Commissural fibers
    - Association fibers
    - Local premotor and transcallosal paths
    - Bilateral premotor cortices

3. **Brodmann Areas (BA)**
    - BA 1
    - BA 2
    - BA 3a
    - BA 3b
    - BA 4a
    - BA 4p
    - BA 6

4. **Other Regions**
    - Visual cortices

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including movement selection, movement execution, visual-to-motor transformation, and interhemispheric interactions.
