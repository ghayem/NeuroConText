1. **Superior Olivary Complex (SOC)**
   - Located in the brainstem

2. **Primary Auditory Cortex**
   - Right primary auditory cortex

3. **Secondary Auditory Cortex**

4. **Primary Visual Cortex**

5. **Secondary Visual Processing Regions**
   - Right inferior temporal gyrus
   - Right middle temporal gyrus
   - Right middle occipital gyrus
   - Brodmann areas 19, 37, and 39 (BA 19/37/39)

6. **Posterior Superior Temporal Gyrus**
   - Left posterior superior temporal gyrus
   - Wernicke’s area and its right hemisphere homolog

7. **Left Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Broca’s area

8. **Middle Occipital Gyrus**
   - Bilateral middle occipital gyrus

9. **Posterior Cingulate/Precuneus**
   - Posterior aspect of the default mode network (DMN)

10. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Anterior DMN regions

11. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**
    - Anterior DMN regions

12. **Precentral and Post-Central Regions**
    - Related to motor activity

13. **Right Motor Region**

14. **Left Superior Temporal Gyrus**
    - Region of Heschl’s gyrus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including auditory processing, visual processing, language processing, cross-modal modulation, and higher-order cognitive functions such as executive function and attention.
