## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Cerebellum**
   - Lobule IV-V

2. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

3. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Putamen
   - Caudate nucleus

4. **Primary Motor Cortex**

5. **Premotor Cortex**
   - Dorsal premotor cortex

6. **Primary Somatosensory Cortex**
   - Postcentral gyrus

7. **Precentral Gyrus**

8. **Thalamus**

9. **Inferior Parietal Cortex**

10. **Superior Parietal Cortex**

11. **Middle Frontal Cortex**

12. **Middle Occipital Cortex**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including motor learning, memory consolidation, and sequence learning.
