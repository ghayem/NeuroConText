## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Frontal Cortex**

2. **Precuneus**

3. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

4. **Insula**

5. **Prefrontal Cortex**

6. **Parietal Lobes**

7. **Amygdala**

8. **Hippocampus**

9. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

10. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex**

11. **Dorsal Lateral Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Right dorsal lateral prefrontal cortex
    - Left dorsal lateral prefrontal cortex

12. **Angular Gyrus**
    - Right angular gyrus

13. **Supramarginal Gyrus**
    - Left supramarginal gyrus

14. **Inferior Parietal Lobule**
    - Right inferior parietal lobule
    - Left inferior parietal lobule

15. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Anterior DMN (aDMN)
        - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
        - Anterior cingulate cortex
    - Posterior DMN (pDMN)
        - Posterior cingulate cortex
        - Precuneus

16. **Salience Network (SN)**
    - Anterior and posterior parts of the insula
    - Anterior cingulate cortex

17. **Central Executive Network (CEN)**
    - Left CEN (lCEN)
        - Left dorsal lateral prefrontal cortex
        - Left supramarginal gyrus
        - Left inferior parietal lobule
    - Right CEN (rCEN)
        - Right dorsal lateral prefrontal cortex
        - Right angular gyrus
        - Right inferior parietal lobule

### These regions are involved in the following cognitive functions and processes:
- Emotion regulation
- Impulse control
- Executive functions
- Social and affective cognitions
- Self-introspections
- Information filtering, detection, and integration
- Executive control processes
- Cognitive processes during goal-directed behaviors
- Top-down attention
- Visuospatial processing
- Self-referential processing
- Episodic memory
