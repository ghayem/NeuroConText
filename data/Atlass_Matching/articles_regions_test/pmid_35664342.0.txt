## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Visual Cortex**
   - Bilateral lingual gyrus (LING)
   - Calcarine (CAL)
   - Superior occipital gyrus (SOG)

2. **Cerebellum**
   - Right cerebellum (CER)_8
   - Left CER_4-5

3. **Postcentral Gyrus**
   - Right postcentral gyrus (PostCG)

4. **Superior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Bilateral superior frontal gyrus

5. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**
   - Left middle temporal gyrus

6. **Precentral Gyrus**
   - Right precentral gyrus

7. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Left inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Right IFG

8. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**
   - Left parahippocampal gyrus

9. **Insula**
   - Left insula
   - Right insula

10. **Precuneus**
    - Left precuneus (PreCUN)

11. **Superior Parietal Lobe**
    - Left superior parietal lobe

12. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Left occipital lobe

13. **Salience Network**

14. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**

15. **Dorsal Attention Network**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as visual processing, motor control, body balance, higher cognitive function, visual attention, working memory, sensorimotor function, pain sensory processing, and visuomotor coordination.
