Sure, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Entorhinal cortex
   - Perirhinal cortex

2. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)
   - Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)
   - Bilateral DLPFC (L, BA9; R, BA46)
   - Right precentral gyrus (BA6)
   - Left medial frontal gyrus (BA6)
   - Left superior frontal gyrus (BA6)
   - Right DLPFC (BA9)
   - Right precentral gyrus (BA6)
   - Superior frontal gyrus (BA8)
   - Middle frontal gyrus (L, BA6)

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Bilateral precuneus (BA7 and BA19)
   - Right superior parietal lobule (BA7)
   - Left angular gyrus (BA39)
   - Bilateral precuneus (BA7)
   - Right precuneus (BA31/7)
   - Right precuneus (BA31)

4. **Limbic Lobe**
   - Bilateral parahippocampal gyrus (R, L, BA27 and L, BA36)
   - Bilateral entorhinal cortex
   - Left perirhinal cortex
   - Bilateral entorhinal (BA27, 28)
   - Bilateral perirhinal cortex (BA36)

5. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Right lingual gyrus (BA18)
   - Right cuneus (BA17)
   - Right middle occipital gyrus (BA19)
   - Bilateral lingual gyrus (BA18)
   - Bilateral middle occipital gyrus (L, BA18; R, BA19)
   - Bilateral cuneus (BA17)

6. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Left fusiform gyrus (BA37)
   - Bilateral fusiform gyrus (BA37)
   - Left superior temporal gyrus (BA38)
   - Left middle temporal gyrus (BA39)
   - Superior temporal gyrus (BA22, 38)

7. **Subcortical Structures**
   - Left sub-lobar thalamus
   - Left thalamus
   - Right lateral globus pallidus
   - Bilateral thalamus
   - Left lentiform nucleus
   - Right lingual gyrus (BA18)
   - Right claustrum
   - Left amygdala
   - Bilateral globus pallidus
   - Bilateral thalamus pulvinar

8. **Cerebellum**
   - Bilateral cerebellum
   - Part of left cerebellum
   - Part of right cerebellum

9. **Other Regions**
   - Left insular cortex (BA13)
   - Right posterior cingulate (BA23)
   - Left posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Bilateral hippocampus
   - Right extra-nuclear (BA7)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including memory (episodic memory, encoding, retrieval), language, attention/executive function, visuospatial skills, and semantic knowledge representation.
