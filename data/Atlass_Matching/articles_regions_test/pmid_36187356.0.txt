Based on the provided article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Insular Cortex**
   - Posterior insula

2. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Prefrontal cortex

3. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulum cortex (ACC)
   - Right anterior cingulum cortex (rACC)
   - Perigenual ACC

4. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Posterior parietal gyrus
   - Superior parietal cortex (SPC)
   - Precuneus
   - Angular gyrus (AG)
     - Bilateral angular gyrus
     - Right angular gyrus

5. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)
   - Left inferior temporal gyrus

6. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

7. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Includes ACC, ITG, AG, precuneus, and other regions

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including pain perception, emotion regulation, and sensory processing.
