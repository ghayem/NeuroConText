## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Hippocampus**
    - Anterior hippocampus
    - Posterior hippocampus
    - Bilateral hippocampus

2. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
    - Perirhinal cortex (PRC)
    - Parahippocampal cortex (PHC)
    - Entorhinal cortex

3. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Superior medial prefrontal cortex (BA 10)
    - Superior lateral prefrontal cortex (BA 8/9)
    - Inferior prefrontal cortex (BA 45)
    - Middle frontal gyrus (BA 8)

4. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Angular gyrus (BA 39/40)
    - Inferior parietal lobe (BA 39/40)
    - Superior parietal lobe (BA 7)
    - Precuneus (BA 7)
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (BA 31)
    - Retrosplenial cortex

5. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Middle temporal gyrus (BA 21)
    - Middle occipital gyrus (BA 19)
    - Fusiform gyrus (BA 19/37)

6. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Middle occipital gyrus (BA 19)

7. **Subcortical Structures**
    - Caudate nucleus
    - Thalamus (ventral lateral thalamus)
    - Dorsomedial nucleus of the thalamus

8. **Other Cortical Regions**
    - Superior frontal gyrus (BA 6)
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (BA 45)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including memory (recollection and familiarity), language, and executive functions.
