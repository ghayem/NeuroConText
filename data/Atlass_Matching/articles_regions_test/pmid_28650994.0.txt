## The brain regions:

1. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Fusiform gyrus
   - Entorhinal cortex

2. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Precuneus (PCU)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Lateral parietal lobe
   - Angular gyrus
   - Inferior parietal lobe
   - Visual cortex

3. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Superior occipital gyrus (SOG)
   - Cuneus (CUN)

4. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Orbitofrontal cortices

5. **Other Regions**
   - Left temporal pole (middle) (TPOmid)
   - Middle occipital gyrus
   - Geniculate nucleus

   - These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: episodic memory, visual memory, visuospatial perception, and other complex cognitive functions.
