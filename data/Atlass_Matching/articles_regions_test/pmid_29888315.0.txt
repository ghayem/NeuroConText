    - Parahippocampus

13. **Loudness Network**
    - Parahippocampal area
    - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex/ventromedial prefrontal cortex

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions and processes:
- Tinnitus loudness and distress
- Auditory sensory uncertainty and prediction
- Self-referential processing and adaptation
- Memory retrieval and processing
- Attention and cognitive processing
- Emotional and cognitive distress
- Salience-based cognitive auditory comparator function
- Cross-frequency coupling and communication between cortically distant areas
