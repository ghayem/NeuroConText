## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Medial Parietal Cortex**
    - Ventral precuneus

2. **Anterior Prefrontal Cortex**

3. **Inferior Parietal Cortex**

4. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

5. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex**

6. **Posterior Parietal Cortex**

7. **Medial Temporal Lobes (MTL)**

8. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Caudate (head and body)
    - Putamen

9. **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)**

10. **Temporal Pole**

11. **Insular Cortex**

12. **Precentral Gyrus**

13. **Temporo-parietal Regions**

14. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Prefrontal cortex
    - Frontoparietal regions

15. **Visual Cortex**

16. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Bilateral MTL

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including judgment, decision-making, memory, attention, and categorization.
