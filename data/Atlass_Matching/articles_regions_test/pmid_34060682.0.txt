## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Auditory Cortex**
   - Primary auditory cortex (A1)
   - Heschl's gyrus
   - Superior temporal gyri
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Middle superior temporal cortex
   - Anterior superior temporal sulcus
   - Middle superior temporal sulcus

2. **Visual Cortex**
   - Primary visual cortex
   - Calcarine
   - Cuneus
   - Lingual gyrus
   - Superior occipital gyrus
   - Middle occipital gyrus
   - Inferior occipital gyri

3. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
   - Paracentral lobule
   - Post-central gyri
   - Pre-central gyri
   - Intraparietal sulcus
   - Inferior parietal lobule
   - Superior parietal lobule

4. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Inferior parietal cortex
   - Precuneus
   - Medial frontal cortex
   - Medial parietal cortex
   - Medial temporal cortex

5. **Salience Network (SN)**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex
   - Insula
   - Parahippocampal gyrus

6. **Attention Network (AN)**
   - Intraparietal sulcus
   - Inferior parietal lobule
   - Superior parietal lobule

7. **Frontal Network (FN)**
   - Dorsal frontal cortex

8. **Additional Regions**
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Anterior cingulate cortex
   - Ventricular region
   - White matter
   - Cerebrospinal fluid (CSF)
   - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including auditory processing, visual processing, sensorimotor integration, default mode processing, salience detection, attention, and frontal executive functions.
