Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
    - Ventral-medial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
    - Dorsal-medial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
    - Right dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (rDLPFC)

2. **Striatum**
    - Ventral striatum (VS)

3. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

4. **Temporo-parietal Junction (TPJ)**
    - Right temporo-parietal junction (rTPJ)

5. **Insula**
    - Anterior insula (AntIns)

6. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
    - Left inferior parietal lobule (left IPL)
    - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)

7. **Caudate**

8. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

9. **Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including decision-making, moral judgment, emotional processing, and value computation.
