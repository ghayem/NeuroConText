## The brain regions and sub-regions:

1. **Subthalamic nucleus (STN)**

2. **Nucleus ruber (NR)**
   - Red nucleus
   - Capsule of red nucleus (cr)

3. **Substantia nigra**
   - Pars compacta (SNC)
   - Pars reticulata (SNR)

4. **Thalamus**
   - Central medial thalamic nucleus (CeMe)
   - Centromedian thalamic nucleus (CM)
   - Parafascicular thalamic nucleus (PF)
   - Paraventricular thalamic nucleus (PV)
   - Reunions thalamic nucleus (Re)
   - Ventral anterior thalamic nucleus (VA)
   - Ventral lateral posterior thalamic nucleus (VLP)
   - Ventral lateral posterior thalamic nucleus, external part (VLPE)
   - Ventromedial thalamic nucleus (VM)
   - Ventral posterior medial nucleus, parvocellular part (MCP)
   - Zona incerta (ZID)

5. **Globus pallidus**
   - Internal globus pallidus (IG)

6. **Midbrain**

7. **Internal medullary lamina of thalamus (iml)**

8. **Optic tract (opt)**

9. **Ansa lenticularis (al)**

10. **Band of Giacomini (bG)**

11. **Comb system (comb)**

12. **Cerebello-rubro-thalamic fibers (crt)**

13. **Fasciculus retroflexus (fr)**

14. **Thalamic fasciculus (field H1)**

15. **Thalamic fasciculus (field H2)**

16. **Interpeduncular system (IPCi)**

17. **Tenia of fimbria (tfi)**

18. **Third ventricle (3V)**

19. **Anterior commissure (AC)**

20. **Posterior commissure (PC)**

These regions are involved in various motor functions and are targets for deep brain stimulation (DBS) in Parkinson's disease.
