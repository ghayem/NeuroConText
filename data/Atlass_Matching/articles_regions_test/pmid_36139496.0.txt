Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Postcentral gyrus
   - Superior parietal lobule

2. **Salience Network (SN)**
   - Left supramarginal gyrus
   - Right supramarginal gyrus
   - Postcentral gyrus
   - Brain stem
   - Precuneus cortex
   - Intracalcarine cortex
   - Cerebellum
   - Supracalcarine cortex

3. **Language Network**
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Inferior temporal gyrus
   - Lateral occipital cortex

4. **Frontoparietal Network**
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Precentral gyrus

5. **Other Regions**
   - Left supramarginal gyrus
   - Superior parietal lobule
   - Frontal network
   - Anterior cingulate cortex
   - Anterior insula (left/right)
   - Rostral prefrontal cortex (left/right)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotional regulation, stress response, and cognitive processing.
