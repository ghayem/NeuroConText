## Cognitive Functions and Associations

- **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**: Reward and punishment processing, recognition of angry expressions, reactive aggression, top-down affect regulation, moral cognition, stimulus-based learning, decision-making.
- **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**: Empathy, affective processing, moral reasoning, inferring and predicting mental and emotional states of others.
- **Amygdala**: Emotion processing, empathy, emotional arousal to fear and distress, responses to cues of danger and punishment.
- **Putamen**: Inhibitory control, reinforcement learning.
- **Pre-postcentral Gyrus and Supramarginal Gyrus**: Spatial localization, control of action, emotional contagion, empathic processing.
- **Orbitofrontal-paralimbic System**: Emotion regulation, motivation, reward processing, decision-making.
- **Frontostriatal Cortex**: Regulating social behavior, processing socio-emotional stimuli.
- **Mirror Neuron System (MNS)**: Imitating actions, understanding motor plans, emotional contagion, empathic processing.
