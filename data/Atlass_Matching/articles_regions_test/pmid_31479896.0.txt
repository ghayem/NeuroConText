Based on the neuroscientific article, the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned are:

1. **Frontal Lobe**
   - **Inferior frontal gyrus**
     - Pars triangularis (IFG tri)
     - Pars opercularis (IFG oper)
   - **Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)**
   - **Precentral gyrus**

2. **Parietal Lobe**
   - **Inferior parietal lobe**
     - Supramarginal gyrus (anterior and posterior)
     - Angular gyrus (ANG)
   - **Superior parietal lobule (SPL)**
   - **Posterior parietal areas**

3. **Occipital Lobe**
   - **Superior lateral occipital cortex (sLOC)**

4. **Temporal Lobe**
   - **Temporo-occipital middle temporal gyrus**
   - **Superior temporal areas**

5. **Basal Ganglia**
   - **Pallidum**

6. **Ventral Premotor Cortex**

7. **Dorsal Premotor Cortex**

8. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

9. **White Matter**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including praxis abilities, motor control, and visuospatial processing.
