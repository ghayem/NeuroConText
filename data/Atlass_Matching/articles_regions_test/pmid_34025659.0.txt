### Cognitive Functions:
- **Visual Functions**: Primary visual cortex (V1), calcarine, superior occipital gyrus, inferior occipital gyrus
- **Somatosensory Functions**: Postcentral gyrus (S1), paracentral lobule
- **Visuomotor Coordination**: Cerebellum (left cerebellum crus1, left cerebellum 10, vermis 9, cerebelum 6 left)
- **Memory and Emotion**: Hippocampus
- **Visual Information Processing**: Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG), middle temporal gyrus
- **Emotional Regulation**: Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)
- **Higher Cognitive Function**: Thalamus, superior parietal gyrus
