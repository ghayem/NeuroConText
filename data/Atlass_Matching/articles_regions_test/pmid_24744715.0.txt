## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Anterior cingulate gyrus (BA 32)
    - Medial frontal gyrus (BA 6)
    - Superior frontal gyrus (BA 8, 9)
    - Middle frontal gyrus (BA 10)
    - Orbitofrontal cortex

2. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Parietal sites (P9, P10)
    - Parieto-occipital sites (P9, PO7, P10)
    - Parietal and occipital electrodes (Pz, Oz, P4)
    - Parieto-occipital electrodes (P3, Pz, Oz)

3. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Left middle temporal gyrus (BA 39)
    - Temporo-parietal sites

4. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Occipital electrodes (Oz, O2, O1)
    - Occipital and parietal electrodes

5. **Central Regions**
    - Central and parietal sensors (Cz, CPz, Pz)
    - Central electrodes (Cz, CPz, C1)
    - Fronto-central sites (Cz, FCz, Fz)

6. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Left anterior cingulate (BA 32)

7. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Right anterior cingulate gyrus (BA 32)
    - Medial frontal gyrus (BA 6)

8. **Motor Cortex**
    - Right superior and medial frontal gyri (BA 6, 8)

9. **Visual Cortex**
    - Extrastriate visual cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including memory, attention, perception, decision-making, and motor planning.
