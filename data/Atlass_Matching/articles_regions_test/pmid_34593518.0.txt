Certainly! Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

2. **Left Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**

3. **Insular Cortex**

4. **Motor Cortex**
   - Primary motor cortex
   - Somatosensory cortex

5. **Parietal Operculum Cortex**

6. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

7. **Paracingulate Gyri**
   - Left paracingulate gyrus
   - Right paracingulate gyrus

8. **Dorsal ACC**

9. **Bilateral Insula**

10. **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (VMPFC)**

11. **Subgenual ACC**

12. **Right Insula**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: cognitive control, decision-making, emotion regulation, distress tolerance, and response inhibition.
