## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Middle frontal gyrus (BA 11/10)
    - Superior frontal gyrus (BA 11)
    - Precentral gyrus (BA 44)
    - Anterior cingulate gyrus

2. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Central-parietal regions
    - Parietal-occipital regions

3. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Parietal-occipital regions

4. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal plane (STP)
    - Planum temporale (PT)
    - Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)
    - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

5. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Left ventrolateral prefrontal cortex

6. **Limbic System**
    - Amygdala

7. **Other Regions**
    - Prefrontal cortex
    - Anterior cingulate cortex

- These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotional processing, semantic processing, memory retrieval, and perceptual fluency.
