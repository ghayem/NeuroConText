7. **Lingual Gyrus**

9. **Putamen**
   - Left putamen (L putamen)
   - Right putamen (R putamen)

10. **Thalamus**
    - Right thalamus (R thalamus)

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as:
  - **dlPFC**: Regulation of distressing affect and irregular heart rate, distress tolerance, narrative reconstruction, self-referential cognition, cortical thickness, and functional connectivity.
  - **ACC**: Synthesis of thoughts and feelings into a self-referential worldview and belief system.
  - **vmPFC**: Self-referential cognition.
  - **Precuneus**: Recoding trauma memories, abatement of sensitivity to trauma triggers.
  - **Frontal Gyrus**: Attention to threat stimuli, creative thinking, cognitive restructuring, executive functions.
  - **STG**: Auditory processing of lingual and sonic stimuli.
  - **Lingual Gyrus**: Divergent thinking, declarative memory reconstruction, emotion processing.
  - **Insula**: Emotion regulation, extinction of pain and fear responses.
  - **Putamen**: Cognitive processing of language and spoken language articulation.
  - **Thalamus**: Relaying afferent sensory stimuli from the autonomic nervous system to the frontal lobe.
