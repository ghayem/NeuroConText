Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Fronto-parietal brain network**
   - Ventral premotor cortex (vPM)
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)
   - Primary somatosensory cortex (PSC)
   - Temporoparietal cortex
   - Dorsal premotor cortex (PM)
   - Superior parietal lobe (SPL)
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)
   - Primary visual cortex (PVC)
   - Middle temporal area (MT/V5)
   - Primary auditory cortex (PAC)

2. **Subcortical structures**
   - Amygdala (AMG)

3. **Cingulate cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

4. **Insular cortex**
   - Anterior subdivision of the insula (aINS)

5. **Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)**

6. **Superior temporal sulcus (STS)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:
- Multisensory integration and bodily self-representation (vPM, IPS, PSC, temporoparietal cortex)
- Threat processing and emotional responses (AMG, ACC, aINS)
- Visuomotor processing (PVC, MT/V5)
- Auditory processing (PAC)
- Spatial awareness and attention (SPL, SMG)
- Perspective taking and self-other distinction (TPJ, SMG)
- Decision-making and social-emotional processing (ACC)
