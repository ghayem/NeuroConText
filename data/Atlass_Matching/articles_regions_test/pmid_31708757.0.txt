## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**

2. **Superior Parietal Cortex**

3. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Striatum

4. **Medial Temporal Lobe**

5. **Dorsal Frontoparietal Network**
    - Frontal Eye Field
    - Intraparietal Sulcus
    - Superior Parietal Lobe

6. **Ventral Frontoparietal Network**
    - Temporoparietal Junction
    - Inferior Frontal Gyrus
    - Middle Frontal Gyrus

7. **Right Middle Frontal Gyrus (r-MFG)**

8. **Inferior Temporal Cortex**

9. **Right Parietal Region**
    - Parietal Operculum
    - Supramarginal Gyrus

10. **Superior Temporal Gyrus**

11. **Temporal Cortex**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: working memory, attention, inhibitory control, and academic skills.
