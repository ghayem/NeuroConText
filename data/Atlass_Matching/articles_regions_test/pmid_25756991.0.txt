## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Temporal poles (TPs)
   - Anterior inferior temporal gyrus (aITG)
   - Anterior middle temporal gyrus (aMTG)
   - Anterior superior temporal gyrus (aSTG)
   - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Hippocampal and parahippocampal structures
   - Subiculum
   - Entorhinal cortex
   - Hippocampus proper (CA)
   - Inferior fusiform gyrus (FFG)

2. **Limbic Regions**
   - Amygdala
   - Insular complex
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Cingulate cortex
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (aCG)
   - Subcallosal gyrus (SCG)
   - Temporo-parietal junction (TPJ)

3. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Caudate nuclei

4. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Frontal operculum

5. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Inferior parietal lobe (IPL)

6. **White Matter Pathways**
   - Inferior longitudinal fasciculus (ILF)
   - Uncinate fasciculus (UF)

7. **Other Regions**
   - Thalamus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including semantic memory, language, and behavioral alterations.
