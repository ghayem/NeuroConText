5. **Lingual Gyrus (LG)**

6. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

7. **Middle Cingulate Cortex (MCC)**
   - Bilateral MCC

8. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
   - Left PCC/PCU

9. **Parahippocampal Gyrus (PHG)**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: pain processing, cognitive control, attention, memory, and emotional processing.
