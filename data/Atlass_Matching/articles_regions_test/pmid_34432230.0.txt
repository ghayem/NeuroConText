## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Frontal Regions**

2. **Temporal Regions**
    - Temporal pole
    - Temporal fusiform cortex
    - Temporal plane

3. **Limbic Regions**
    - Amygdala
    - Hippocampus
    - Parahippocampal gyrus
    - Anterior limbic network

4. **Cerebellum**
    - Dentate nucleus (DN)
    - Cerebellar cortex
    - Cerebellar lobules
    - Vermis
    - Fastigial nuclei

5. **Parietal Regions**
    - Posterior cingulate gyrus
    - Angular gyrus

6. **Occipital Regions**
    - Lateral occipital cortex

7. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Pulvinar
    - Ventral prefrontal cortex
    - Striatum
    - Thalamus
    - Prefrontal-striatal-thalamic circuit

8. **Other Regions**
    - Cerebral associative regions
    - Default Mode Network (DMN)

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including emotion regulation, affective processing, and cognitive impairments associated with bipolar disorder.
