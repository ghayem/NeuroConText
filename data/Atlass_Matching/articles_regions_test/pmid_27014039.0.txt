# Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Left Inferior Frontal Gyrus (LIFG)**
   - Pars opercularis
   - Pars triangularis
   - Pars orbitalis

2. **Left Middle Frontal Gyrus (LMFG)**

3. **Left Middle Temporal Gyrus (LMTG)**
   - Left posterior middle temporal gyrus (LpMTG)

4. **Left Precentral Gyrus (LPCG)**

5. **Supplementary Motor Areas (SMA)**

6. **Left Anterior Insula**

7. **Ventral Occipito-Temporal Cortex**

8. **Prefrontal Regions**

9. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Including LMFG

10. **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Including LIFG

11. **Posterior Brain Regions**
    - Involved in basic-level semantic processing

12. **Left Hemisphere Language Regions**
    - Left middle temporal gyrus (LMTG)
    - Pars opercularis and triangularis in the left inferior frontal gyrus (LIFG)

13. **Left Middle Frontal Gyrus (LMFG)**
    - Part of the multiple demands network
    - Also known as the task-positive or frontotemporal attention network

14. **Superior Longitudinal Fasciculus (SLF)**
    - Lateral portion
    - Arcuate fasciculus

15. **Inferior Occipito-Frontal Fasciculus (IFOF)**

16. **Middle and Inferior Longitudinal Fasciculi**

17. **Uncinate Fasciculus (UF)**

18. **Association U-fibers**
    - Connect pars opercularis, pars triangularis, and pars orbitalis in LIFG
    - Connect the frontal operculum to the dorsolateral prefrontal cortex

19. **Left Posterior Superior Temporal Gyrus (LpSTG)**

20. **Anterior Temporal Lobe (ATL)**

21. **Superior Temporal Plane (STP)**

22. **Planum Temporale (PT)**

23. **Temporo-Parieto-Occipital Junction (TPO)**

24. **Middle Superior Temporal Cortex**

25. **Anterior Portion of the pSTG/S**

### These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: language, memory, and executive functions
