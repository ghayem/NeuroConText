## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Dorso-lateral fronto-parietal cortex**

2. **Occipito-temporal cortex**
   - Fusiform gyrus

3. **Bilateral sensorimotor cortices**

4. **Superior temporal gyrus**

5. **Insula**

6. **Supplementary motor area (SMA)**
   - Right hemisphere

7. **Inferior occipito-temporal cortices**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions related to self and other face-recognition responses, including empathic and action responses.
