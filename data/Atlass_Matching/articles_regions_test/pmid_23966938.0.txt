## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Cerebellum**

2. **Supplementary Motor Area**

3. **Thalamus**

4. **Caudate Nucleus**

5. **Insula**

6. **Precentral Gyrus**

7. **Postcentral Gyrus**

8. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**

9. **Midline**

11. **Primary Motor Cortex**

12. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
