Based on the neuroscientific article provided, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Prefrontal cortex (PFC)**
   - Ventral anterior cingulate cortex
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
     - Ventral mPFC (vmPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Middle frontal region (including insula, from ventral to dorsal and medial to lateral regions)

2. **Temporal regions**
   - Posterior temporal regions
   - Superior temporal sulcus (STS)

3. **Parietal regions**
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Precuneus
   - Parietal lobule
   - Left posterior cingulate region

4. **Cingulate cortex**
   - Dorsal cingulate cluster
   - Anterior cingulate region
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)

5. **Insula**
   - Right posterior insula (PI)
   - Anterior insula

6. **Other regions**
   - Right post- and precentral regions (sensorimotor regions)
   - Amygdala
   - Occipital regions

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including moral cognition, social cognition, self-reflection, emotion processing, empathy, and guilt processing.
