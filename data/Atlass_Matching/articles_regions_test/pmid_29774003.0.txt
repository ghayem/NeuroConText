Based on the article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Cortical Midline**
   - Anterior cortical midline
   - Posterior cortical midline

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Lateral prefrontal cortex
   - Medial prefrontal gyrus
   - Superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
   - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Lateral temporal cortex

4. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Inferior parietal lobe (IPL)

5. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
   - Anteroinferior PCC

6. **Cerebellum**
   - Cerebellar posterior lobe (CPL)

7. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - PCC/precuneus
   - Bilateral IPL
   - Medial temporal gyrus
   - Medial prefrontal gyrus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:

- Emotional processing
- Attention
- Cognitive function
- Visuospatial memory and processing
- Executive functions
- Self-referential processing
- Social cognition
- Visuospatial skills
- Visuoconstructional skills
- Language (implied through the involvement of the temporal cortex)
- Memory (implied through the involvement of the temporal cortex and DMN)
