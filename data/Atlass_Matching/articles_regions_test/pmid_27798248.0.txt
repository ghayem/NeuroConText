## Brain regions and sub-regions:

1. **Mesolimbic dopaminergic system**
   - Striatum
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vMPFC)

2. **Prefrontal cortical network**
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate (dACC)
   - Lateral prefrontal cortex (LPFC)

3. **Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPJ)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: subjective value, top-down control, conflict monitoring, reappraisal, error correction, cognitive effort, and choice difficulty.
