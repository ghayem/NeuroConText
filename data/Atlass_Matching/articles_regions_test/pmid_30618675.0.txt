Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Cortical areas**
   - Somatosensory cortex
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
   - Inferior parietal cortex
   - Cingulate cortex
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

2. **Somatosensory cortex**
   - Primary somatosensory cortex (S1)
   - Secondary somatosensory cortex (S2)

3. **Motor cortex**
   - Primary motor cortex (M1)
   - Precentral gyrus (PCG)

4. **Frontal cortex**
   - Frontal operculum (FO)

5. **Thalamus**

6. **White matter (WM)**

7. **Gray matter (GM)**

8. **Cerebrospinal fluid (CSF)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including sensorimotor integration, movement planning and execution, cognitive monitoring, and sensory processing.
