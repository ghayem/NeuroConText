## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Cerebellum**
    - Cerebellar culmen

2. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Left superior frontal gyrus

3. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Precuneus
    - Left postcentral gyrus

4. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
    - Precentral gyrus

5. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Right inferior temporal gyrus

6. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Bilateral lingual gyrus

7. **Insular Cortex**
    - Right insula

8. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
    - Right posterior cingulate cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including motor coordination, executive functions, visual-spatial perception, memory, language processing, and sensory processing.
