     - Fornix

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including but not limited to:
- **Frontal Lobe**: Executive functions, decision-making, emotion regulation, and motor control.
- **Limbic System**: Emotion processing, memory, and autonomic regulation.
- **Temporal Lobe**: Language, memory, emotion processing, and sensory integration.
- **Parietal Lobe**: Spatial orientation, attention, and sensory processing.
- **Occipital Lobe**: Visual processing.
- **Cerebellum**: Motor control, cognitive functions, and emotional regulation.
- **Basal Ganglia**: Motor control, cognitive functions, and emotion regulation.
- **Brainstem**: Autonomic functions, arousal, and respiratory control.

These regions and their functions are interconnected and work together to support various cognitive, emotional, and behavioral processes.
