## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Hippocampus**
   - Hippocampal tail
   - Anterior hippocampus

2. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
   - Parahippocampal cortex (PHC)
   - Entorhinal cortex
   - Postsubiculum

3. **Retrosplenial Cortex (RSC)**

4. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
   - Dorsal mPFC
   - Ventral mPFC

5. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Medial prefrontal and posterior cingulate/precuneus areas
   - Hippocampus
   - Retrosplenial cortex (RSC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)

6. **Central Executive Network (CEN)**
   - Fronto-parietal regions
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC)
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Posterior parietal cortex
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)

7. **Parahippocampal Cortex (PHC)**
   - Collateral sulcus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Subiculum region

8. **Thalamus**

9. **Caudate**

10. **Cingulate**
   - Cingulate sulcus

11. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**

12. **Superior Temporal Sulcus**

13. **Pre-central Gyrus**

14. **Postcentral Gyrus**

15. **Superior Parietal Lobule**

16. **Precuneus**

17. **Cerebellum**

18. **Temporo-occipital Gyrus**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: path integration, navigation, episodic memory, working memory, executive functions, self-referential processing, and spatial processing.
