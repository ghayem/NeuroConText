Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Sub-genual cingulate cortex
    - Anterior midcingulate cortex (aMCC)
    - Dorsal anterior cingulate

2. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)

3. **Insula**
    - Posterior insula

4. **Cerebellum**
    - Cerebellar lobule IX

5. **Hippocampus**
    - Left hippocampus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions and emotional processes, including:

- **Cingulate Cortex**: Emotion regulation, cognitive control, error monitoring, and conflict resolution.
- **Temporal Cortex (ITG)**: Visual object recognition and memory.
- **Insula**: Interoception, emotional awareness, and bodily physiological state representation.
- **Cerebellum (Lobule IX)**: Part of the default mode network, involved in off-task 'resting' brain activity.
- **Hippocampus**: Memory formation, learning, and spatial navigation.
