## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Hippocampus**

2. **Amygdala**

3. **Anterior Cingulate Gyrus**

4. **Cingulate Cortex**

5. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**

6. **Cerebellum**

7. **Anterior Cingulate**

8. **Right Parietal Cortex**

9. **Frontal Cortex**

10. **Superior Temporal Cortex**

11. **Right Superior Frontal Gyrus**

12. **Left Frontal Lobe**

13. **Right Frontal Lobe**

14. **Right Corpus Callosum**

15. **Precuneus**

16. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex**

17. **Left Inferior Parietal Lobule**

18. **Right Inferior Frontal Gyrus**

19. **Ventricles**

20. **White Matter (WM)**

21. **Gray Matter (GM)**

22. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

23. **Default Mode Network (DMN)** (a network rather than a specific region, but mentioned in the context of the precuneus)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including memory formation and maintenance, emotional processing, executive functions, spatial and temporal orientation, and the integration of past experiences with recent memories.
