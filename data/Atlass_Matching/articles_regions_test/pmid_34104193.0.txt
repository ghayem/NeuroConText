## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Frontal Brain Regions**
    - Orbital frontal gyrus (OFG)
    - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

2. **Temporal Brain Regions**
    - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
    - Medial temporal lobe
        - Hippocampus
        - Entorhinal cortex

3. **Parietal Brain Regions**
    - Posteromedial cortices

4. **Subcortical Brain Sites**

5. **White Matter Regions**
    - Corpus callosum
    - Capsule
    - Periventricular white matter
    - Cingulum
    - Occipitofrontal fasciculi

6. **Medial Frontal Cortex**

7. **Temporal Cortical Regions**

8. **Occipital Cortices**

9. **Parietal Cortices**

10. **Lateral Temporal Cortex**

11. **Lateral Parietal Cortex**

12. **Medial/lateral Prefrontal Cortex**

13. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex**

    - These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: executive function, semantic memory, episodic memory, verbal short-term memory, global cognitive function, psychomotor speed, and executive control.
