## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Posterior Superior Temporal Cortex**
   - Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)
   - Planum temporale (PT)
   - Inferior parietal cortex
   - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

2. **Anterior Sensorimotor Cortex**
   - Prefrontal cortex
   - Anterior temporal cortex
   - Anterior sensorimotor region
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

3. **Angular Gyrus**
   - Left angular gyrus

4. **Pre-central and Post-central Gyri**
   - Inferolateral post-central gyrus
   - Inferior two-thirds of the pre-central and post-central gyri

5. **Temporal Pole**
   - Left temporal pole

6. **Auditory Cortex**
   - Auditory processing networks

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as language, auditory processing, and perceptual learning.
