## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Ventral Striatum**
    - Nucleus accumbens (Nacc)

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex
    - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex
    - Medial prefrontal cortex

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as reward processing, social behavior, and decision-making.
