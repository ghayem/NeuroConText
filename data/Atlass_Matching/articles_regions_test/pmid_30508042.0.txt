## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Orbitofrontal cortex
   - Frontal pole
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus

2. **Anterior Temporal Lobe**
   - Temporal pole
   - Anterior divisions of the superior temporal gyrus
   - Anterior divisions of the middle temporal gyrus
   - Anterior divisions of the inferior temporal gyrus

3. **Insula**

4. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

5. **Cingulate Cortex**

6. **Uncinate Fasciculus**

7. **Superior Longitudinal Fasciculus**

8. **Inferior Longitudinal Fasciculus**

9. **Inferior Fronto-Occipital Fasciculus**

10. **Internal Capsule**

11. **Genu of the Corpus Callosum**

12. **Body of the Corpus Callosum**

13. **Splenium of the Corpus Callosum**

14. **Fornix**

15. **Cingulum Bundle**

16. **Forceps Minor**

17. **Anterior Thalamic Radiation**

18. **Anterior Corona Radiata**

19. **Amygdala**

20. **Hippocampus**

21. **Parietal Lobe**

22. **Cerebellum**

23. **Thalamus**

- These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including language, memory, executive functions, attention, mental processing speed, social cognition, and visuoconstruction.
