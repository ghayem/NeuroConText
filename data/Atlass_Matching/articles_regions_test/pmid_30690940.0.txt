Sure, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)
   - Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)
   - Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex (VLPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)

2. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)
   - Middle Temporal Gyrus (MTG)
   - Inferior Temporal Gyrus (ITG)

3. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Superior Parietal Gyrus
   - Angular Gyrus
   - Postcentral Gyrus
   - Precuneus

4. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Superior Occipital Gyrus (SOG)
   - Middle Occipital Gyrus (MOG)
   - Inferior Occipital Gyrus (IOG)

5. **Limbic System**
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal Gyrus
   - Amygdala

6. **Other Regions**
   - Fusiform Gyrus
   - Insula
   - Caudate Nucleus
   - Precentral Gyrus

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as working memory, emotional processing, cognitive inhibition, and goal-directed behavior.
