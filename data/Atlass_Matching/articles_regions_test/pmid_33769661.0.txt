## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Precentral Cortex**
   - Primary motor cortex
   - Premotor cortex

2. **Transverse Temporal Cortex**
   - Heschl's gyrus
   - Primary auditory cortex

3. **Superior Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Superior temporal sulcus

4. **Cuneus**
   - Medial occipital gyrus
   - Visual association cortex

5. **Rostral Middle Frontal Cortex**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Frontal eye field

6. **Subcortical Gray Matter Structures**
   - Hippocampus

7. **White Matter Volume (WMV)**
   - White matter tracts

8. **Cortical Gray Matter Volume (GMV)**
   - Cortical gray matter

9. **Total Brain Volume (TBV)**
   - Overall brain volume

10. **Brain Parenchymal Fraction**
    - Proportion of brain tissue relative to intracranial volume

11. **Medial Temporal Lobe Structures**
    - Hippocampus
    - Parahippocampal gyrus
    - Entorhinal cortex

12. **Left Rostral Middle Frontal Cortex**
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
    - Frontal eye field

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including motor regulation, auditory processing, visual information processing, and higher cognitive functions such as language processing and executive function.
