## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

2. **Premotor Cortices**
   - Dorsal Premotor Cortex (PMd)
   - Ventral Premotor Cortex (PMv)

3. **Parietal Lobule**
   - Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)
   - Superior Parietal Lobule (SPL)

4. **Prefrontal Areas**

5. **Cingulate Cortex**

6. **Cerebellar Regions**

7. **Cerebral Palsy (CP)**
   - Perinatal ischemic stroke
   - Malformations of white-matter
   - Unilateral porencephalic cavities (or cysts)
   - Unilateral brain lesion
   - Periventricular and deep white matter
   - Periventricular leukomalacia (PVL)
   - Subcortical leukomalacia

8. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Putamen
   - Pallidum

9. **Thalamus**

10. **Precuneus**

11. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
    - IFG pars opercularis

12. **Middle Frontal Gyrus (MfG)**

13. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**

14. **Superior Frontal Gyrus/Sulcus**

15. **Posterior Superior Temporal Gyri (pSTG/MTG)**

16. **Intraparietal Sulcus (IPS)**

17. **Cerebellum**
    - Lobule VI

18. **Superior Temporal Gyrus/Sulcus (STG/MTG)**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as motor planning, motor imagery, sensorimotor processing, and executive functions.
