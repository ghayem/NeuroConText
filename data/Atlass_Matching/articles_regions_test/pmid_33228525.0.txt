These regions are involved in the following cognitive functions and attention systems:

- **Alerting**: Thalamus, Temporoparietal Junction, Mid-Frontal Gyrus
- **Orienting**: Superior Parietal Cortex, Frontal Eye Field, Fusiform Face Area (FFA)
- **Executive Control (EC)**: Anterior Cingulate Cortex, Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex, Inferior Frontal Gyrus, Precentral Gyrus (for orienting and saccades)
- **Face Perception**: Fusiform Gyrus (FFA)
- **Saccades ( Eye Movements)**: Precentral Gyrus, Frontal Eye Field
- **Cholinergic Function**: Fusiform Gyrus (related to orienting and social interaction)
