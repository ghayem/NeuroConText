## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Cerebrum**
    - **Premotor Cortex (PMC)**
    - **Primary Motor Cortex (M1)**
    - **Inferior Parietal Areas**
    - **Superior Parietal Lobule (SPL)**
    - **Temporo–parietal Junction (TPJ)**
    - **Precuneus (PCun)**
    - **Primary Somatosensory Cortex (S1)**
    - **Postcentral Gyrus (PoCG)**
    - **Precentral Gyrus (PrCG)**
    - **Central Operculum (COp)**
    - **Supplementary Motor Cortex (SMC)**
    - **Superior Frontal Gyrus (SFG)**
    - **Heschl’s Gyrus (HG)**
    - **Planum Temporale (PT)**
    - **Planum Polare (PP)**
    - **Temporal Pole (TP)**

2. **Cerebellum**
    - **Cerebellar Sensorimotor Regions IV and V**
    - **Cerebellar Motor Region VIII**
    - **Cerebellar Lobule VIIIb**

3. **Basal Ganglia (BG)**
    - **Pallidum**
    - **Putamen**
    - **Caudate Nucleus**

4. **Brainstem**
    - **Thalamus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including sensorimotor integration, motor control, timing and rhythm processing, audio-motor coordination, and self-other integration and segregation during joint action.
