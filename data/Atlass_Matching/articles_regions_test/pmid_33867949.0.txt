## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Amygdala**
    - Left amygdala
    - Right amygdala

2. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

3. **Rostral Anterior Cingulate Cortex (rACC)**
    - Left rostral anterior cingulate cortex (rACC)

4. **Dorsal/Midcingulate Cortex**

5. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**

6. **Subgenual Anterior Cingulate Cortex (sgACC)**

7. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**
    - Left superior temporal gyrus (STG)

8. **Insula**
    - Left insula

9. **Postcentral Gyrus (PCG)**
    - Bilateral postcentral gyrus (PCG)

10. **Parahippocampal Gyrus (PHG)**
    - Right parahippocampal gyrus (PHG)

11. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
    - Right inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

12. **Cerebellum**
    - Right cerebellum

These regions are involved in emotional processing, cognitive control, memory encoding, language comprehension, sensory information processing, and executive functions.
