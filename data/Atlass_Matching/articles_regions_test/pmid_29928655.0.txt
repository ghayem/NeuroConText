## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Frontopolar area (fpPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (sACC)

3. **Motor Cortex**
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Primary motor cortical areas
   - Paramedian motor cortex (leg region)
   - Postcentral gyrus
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)

4. **Visual Cortex (VC)**

5. **Thalamus**
   - Bilateral thalamus
   - Prefrontal projection zone of the thalamus
   - Mediodorsal thalamus

6. **Cerebellum**
   - Cerebellar lobules
   - Cerebellar vermis
   - Posterior cerebellar lobules
   - Cerebellar lobule VI

7. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)
   - Posterior parietal cortex (PPC)
   - Superior parietal lobule

8. **Frontal Eye Fields (FEF)**

9. **Parietal Eye Fields (PEF)**

10. **Supplemental Eye Fields (SEF)**

11. **Medial Superior Temporal (MST)**

12. **Insular Cortex**
   - Anterior insula (AI)
   - Insular sulcus

13. **Amygdala**

14. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

15. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Middle temporal gyrus

16. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Middle occipital gyrus
   - Calcarine fissure

17. **Lingual Gyrus**

18. **Cuneus**

19. **Precuneus**

20. **Vestibular Cortex**
   - Postero-insular vestibular cortex

21. **Hippocampus**

22. **Somatosensory Cortex (SC)**

23. **Dentate Gyrus (DG)**

24. **Fear-Generation Network (FGN)**

25. **Interoceptive Network (IN)**

26. **Orbitofrontal Network (OFN)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including mood regulation, motor control, emotional processing, and interoception.
