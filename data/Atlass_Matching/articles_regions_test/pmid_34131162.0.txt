## The brain regions:

1. **Cerebral Cortex**
    - **Primary Motor Cortex (M1)**
    - **Primary Somatosensory Cortex (S1)**
    - **Premotor Cortex (PM)**
    - **Superior Parietal Lobule (SPL)**
    - **Intraparietal Sulcus (IPS)**
    - **Sensorimotor Cortex**
        - Brodmann Area 1 (BA1)
        - Brodmann Area 4a (BA4a)
        - Brodmann Area 6 (BA6)
    - **Insular Cortex (Insula Id1)**
    - **Inferior Parietal Lobule (PFop, PFcm, PFm)**
    - **Opercular Cortex (Parietal Operculum OP3)**
    - **Visual Cortex**
        - Brodmann Area 17 (BA17)
        - Brodmann Area 18 (BA18)
    - **Intra-parietal Sulcus (hIP2)**
    - **Broca’s Area (BA44)**

2. **Cerebellum**
    - **Regions I–IV**
    - **Region VI**
    - **Region VIIIa**
    - **Crus I**
    - **Crus II**

3. **Subcortical Structures**
    - **Thalamus**
    - **Putamen**
    - **Caudate Nucleus**
    - **Basal Ganglia**
    - **Midbrain**
    - **Pons**
    - **Medulla**
        - Ventral Medullary Reticular Nucleus
        - Dorsal Medullary Reticular Nucleus
    - **Periaqueductal Gray Substance**
    - **Raphe Nuclei**
    - **Reticular Formation (RF)**
        - Medial Tract
        - Lateral Tract
    - **Pontine Nucleus**
    - **Medial Lemniscus**

4. **Brainstem**
    - **Medullary Reticular Formation**
    - **Pontomedullary Reticular Formation**
    - **Parieto-occipito-temporo-pontine (cortico-reticular) Pathway**

5. **White Matter Tracts**
    - **Corticospinal Tract (CST)**
    - **Reticulospinal Tract (RST)**
        - Medial Reticulospinal Tract
        - Lateral Reticulospinal Tract

- These regions are involved in these cognitive and motor functions: motor control, sensory processing, motor initiation, postural maintenance, sensory-motor integration, and the modulation of reflexes.
