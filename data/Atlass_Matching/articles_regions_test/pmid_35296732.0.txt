## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**

2. **Frontopolar Cortex**

3. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**

4. **Anterior Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (aDLPFC)**

5. **Posterior Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (pDLPFC)**

6. **Dorsal Anterior Cingulate Cortex (dACC)**

7. **Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex (dmPFC)**
    - Brodmann area (BA) 8/32

8. **Premotor Cortex (PM)**

9. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**

10. **Presupplementary Motor Area (preSMA)**
    - Brodmann area (BA) 6

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: control processes, semantic retrieval, inhibition of representations, semantic processing, working memory (WM) functions, attentional control, attentional modulation, and switching from external stimuli to internal representations.
