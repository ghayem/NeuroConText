## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Auditory Cortex**
   - Primary auditory cortex
   - Left superior temporal gyrus
   - Heschl’s gyrus

2. **Language Centres**
   - Broca’s area
   - Wernicke’s area

3. **Left Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Broca’s area

4. **Left Superior Temporal Gyrus**
   - Primary auditory cortex for speech perception
   - Wernicke’s area for speech comprehension

5. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

6. **Left Inferior Parietal Lobule**
   - Postcentral gyrus

7. **Insula**
   - Left insula
   - Right insula

8. **Cerebellum**
   - Right anterior lobe of the cerebellum
   - Culmen
   - Dentate

9. **Left Arcuate Fasciculus**

10. **Right Arcuate Fasciculus**

11. **Claustrum**

12. **Putamen**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as inner speech production, inner speech perception, self-referential processing, and salience attribution.
