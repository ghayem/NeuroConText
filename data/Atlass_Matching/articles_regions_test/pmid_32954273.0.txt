1. **Parahippocampal place area**

5. **Face processing network**

6. **Lateral occipital area (LO)**

7. **Visual word form area (VWFA)**

8. **Occipital face area (OFA)**

9. **Fusiform face area (FFA)**

10. **Anterior temporal lobe (ATL)**

11. **Posterior fusiform gyrus**

12. **Inferior frontal gyrus**

13. **Middle temporal gyrus**

14. **Lingual gyri**

15. **Lateral occipital cortex**

16. **Temporo-occipital fusiform cortex**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including face recognition, body perception, topographical processing, and reading.
