## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Cerebellum**
    - Posterior cerebellar crus (1 and 2)
    - Lobule IX

2. **Cerebral Cortex**
    - Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)
        - Angular gyrus
    - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
        - Superior medial gyrus
    - Medial precuneus
    - Middle temporal frontal gyrus
    - Middle frontal gyrus
        - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
    - Superior frontal gyrus
    - Hippocampus

3. **Other Cortical Areas**
    - Precuneus
    - Supramarginal gyrus
    - Superior temporal gyrus
    - Fusiform gyrus
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Cuneus

## Cognitive Functions:
These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as social mentalizing, prediction of social action sequences, sequencing of social actions, and metacognition.
