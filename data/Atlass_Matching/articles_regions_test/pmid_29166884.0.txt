## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Cerebellum**
   - Vermis
   - Tonsil
   - Horizontal fissure
   - Anterior lobe
   - Posterior lobe
   - Left cerebellar posterior lobe

2. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Right middle frontal gyrus
   - Right superior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus

3. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Right middle occipital gyrus
   - Superior occipital gyrus

4. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Left supplementary motor area (SMA)
   - Bilateral precentral gyrus
   - Right precentral gyrus
   - Posterior central gyrus

5. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Hippocampus

6. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Left caudate lobe
   - Left putamen
   - Bilateral caudate nucleus

7. **Other Regions**
   - Lateral ventricle
   - White matter
   - Gray matter

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions such as motor skills, working memory, language, and emotional control.
