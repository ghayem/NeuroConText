## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Fusiform Face Area (FFA)**
   - Temporal fusiform cortex (fusiform gyrus)

2. **Occipital Face Area (OFA)**
   - Lateral occipital cortex (inferior occipital gyrus)

3. **Posterior Superior Temporal Sulcus (pSTS)**
   - Face-selective area in the pSTS

4. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**
   - Superior temporal gyrus

5. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus

6. **Amygdala**
   - Amygdala

7. **Insula**
   - Insula

8. **Middle Temporal Gyrus (MTG)**
   - Middle temporal gyrus

9. **Supramarginal Gyrus**
   - Supramarginal gyrus

10. **Lingual Gyrus**
    - Lingual gyrus

11. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**
    - Parahippocampal gyrus

12. **Postcentral Gyrus**
    - Postcentral gyrus

13. **Heschl’s Gyrus**
    - Heschl’s gyrus

14. **Hippocampus**
    - Hippocampus
