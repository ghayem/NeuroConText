## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Cerebral Cortex**
   - Bilateral cerebral cortex

2. **Medulla Oblongata**

3. **Motor Cortex**
   - Bilateral motor cortices
   - Affected hemisphere motor cortex (M1)
   - Unaffected hemisphere motor cortex
   - Left suprahyoid motor cortex
   - Right suprahyoid motor cortex

4. **Primary Motor Cortex**
   - Abductor pollicis brevis muscle

5. **Pharyngeal Motor Cortex**
   - Ipsilateral pharyngeal motor cortex
   - Contralateral pharyngeal motor cortex

6. **Primary Sensorimotor Cortex**
   - Left primary sensorimotor cortex

7. **Primary Somatosensory Cortex**
   - Right postcentral cortex

8. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (BA 48)**
   - Left inferior frontal gyrus

9. **Cerebellum**
   - Left cerebellum

10. **Medial Frontal Gyrus (BA 11)**
    - Left medial frontal gyrus

11. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (BA 48)**
    - Right superior temporal gyrus

12. **Superior Frontal Gyrus (BA 10)**
    - Right superior frontal gyrus

13. **Postcentral Gyrus (BA 3)**
    - Right postcentral gyrus

14. **Paracentral Lobule (BA 4)**
    - Left paracentral lobule

15. **Superior Middle Temporal Gyrus**
    - Right superior middle temporal gyrus

16. **First Dorsal Interosseous (FDI) Muscle**

17. **Suprahyoid Muscle**

18. **Corpus Callosum**

19. **Basal Ganglia**

20. **Thalamus**

21. **Cingulate Gyrus**

22. **Cranial Nerves**

These regions are involved in various sensorimotor tasks, including swallowing, and in the neural mechanisms of dysphagia and its treatment using transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) and theta-burst stimulation (TBS).
