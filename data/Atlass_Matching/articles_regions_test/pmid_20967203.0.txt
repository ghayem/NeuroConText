## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Default Network (DN)**
   - Angular gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Medial temporal lobes
   - Ventromedial frontal cortex
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Cerebellum
   - Posterior cingulate (PCC)
   - Inferior frontal gyri
   - Subcortical areas

2. **Task-Positive Network (TPN)**
   - Anterior portion of the insula
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Anterior cingulate
   - Lingual gyrus
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Parietal areas
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)

3. **Other Regions**
   - Medial frontal gyrus
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Amygdala
   - Fusiform face area
   - Precuneus
   - Anterior cingulate
   - Paracentral lobule
   - Motor cortex
   - Medial frontal/SMA
   - Postcentral gyrus

   - These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: cognitive tasks, language, motor functions, and visual processing.
