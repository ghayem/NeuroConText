## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
    - Ventral medial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
    - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC)

2. **Hippocampus**
    - Longitudinal axis of the hippocampus

3. **Angular Gyrus**

4. **Medial Temporal Lobe**
    - Parahippocampal gyrus

5. **Cingulate and Paracingulate Gyrus**

6. **Lateral Occipital Cortex**

7. **Parietal Lobe**

8. **Cerebrospinal Fluid Compartments**

9. **White Matter**

10. **Cerebellum**

11. **Brainstem**

    - These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: memory, executive functions, cognitive control, schema formation, and associative memory processes.
