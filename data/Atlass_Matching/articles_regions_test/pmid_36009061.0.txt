## These regions are involved in these cognitive functions:
- **Executive functions**: Planning, attentional regulation, cognitive flexibility, working memory, behavioral inhibition, and emotion regulation.
- **Memory**: Visuospatial memory, episodic memory retrieval.
- **Emotion and Cognition**: Memory, social, decision-making, emotional, and cognitive functions.
- **Sensory and Motor Functions**: Visual information processing, attention, emotion processing, and motor functions.
- **Self-Processing and Consciousness**: Higher-order cognitive functions, self-processing, and consciousness.
- **Perception and Social Cognition**: Bottom-up perception and social cognition.
