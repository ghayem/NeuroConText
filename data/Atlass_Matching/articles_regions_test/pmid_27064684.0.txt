## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article

1. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Posterior parietal cortex
   - Parietal lobule
   - Inferior parietal lobule

2. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Occipital cortex

3. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Frontal cortex
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Paracentral lobule

4. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Cingulate gyrus

6. **Precuneus**

7. **Temporo-Parieto-Occipital Regions**

8. **Posterior Cingulate and Parietal Association Regions**

9. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

10. **Postcentral Gyrus**

11. **Supramarginal Gyrus**

12. **Angular Gyrus**

13. **Cuneus**

14. **Nucleus Basalis of Meynert**

15. **Supranuclear Gaze**

16. **Posterior Parietal-Occipital Lobes**

17. **Mesocortical Dopaminergic System**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including attention, memory, executive function, language, visuospatial function, and overall cognition.
