## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Frontal Cortex**
    - **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**
    - **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex (VLPFC)**
    - **Posterior Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
    - **Superior Frontal Gyrus (SFG)**
    - **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
    - **Supplementary Eye Fields (SEF)**
    - **Frontal Eye Fields (FEF)**

2. **Parietal Cortex**
    - **Posterior Parietal Cortex (PPC)**
    - **Superior Parietal Lobule (SPL)**
    - **Anterior Intraparietal Sulcus (aIPS)**
    - **Superior Frontal Sulcus**

3. **Temporal Cortex**
    - **Left Temporo-Parietal Junction (TPJ)**
    - **Left Ventral Occipital Complex (VOC)**
    - **Medial Temporal Lobe**
    - **Hippocampus**

4. **Occipital Cortex**
    - **Visual Cortex**

5. **Other Regions**
    - **Caudate**
    - **Insula**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as visual working memory (VWM), visual cognition, attention, and executive functions.
