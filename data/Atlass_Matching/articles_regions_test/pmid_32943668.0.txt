# Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Striatum**
   - Dorsal striatum
     - Caudate
     - Putamen
   - Ventral striatum
     - Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
     - Pars opercularis
     - Pars triangularis
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Frontal operculum
   - Frontal pole

3. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**

4. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Paracingulate gyrus

5. **Insular Cortex**
   - Anterior insula
   - Bilateral anterior insula

6. **Parietal Regions**
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex/supplementary motor area (dmPFC/SMA)

7. **Other Regions**
   - Thalamus
   - Brain stem
   - Temporal pole

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including reward processing, decision-making, curiosity, salience detection, uncertainty, cognitive control, and emotional processing.
