1. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Auditory cortex
   - Temporal voice area
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Mesial temporal structures (e.g., amygdala, hippocampus)

2. **Limbic and Subcortical Structures**
   - Amygdala
   - Insula

3. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Dorsal medial prefrontal cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex

4. **Temporo-parietal Junction (TPJ)**
   - Right TPJ

5. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

6. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

7. **Anterior Temporal Cortex**

8. **Lateral Parietal Cortex**
   - TPJ and posterior superior temporal sulcus (pSTS)

9. **Entorhinal Cortex/Hippocampus**

10. **Fusiform Gyrus**
    - Right fusiform

11. **Cerebellum**
    - Inferior semi-lunar lobule

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: social cognition, emotion recognition, and mentalizing.
