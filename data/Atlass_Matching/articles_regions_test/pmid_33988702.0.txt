### These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as:
- Empathy for pain
- Emotional processing
- Aesthetic judgement (AJ)
- Sensorimotor and visceromotor engagement
- Motor mirror-neuron mechanism
- Face perceptual analysis
- Visual and self-referential processing
- Memory retrieval and self-processing operations
- Aesthetic appreciation and evaluation
- Emotional experience and recognition
