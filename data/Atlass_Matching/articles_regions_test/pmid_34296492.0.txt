1. **Hippocampus**

   - Median cingulate cortex (MCC)

14. **Temporal Pole**
    - Right temporal pole

15. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**
    - Right middle temporal gyrus

16. **Amygdala**
    - Right amygdala

17. **Middle Occipital Gyrus**
    - Left middle occipital gyrus

18. **Superior Frontal Gyrus**
    - Bilateral superior frontal gyrus

19. **Precuneus**
    - Bilateral precuneus

20. **Subcallosal Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including emotion regulation, attention, memory, reward processing, cognitive control, body image perception, and energy sensing.
