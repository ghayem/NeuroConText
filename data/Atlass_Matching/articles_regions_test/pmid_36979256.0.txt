## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
   - Perirhinal/entorhinal cortex
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal cortex

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Medial posterior frontal cortex
   - Anterior prefrontal cortex
   - Prefrontal cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

3. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Precuneus
   - Parietal regions

4. **Visual-Processing Regions**
   - Visual cortex (V1)
   - Cuneus
   - Lingual gyrus

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Cingulate sulcus

6. **Insular Cortex**
   - Insula

7. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Putamen

8. **Sensorimotor Regions**
   - Sensorimotor cortex

9. **Anterior Lateral Prefrontal Cortex**

10. **Default Mode Network**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: long-term memory, source memory, item memory, spatial memory, verbal processing, and spatial processing.
