## The brain regions:

1. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Frontal precentral gyrus (BA 6/BA 4)
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Medial frontal gyrus (BA 6)
   - Superior frontal gyrus (BA 6, BA 9)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (BA 44, BA 47)
   - Fronto-insular cortex

2. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior temporal gyri
   - Middle temporal gyrus (BA 21, BA 22)
   - Inferior temporal gyrus (BA 20)
   - Temporal lobe
   - Temporal fusiform gyrus (BA 20)
   - Temporoparietal regions
   - Right temporal supramarginal gyrus (BA 40)

3. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Parietal association cortices
   - Parietal postcentral gyrus (BA 43)
   - Parietal precuneus (BA 7)
   - Inferior parietal lobe (BA 40)
   - Superior parietal gyrus (BA 7)

4. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Occipital lingual gyrus (BA 30)

5. **Cerebellum**
   - Anterior cerebellum
   - Cerebellar cortex

6. **Thalamus**
   - Left thalamus

7. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Caudate (body)
   - Putamen
   - Lentiform nucleus
   - Right nucleus accumbens

8. **Limbic System**
   - Amygdala
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus (BA 27)
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex
   - Left rostral anterior cingulate cortex
   - Right fronto-insular cortex
   - Insula (BA 13)
   - Extra nucleus (BA 13)

9. **Brainstem**

10. **Motor and Supplementary Motor Cortices**

11. **Uncus**

12. **Fusiform Gyrus (BA 37)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including sensory processing, motor control, language, memory, emotion regulation, and executive functions.
