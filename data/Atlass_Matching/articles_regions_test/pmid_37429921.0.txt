1. **Primary Taste Cortex**
   - Anterior insula/frontal operculum

2. **Secondary Cortical Taste Area**
   - Caudolateral orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)

3. **Primary Olfactory Cortex**
   - Anterior olfactory nucleus
   - Pyriform cortex
   - Rostral entorhinal cortex
   - Amygdala
   - Periamygdaloid complex regions

4. **Secondary Olfactory Cortex**
   - Orbitofrontal cortex

5. **Overlapping Regions for Taste and Odor**
   - Insula
   - Operculum
   - OFC
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

6. **Other Activated Brain Regions**
   - Anterior dorsal insula
   - Anterior ventral insula
   - Posterior parietal
   - Right inferior frontal operculum (FO)
   - Right rolandic operculum
   - Right ACC
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Angular gyrus
   - Claustrum
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Inferior occipital gyrus
   - Substantia nigra
   - Rectus
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Hippocampus
   - Temporal pole

7. **Regions Related to Pleasantness**
   - Posterior cingulate gyrus
   - Substantia nigra compacta
   - Precuneus
   - Medial temporal-lobe

8. **Regions Related to Motor Planning**
   - Precentral gyrus

9. **Regions Related to Emotional Processing**
   - Amygdala

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including sensory perception, emotional processing, motor planning, and reward processing.
