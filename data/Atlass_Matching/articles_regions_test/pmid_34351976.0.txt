Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (sgACC)
   - Pregenual anterior cingulate cortex (pgACC)

2. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Right ventral prefrontal cortex (VPFC)
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
   - Pars opercularis of the left inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

3. **Precuneus**

4. **Insula**
   - Left insula (AI)

5. **Superior Temporal Gyrus**

6. **Temporal Pole**

7. **Superior Parietal Lobe (SPL)**

8. **Frontal Eye Fields (FEF)**
   - Bilateral frontal eye fields located in the superior frontal gyrus

9. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

10. **Postcentral Gyrus**

11. **Middle Temporal Cortex**
    - Extending to fusiform gyrus (FFG)

12. **Occipital Regions**
    - Bilateral occipital regions (V5/MT)

13. **Cerebellum**
    - Left cerebellum

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including social exclusion, emotional regulation, and visuo-motor processing.
