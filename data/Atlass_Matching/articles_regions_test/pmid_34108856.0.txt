## The brain regions:

1. **Thalamus**
   - Bilateral thalamus

2. **Supplemental Motor Area (SMA)**
   - Right supplemental motor area (SMA)
   - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex (rACC)

3. **Superior Frontal Gyrus (SFG)**
   - Right superior frontal gyrus (SFG)

4. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex (rACC)

5. **Precuneus (PCU)**
   - Right precuneus (PCU)

6. **Middle Frontal Gyrus (MFG)**
   - Right middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Right dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)

7. **Precentral Gyrus**
   - Left precentral gyrus

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: pain modulation, pain cognition, and pain-related emotional response.
