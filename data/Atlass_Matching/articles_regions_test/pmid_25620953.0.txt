## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article

1. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Globus pallidus internus (GPi)

2. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
   - Primary sensorimotor cortex (SM1)

3. **Cerebellum**
   - Anterior cerebellar lobe
   - Posterior cerebellar lobe
   - Cerebellar vermis IV/V
   - Cerebellar lobules IV/V
   - Cerebellar lobule VIII
   - Cerebellar lobules crus I
   - Cerebellar lobules crus II
   - Cerebellar lobules VII
   - Cerebellar lobules VIIb
   - Cerebellar lobule VI
   - Cerebellar lobule VIIIb

4. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

5. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
   - Dorsal premotor cortex (dorsal PMC)

6. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Inferior parietal gyrus
   - Superior parietal gyrus
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Precuneus
   - Postcentral gyrus

7. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Temporal pole
   - Inferior temporal gyrus

8. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Middle occipital gyrus
   - Calcarine fissure
   - Lingual gyrus
   - Visual cortex

9. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate
   - Paracingulate
   - Middle cingulate

10. **Insular Cortex**

11. **Hippocampus**

12. **Olfactory Cortex**

13. **Rolandic Operculum**

14. **Fusiform Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including motor planning, sensory processing, visual perception, emotional processing, and spatial information processing.
