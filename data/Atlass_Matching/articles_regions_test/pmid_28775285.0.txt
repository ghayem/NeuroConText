## The brain regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)
    - Right Prefrontal Cortex
    - Right Lateral Prefrontal Cortex (rlPFC)
        - Right Middle Frontal Gyrus (rMFG)
        - Right Inferior Frontal Gyrus (rIFG)

2. **Striatum**

3. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)
        - Dorsal Anterior Cingulate Cortex (dACC)
    - Cingulate Fronto-Parietal Circuit Network

4. **Insula**

5. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**
    - Bilateral Orbitofrontal Cortex

6. **Frontoparietal Control Network**
    - Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: behavioral inhibition, interference control, cognitive control, inhibition impairment, attentional control, response inhibition, executive functions, and flexible behavioral change.
