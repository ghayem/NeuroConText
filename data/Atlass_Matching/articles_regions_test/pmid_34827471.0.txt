## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Dorsal Striatum**
    - Dorsolateral striatum
        - Putamen (in humans)
    - Dorsomedial striatum
        - Caudate (in humans)

2. **Insular Cortex**

3. **Cingulate Cortex**

4. **Putamen**
    - Left putamen
    - Right putamen

5. **Insula**
    - Left insula
    - Right insula

6. **Cingulate**
    - Right cingulate

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: instrumental learning, goal-directed learning, avoidance conditioning and learning, and Pavlovian-to-instrumental transfer (PIT).
