## The brain regions:

1. **Frontal lobe**
2. **Cingulate cortex**
3. **Anterior insular lobe**
4. **Hippocampus**
5. **Right superior temporal gyrus**
6. **Right inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)**
7. **Right paracentric lobule**
8. **Right cingulate gyrus**
9. **Parietal lobe**
10. **Supplementary motor area**
11. **Right precentral gyrus**
12. **Right middle frontal gyrus**
13. **Bilateral cingulate cortex**
14. **Bilateral precuneus**

    - These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including executive functions, emotion regulation, memory, and motor control.
