Based on the provided article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Polar medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
   - Medial frontal cortex (MFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)

2. **Premotor Cortex**
   - Premotor cortex (PMC)

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
   - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)

4. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
   - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
   - Temporal-parietal junction (TPJ)
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)
   - Angular gyrus (AG)

5. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Cuneus
   - Precuneus
   - Occipital gyrus

6. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
   - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)

7. **Motor Cortex**
   - Precentral gyrus (PreCG)
   - Postcentral gyrus (PostCG)
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)

8. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:

- **Internally directed attention/spontaneous cognition**: MPFC, MFC
- **Externally directed attention/cognitive control**: IFG, PMC, DLPFC
- **Feedback regulation of perceptual-motor processing and control**: STG, IPL, TPJ, PMC, PreCG, PostCG
- **Internally motivated cognition (self-expression)**: Polar MPFC
- **Executive control mediated by conscious self-monitoring**: DLPFC
- **Low-level imaginative processes**: MFC, IPL
- **Coordination of complex action underlying sequential planning**: MFC, ACC, SMA
- **Sensory predictive feedback**: IPL
- **Transformation of auditory signals into a form that constrains motor processing**: TPJ
- **Reward-based modulation by music**: TPJ
- **Visual observation and processing**: Cuneus, precuneus, occipital gyrus
