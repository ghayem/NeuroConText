Based on the provided article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Cerebral Cortex**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Parietal cortex
   - Visual cortex
   - Lateral temporal cortex

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Inferior frontal cortex
   - Medial frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Middle frontal gyrus

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Inferior temporal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus

4. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Superior parietal lobule

5. **Other Regions**
   - Precuneus
   - Posterior cingulate cortex/precuneus
   - Lateral temporal and parietal cortex
   - Medial prefrontal cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including but not limited to:
- **Executive function and cognitive control**: Medial frontal gyrus, middle frontal gyrus, inferior frontal gyrus
- **Language processing**: Middle and inferior frontal gyrus
- **Concentration and memory**: Posterior cingulate cortex/precuneus
- **Visual processing**: Visual cortex
- **Multisensory integration**: Lateral temporal and parietal cortex
