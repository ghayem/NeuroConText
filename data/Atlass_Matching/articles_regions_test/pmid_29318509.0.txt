Based on the provided article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)

2. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**
   - Posterior TPJ (pTPJ)
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)

3. **Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)**
   - Posterior STS (pSTS)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)

4. **Striatum**
   - Putamen

5. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)

6. **Insula**
   - Anterior insula

7. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

8. **Precuneus**

9. **Angular Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:

- Social cognition and mentalizing (mPFC, STS, TPJ)
- Self-regulation and cognitive control (vlPFC, dlPFC)
- Reward processing and anticipation (striatum)
- Conflict monitoring and error detection (dACC, anterior insula)
- Motor control and planning (SMA)
- Episodic memory retrieval and self-processing (precuneus)
- Language and semantic processing (angular gyrus, MTG)
