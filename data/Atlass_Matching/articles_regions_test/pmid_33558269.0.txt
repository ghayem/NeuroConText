Based on the neuroscientific article provided, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Frontoparietal Network**
   - Medial superior parietal lobule (SPL)
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)
   - Precentral sulcus/gyrus

2. **Visual Cortex**
   - Visual area V4
   - Visual area V1

3. **Attention Shift Network**
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
   - Temporal gyri
   - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)

4. **Other Regions**
   - Ventral areas
   - Superior parietal regions
   - Lateral intraparietal area (LIP) (in monkeys)
   - Superior colliculus (SC) (in monkeys)
   - Frontal eye field (FEF) (in monkeys)
   - Striate and extrastriate cortex (in monkeys)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:
- Attention allocation and shifts
- Saccade execution and planning
- Visual processing and perception
- Spatial processing and updating
- Reference frame transformations (retinotopic and spatiotopic)
