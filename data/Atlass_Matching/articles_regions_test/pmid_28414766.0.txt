## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Prefrontal cortex
        - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
        - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (VLPFC)
    - Orbito-frontal cortex (OFC)
    - Inferior frontal gyrus

2. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Superior temporal gyrus
    - Inferior temporal gyrus
    - Temporo-parietal junction
    - Angular gyrus (AG)

3. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Precuneus
    - Inferior parietal lobule
    - Superior parietal lobule

4. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Fusiform gyrus

5. **Insula**

6. **Limbic System**
    - Hippocampus

7. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Anterior cingulate
    - Posterior cingulate

8. **Other Regions**
    - Insular cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including:
- **Insight and self-awareness**: Prefrontal cortex, precuneus, insula
- **Language and social cognition**: Superior temporal gyrus
- **Face recognition**: Fusiform gyrus
- **Memory and self-processing**: Precuneus, hippocampus
- **Decision making and social behavior**: Orbito-frontal cortex
- **Self-reflection and metacognition**: Dorsomedial and ventromedial prefrontal cortex, anterior and posterior cingulate, anterior insula, temporo-parietal junction, inferior parietal lobule
