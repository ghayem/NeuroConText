### Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Inferior Temporal Cortex**
   - Involved in visual stimuli processing.

2. **Primary Somatosensory Cortex**
   - Involved in tactile stimuli processing.

3. **Primary Auditory Cortex**
   - Involved in auditory stimuli processing.

4. **Posterior Auditory Fields**
   - Involved in auditory stimuli processing.

5. **Posterior Association Cortices**
   - Involved in representing cross-modal associations.

6. **Prefrontal Association Cortices**
   - Involved in representing cross-modal associations.

7. **Medial Temporal Cortex**
   - Involved in mediating memory traces.

8. **Inferior Temporal Sulcus**
   - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

9. **Inferior Parietal Lobule**
   - Involved in lexical/semantic processing.
     - **Angular Gyrus (AG)**
       - Involved in semantic processing and supramodal integration.

10. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**
    - Involved in lexical/semantic processing and episodic memory.

11. **Inferior Frontal Gyri**
    - Involved in word processing, comprehension, and naming.
     - **Pars Triangularus**
     - **Pars Opercularis**

12. **Superior Temporal Sulcus**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

13. **Superior Temporal Gyrus**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.
     - **Middle Temporal Gyrus**
       - Involved in lexical access or comprehension.

14. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

15. **Supplementary Motor Area**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

16. **Ventral Thalamus**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

17. **Motor Cortex**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

18. **Intraparietal Sulcus**
    - Involved in spatial representation, visual search, and visual working memory.
     - **Ventral Intraparietal Sulcus (HIP1)**

19. **Rolandic Operculum**
    - Involved in non-linguistic auditory processing.

20. **Hippocampus**
    - Involved in episodic memory and semantic memory.

21. **Insula**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

22. **Primary Visual Cortex**
    - Involved in linguistic AV memory.

23. **Middle Longitudinal Fasciculus**
    - Connects AG with middle temporal gyrus and other linguistically related temporal areas.

24. **Posterior Segment of the Arcuate Fasciculus**
    - Connects AG with other linguistically related temporal areas.

25. **Third Branch of the Superior Longitudinal Fasciculus**
    - Connects AG and Broca's area.

### Cognitive Functions Involved:

- **Language**: Involves regions such as the inferior frontal gyri, angular gyrus, and middle temporal gyrus.
- **Memory**: Involves regions such as the medial temporal cortex, hippocampus, and posterior cingulate cortex.
- **Executive Functions**: Involves regions such as the prefrontal association cortices and anterior cingulate cortex.
- **Sensory Processing**: Involves regions such as the inferior temporal cortex, primary somatosensory cortex, and primary auditory cortex.
