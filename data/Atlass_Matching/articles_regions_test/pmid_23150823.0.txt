## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Mesial temporal lobe
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Fusiform gyrus
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Amygdala
   - Uncus

2. **Extratemporal Areas**
   - Basal ganglia
   - Insula
   - Thalamus
   - Cerebellum
   - Frontal lobe
   - Parietal lobe
   - Occipital lobe
   - Cingulate gyrus
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Lingual gyrus
   - Corpus callosum

These regions are involved in various functions, including memory, emotion, sensory processing, motor control, and cognitive functions.
