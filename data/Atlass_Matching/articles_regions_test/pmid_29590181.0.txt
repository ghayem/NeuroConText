## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Right Inferior Frontal Gyrus (rIFG)**
2. **Pre-Supplementary Motor Area (preSMA)**
3. **Subthalamic Nucleus**
4. **Ventrolateral Prefrontal Cortex (VLPFC)**
5. **Right Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**
6. **Left Orbitofrontal Cortex (lOFC)**
7. **Left Supplementary Motor Area (lSMA)**
8. **Right Middle Frontal Gyrus (rMFG)**
9. **Left Thalamus**
10. **Motor Cortex (M1)**
11. **Premotor Cortex**
12. **Putamen**
13. **Basal Ganglia**
14. **Striatum**

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as inhibitory control, motor response, and executive functions.
