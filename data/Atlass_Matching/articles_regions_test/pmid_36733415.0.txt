## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Amygdala**
    - Basolateral amygdala (BLA)
    - Lateral amygdala (LA)
    - Central amygdala (CEA)
    - Basomedial amygdala (BMA)
    - Medial amygdala (MEA)
    - Centromedial amygdala

2. **Hypothalamus**
    - Dorsomedial part of ventromedial hypothalamus (dmVMH)
    - Ventrolateral part of ventromedial hypothalamus (vlVMH)
    - Anterior hypothalamic nucleus (AHN)
    - Ventrolateral part of dorsal premammillary nucleus (vlPMD)
    - Dorsomedial part of dorsal premammillary nucleus (dmPMD)
    - Medial preoptic nucleus (MPN)
    - Ventral premammillary nucleus (PMV)

3. **Periaqueductal gray (PAG)**
    - Ventrolateral PAG (vlPAG)
    - Dorsolateral PAG (dlPAG)
    - Dorsomedial PAG (dmPAG)
    - Lateral PAG (lPAG)

4. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
    - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
    - Pre- and postcentral gyrus
    - Paracingulate gyrus

6. **Insula**
    - Anterior insula
    - Ventral insula

7. **Other Cortical Regions**
    - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
    - Frontal operculum
    - Occipital pole
    - Cerebellum
    - Thalamus

8. **Rostral Anterior Cingulate (rACC)**
    - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)

These regions are involved in threat processing, defensive responses, and various cognitive and emotional functions related to threat and fear conditioning.
