### Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Parietal-Frontal Circuit**
   - Parietal cortex
   - Frontal cortex

2. **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)**
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex
   - Medial orbitofrontal cortex (mOFC)

3. **Amygdala**

4. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Lateral PFC

5. **Striatum**

### These regions are involved in these cognitive functions:
- Intention formation
- Episodic future thinking (prospective thinking)
- Subjective valuation of choice options
- Reward processing
- Planning and execution of choices
- Value coding during dynamic persistence
- Multi-step reinforcement learning
- Economic saving behavior
- Temporal discounting
- Representation of goal states
- Imagining future states (episodic prospection)
