## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Cerebral Cortex**
   - Entire cerebral cortex

2. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Medial frontal areas
   - Bilateral paracentral areas
   - Right superior frontal gyrus
   - Right rostral middle frontal gyrus
   - Pars triangularis of the right inferior frontal gyrus
   - Bilateral superior frontal gyrus
   - Left superior frontal cortex
   - Anteromedial part of the left superior frontal cortex
   - Dorsolateral part of the left superior frontal cortex
   - Orbital part of the right inferior frontal gyrus
   - Orbital part of the right inferior cortex

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Medial parietal areas
   - Right superior parietal gyrus
   - Bilateral medial and lateral parietal regions
   - Left precuneus

4. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Right superior and middle temporal gyrus

5. **Insula**
   - Left insular

6. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Bilateral basal ganglia

7. **White Matter (WM)**
   - Periventricular WM
   - Deep WM of bilateral frontal and parietal lobes

8. **Subcortical Regions**
   - Subcortical regions

9. **Gray Matter (GM)**
   - GM volume loss in the frontal cortex

10. **Cortical Gray Matter–White Matter (GM–WM) Interface**
    - Cortical GM–WM interface

## Cognitive Functions Involved:
- Executive functions
- Memory
- Language
- Visuospatial functions
- Cognitive control
- Information integration
