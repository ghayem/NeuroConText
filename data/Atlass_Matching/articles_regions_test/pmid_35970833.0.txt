## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Thalamus**

2. **Putamen**

3. **Hippocampus**

4. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Right temporal cortex
    - Left superior temporal gyrus (STG.L)

5. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Left occipital cortex
    - Right calcarine fissure/surrounding cortex (CAL.R)

6. **Parietal Cortex**

7. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Right superior frontal gyrus, medial orbital (PFCventmed.R)

8. **Corpus Callosum (CC)**

9. **Internal Capsule**
    - Left anterior limb of the internal capsule

10. **External Capsule**
    - Left external capsule

11. **Anterior Thalamic Radiation (ATR)**

12. **Corticospinal Tract (CST)**

13. **Cingulum**

14. **Inferior Fronto-Occipital Fasciculus (IFOF)**
    - Right inferior fronto-occipital fasciculus (IFOF.R)

15. **Superior Longitudinal Fasciculus (SLF)**
    - Left superior longitudinal fasciculus temporal part (SLF-temp)

16. **Uncinate Fasciculus (UF)**

17. **Inferior Longitudinal Fasciculus (ILF)**
    - Right inferior longitudinal fasciculus (ILF.R)

18. **Cerebellum**
    - Undefined area in cerebellum (32, −60, −42)
    - Cerebellar dentate nucleus (CDN)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including language, memory, executive functions, and sensory processing.
