## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**

2. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**

3. **Hippocampus**

4. **Middle Frontal Sulcus (MFS)**

5. **Precentral Sulcus (PreCS)**

6. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

7. **Angular Gyrus (AG)**

8. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**

9. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**

10. **Insula**

11. **Middle Cingulate Cortex (MCC)**

12. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: language, memory, and executive functions.
