## The brain regions:

1. **Cerebral hemispheres**
   - Bilateral

2. **Spinal cord**

3. **Brainstem**

4. **Cerebellum**

5. **Dorsal medulla**

6. **Thalamus**
   - Posterior part
   - Pulvinar
   - Dorsal thalamus

7. **Parietal lobe**
   - Posterior parietal lobe
   - Posterior parietal cortex
   - Brodmann area 7

8. **Hippocampus**

9. **Dorsal root ganglia**

10. **Spinocerebellar tract**

11. **Pyramidal tract**

12. **Dorsal columns**

These regions are involved in various neurological and cognitive functions, including sensory processing, motor control, and higher-order cognitive functions.
