## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Bilateral frontal lobe (BA11, BA46)

2. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Right superior temporal gyrus (BA 41)
   - Right temporal lobe (BA37)
   - Right middle and inferior temporal gyrus

3. **Caudate Nucleus**
   - Left caudate nucleus

4. **Subcortical Structures**
   - Cerebral volumes
   - Deep nuclear gray matter

5. **Other Regions**
   - White matter fasciculi
   - Thalamus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including motor control, cognition, attention, memory, language, and neuropsychiatric function.
