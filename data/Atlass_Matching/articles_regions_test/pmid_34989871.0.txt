## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Caudal Parahippocampal Cortex (PHC)**
   - Part of the ventral temporal cortex and its transition to the occipital cortex
   - Parahippocampal place area (PPA)

2. **Ventral Temporal Cortex**
   - Collateral sulcus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Fusiform gyrus (FG)
     - Fusiform face areas (FFA-1/2)
     - Region of the collateral sulcus
     - Parahippocampal gyrus

3. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Extrastriate area V2/BA18

4. **Ventral and Lateral Occipito-Temporal Cortex**
   - Fusiform face areas (FFA-1/2)
   - Category-selective regions

5. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Hippocampus
   - Medial temporal lobe

6. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Retrosplenial cortex
   - Anterior calcarine fissure
   - Isthmus of the retrosplenial cortex
   - Lingual gyrus

7. **Cingulate Cortex**

8. **Somatosensory Associated Regions**

9. **Visual Information Processing Regions**

10. **Sulci and Fissures**
    - Collateral sulcus
    - Rhinal sulcus
    - Hippocampal fissure
    - Anterior calcarine fissure

11. **Cytoarchitectonic Areas**
    - Brodmann’s area 28
    - Areas TH and PH according to von Economo and Koskinas
    - Fusiform areas (FG1-4)
    - New areas Ph1, Ph2, Ph3, and CoS1

12. **Other Specific Regions**
    - Uncus

13. **Functional Regions of Interest (ROI)**
    - Parahippocampal place area (PPA)
    - Fusiform face areas (FFA-1/2)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including visual–spatial processing, memory (episodic, associative), emotional stimuli processing, auditory and odor stimuli processing, and visuo-motor control.
