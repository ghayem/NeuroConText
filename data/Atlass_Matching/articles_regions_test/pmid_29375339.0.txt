## Cognitive Functions and Associations

- **Emotion Regulation**: Amygdala, insula, prefrontal cortex, temporal cortex, thalamus, putamen
- **Cognitive Deficits**: Inferior frontal gyrus, ventromedial prefrontal cortex, hippocampus
- **Reward Processing**: Striatum
- **Memory**: Hippocampus, medial temporal lobe
- **Salience Network**: Insula
- **Default Mode Network (DMN)**: Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
- **Sensory Information Transmission**: Thalamus
- **Motor Regulation and Learning**: Putamen
- **Attention and Cognitive Control**: Prefrontal cortex
- **Fear and Anxiety Response**: Amygdala, fear network
- **Somatic Anxiety Symptoms**: Putamen
- **Mind-Wandering and Passive Rest**: Default mode network (DMN)
- **Emotional, Motivational, Associative, and Cognitive Functions**: Basal ganglia
- **Executive Functions**: Prefrontal cortex, inferior frontal gyrus
- **Language and Memory**: Superior temporal gyrus (STG), temporal pole
- **Emotional Arousal and Memory Enhancement**: Medial temporal lobe, amygdala
