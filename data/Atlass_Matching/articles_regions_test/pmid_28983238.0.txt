### Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Hemispheres**
   - Left hemisphere
   - Right hemisphere

2. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Left inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
     - Broca's area (BA 44/45)
     - Frontal operculum
   - Bilateral ventral premotor cortices (PMv)
   - Anterior banks of the precentral gyri
   - Left dorsal premotor cortex (PMd)
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
   - Middle part of the cingulate cortex (mCC)
   - Bilateral anterior insulae (aI)

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Left superior temporal sulcus (STS)
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)
   - Angular gyrus (ANG)
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)

4. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Bilateral inferior temporal gyri (ITG)
   - Fusiform gyri (FG)
   - Superior temporal sulcus (STS)
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Caudal middle temporal gyrus (cMTG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)

5. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Left and right inferior parts of the middle occipital gyri (MOG)
   - Occipital poles (OP)
   - Cuneus
   - Parieto-occipital sulcus

6. **Subcortical Structures**
   - Left and right putamen
   - Caudate nucleus
   - Thalamus (Th)
   - Caudal middle frontal gyrus (cMFG)
   - Superior frontal sulcus (SFS)
   - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Insular cortex
   - Heschl's gyri

7. **Cerebellum**
   - Anterior lobes of the cerebellum
   - Left cerebellar seed

8. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Angular gyrus (ANG)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (pC)
   - Superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)

9. **Ventral Attention Network**
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)
   - Anterior insula (aI)
   - Anterior middle frontal gyrus (aMFG)

10. **Wernicke's Area (WA)**
    - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
    - Superior temporal sulcus (STS)

11. **Other Structures**
    - Precuneus (pC)
    - Postcentral sulcus
    - Anterior middle frontal gyrus (aMFG)
    - Rostral division of middle frontal gyrus (MFG)

### Cognitive Functions

- **Language**
  - Language production
  - Language lateralization
  - Verbal fluency
  - Word generation
  - Word reading
  - Gesture planning
  - Visuospatial processing
  - Semantic processes

- **Memory**
  - Autobiographical memory recall

- **Executive Functions**
  - Motor planning and execution
  - Praxis planning
  - Action imitation

- **Attention**
  - Visuospatial attention

- **Mentalization**
  - Internal mentalization related to plans for the future
