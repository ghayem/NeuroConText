## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
    - Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex (dmPFC)
    - Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)

3. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
    - Subgenual Anterior Cingulate Cortex (sgACC)
    - Rostral Anterior Cingulate Cortex (rACC)

4. **Superior Frontal Gyrus (SFG)**

5. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**

6. **Hypothalamus**

7. **Hippocampus**

8. **Insula**

9. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**

10. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: emotion regulation, social cognition, decision-making, and memory.
