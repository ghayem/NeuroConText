## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Paraventricular Nucleus**

3. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (VmPFC)

4. **Liver**
    - Kupffer cells

5. **Hypothalamic-Pituitary-Adrenal (HPA) Axis**
    - Hypothalamus
    - Pituitary Gland
    - Adrenal Glands

6. **Central Nervous System (CNS)**
    - Microglia

7. **Ventral Anterior Cingulate**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 24

8. **Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 9

9. **Lateral Orbitofrontal Cortex**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 47

10. **Angular Gyrus**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 39

11. **Frontal Eyefields**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 8

12. **Ventral Posterior Cingulate Cortex**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 23

13. **Dorsal Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
    - Brodmann Area (BA) 32

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including emotional processing, stress response, decision-making, and executive functions.
