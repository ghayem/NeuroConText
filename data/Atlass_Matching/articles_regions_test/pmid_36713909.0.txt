1. **White Matter (WM)**
   - Superior longitudinal fasciculus (SLF)
   - Corpus callosum
   - Anterior limb of the internal capsule
   - Cingulum
   - Anterior thalamic radiation
   - Posterior thalamic radiation (PTR)
   - Splenium of corpus callosum
   - Right anterior corona radiata
   - Left PTR
   - Left SLF
   - Visual network
   - Sensorimotor network

2. **Thalamus**
   - This region is involved in processing and integrating sensory information.

3. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Posterior visual cortex
   - Optic radiation from the lateral geniculate body
   - This region is involved in visual perception and processing.

4. **Pre/postcentral gyrus**
   - This region is involved in integrating and processing bodily information.

5. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Central paracentral lobule
   - Precuneus

6. **Parietal Lobe**
   - This region is involved in somatic discomfort, working memory performance, attention, executive function, and emotion.

7. **Cingulate or Paracingulate Gyrus**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex
   - This region is involved in cognitive and emotional functioning.

8. **Basal Ganglia**
   - This region is involved in oscillations stronger in slow-4 band.

9. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**
   - This region is involved in the segmentation process but not directly related to cognitive functions.
