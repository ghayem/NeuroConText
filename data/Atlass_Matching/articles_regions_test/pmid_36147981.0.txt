## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Striatum**
    - Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)
    - Ventral striatum

2. **Frontal cortex**
    - Frontal cortex
    - Frontal lobe

3. **Hippocampus**

4. **Amygdala**

5. **Parietal cortex**

6. **Temporal cortex**
    - Temporal lobe
    - Inferior temporal gyrus
    - Middle temporal gyrus

7. **Limbic system**
    - Cingulate cortex
    - Insula
    - Parahippocampal gyrus
    - Ventral tegmental area

8. **Superior frontal gyrus**

9. **Anterior cingulate gyrus**

10. **Inferior frontal gyrus**

11. **Fusiform gyrus**

12. **Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)**

13. **Cerebrospinal fluid**

14. **White matter**

15. **Gray matter**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including language, memory, executive functions, emotional processing, and motor control.
