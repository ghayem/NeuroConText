1. **Default Mode Network**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
   - Hippocampal formation

2. **Dorsal Attention Network**
   - Superior parietal lobule
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Intraparietal sulcus
   - Frontal eye-fields

3. **Executive Control Network**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Posterior parietal cortex

4. **Frontoparietal Network**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (Pars opercularis and pars triangularis)
   - Angular gyrus
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)

5. **Limbic Network**
   - Amygdala
   - Hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Cingulate gyrus

6. **Salience Network**
   - Anterior insula
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (VLPFC)
   - Sublateral areas

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: attention, emotion processing, executive functions, and social cognition.
