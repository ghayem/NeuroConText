Based on the neuroscientific article provided, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Caudate
   - Putamen
   - Lenticular nucleus

2. **Limbic System**
   - Amygdala
   - Anterior cingulate cortex
   - Paracingulate gyri (BA 24)
   - Hippocampus (as part of medial temporal structures)

3. **Cerebellum**
   - Middle cerebellar peduncles
   - Cerebellar lobules (VI, VII, VIII)

4. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Precentral gyrus (BA 6)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (BA 45)
   - Superior frontal gyrus (BA 10)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (BA 11)

5. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Superior temporal gyrus (BA 48)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (BA 21)
   - Medial temporal structures
   - Parahippocampal gyrus

6. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Anterior gyrus
   - Posterior gyrus

7. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Optic radiations

8. **Other Regions**
   - Insula
   - Thalamus
   - Extranuclear
   - Inferior network
   - Inferior longitudinal fasciculus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotion regulation, memory, executive functions, language processing, and sensory processing.
