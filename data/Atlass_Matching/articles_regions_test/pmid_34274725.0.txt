### Cognitive Functions and Associated Brain Regions:
- **Speech Motor Function**: Bilateral middle and lower portions of the precentral gyrus, insular cortex, SMA, parietal opercular cortex, cerebellum, thalamus.
- **Laryngeal/Phonation Area**: Middle portion of the precentral gyrus.
- **Articulatory Movements**: Rolandic operculum, lower portion of the precentral and postcentral gyrus.
- **Speech Initiation**: SMA.
- **Somatosensory Feedback**: Parietal operculum.
- **Motor Regulation of Speech**: SMA, cerebellum, thalamus.
- **Feedforward Control System**: Trans-cerebellar pathway, thalamus, SMA.
