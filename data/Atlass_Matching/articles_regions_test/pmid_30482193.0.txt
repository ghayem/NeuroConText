# Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Precuneus
    - Posterior cingulate cortex
    - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex

2. **Other Brain Regions**
    - Inferior temporal gyrus
    - Lentiform nucleus
    - Medial prefrontal cortex
    - Middle occipital gyrus
    - Middle temporal gyrus
    - Parahippocampus
    - Postcentral gyrus
    - Ventral striatum
    - Medial orbitofrontal cortex
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
    - Inferior parietal cortex
    - Frontal pole
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Posterior cingulate cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including social emotions, affective processing, self-reference, mentalizing, and reward processing.
