These regions are involved in various cognitive functions such as:
- **Executive Control**: Ventromedial prefrontal cortex, dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
- **Attentional Processes**: ACC, thalamus, hypothalamus, amygdala
- **Emotional Processes**: Amygdala, ACC, AI
- **Sensorimotor Processes**: PreCG, PosCG, SPL, IPL
- **Visuospatial Processes**: IPL, TPJ, STG
- **Motor Planning**: SMA, cingulate motor area
- **Language**: IFG (Broca's area), superior temporal gyrus
- **Memory**: Hippocampus, temporal cortex
- **Visual Processing**: Occipital gyrus, fusiform gyrus
- **Social Cognition**: TPJ, fusiform gyrus
- **Empathy**: AI, ACC, IFG, IPL, SPL, STG
