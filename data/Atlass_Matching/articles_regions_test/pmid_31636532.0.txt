## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Primary and Secondary Auditory Cortex**

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Right fronto-parietal area
   - Left frontal cortex
   - Right frontal cortex
   - Inferior frontal cortex
   - Bilateral prefrontal cortex
   - Left ventral medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Right medial dorsal prefrontal cortex
   - Prefrontal inter-hemisphere
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Left prefrontal cortex
   - Right prefrontal cortex

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Left temporal cortex
   - Right temporal cortex
   - Anterior superior temporal gyrus (aSTG)
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Anterior part of the right temporal cortex

4. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal lobe
   - Right inferior parietal cortex (IPC)
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Posterior cingulate gyrus
   - Medial part of the parietal lobe
   - Right supramarginal gyrus

5. **Premotor Cortices**

6. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Posterior cingulate gyrus

7. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

8. **Scalp**

9. **Skull**

10. **Brain (as a general term)**

11. **Cortical Regions**
    - Regions of the right hemisphere
    - Regions of the left hemisphere

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including auditory perception, sound category discrimination, motor planning, action representation, semantic processing, emotional processing, and arousal state.
