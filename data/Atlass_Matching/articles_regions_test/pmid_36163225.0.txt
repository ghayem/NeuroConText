## Brain Regions and Sub-regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Motor Cortex**
    - Premotor cortex
    - Primary motor cortex
    - Dorsal motor cortex

2. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
    - Brodmann area 9 (BA 9)
    - Brodmann area 46 (BA 46)

3. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Inferior frontal cortex (Broca’s area)
    - Prefrontal cortex
    - Frontal executive
    - Frontal-temporal-occipital lobes

4. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal cortex (Wernicke’s area)
    - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)
    - Inferior temporal cortex
    - Temporo-occipital cortex

5. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Inferior parietal cortex
    - Intraparietal sulcus
    - Angular gyrus
    - Superior parietal cortex

6. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Inferior occipital cortex

7. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**
    - Dorsal SMA

8. **Central Executive**

9. **Hippocampus**

10. **Cerebellum**

11. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Basal ganglia

12. **Connector Hubs**
    - Multimodal areas with high degree of connectivity

13. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
    - Frontocentral sensorimotor cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including language processing, motor control, working memory, and semantic processing.
