## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Cortical Regions**
    - Anterior temporal poles
    - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
    - Precuneus (PCU)
    - Lateral occipital gyrus (LOC)

2. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Deep gray matter
        - Caudate
        - Putamen
        - Thalamus
    - Basal ganglia

3. **White Matter**
    - White matter hyperintensities (WMHs)
    - Normal-appearing white matter (NAWM)

4. **Cerebral Small Vessel Disease (cSVD) Imaging Characteristics**
    - White matter hyperintensities (WMHs)
    - Cerebral microbleeds (CMBs)
    - Enlarged perivascular spaces (ePVS)
    - Lacunes

5. **Other Regions**
    - Entire brain
    - Corticosubcortical regions

- These regions are involved in various cognitive functions: memory, executive function, attention, working memory, visuospatial abilities, and language performance.
