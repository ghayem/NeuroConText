## The brain regions:

1. **Hippocampus**

2. **Olfactory bulb**

3. **Precuneus (PCUN)**
   - Left precuneus
   - Right precuneus

4. **Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)**
   - Bilateral medial orbital superior frontal gyrus
   - Bilateral rectus
   - Bilateral olfactory

5. **Cuneus**
   - Bilateral cuneus

6. **Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)**

7. **Default mode network (DMN)**
   - Precuneus (PCUN)

8. **Limbic system**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including memory, emotion processing, reward monitoring, self-introspection, and the appraisal and expression of negative emotion.
