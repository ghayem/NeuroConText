## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Locus Coeruleus (LC)**
   - Dorsal pons

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

3. **Neocortex**

4. **Basal Forebrain**

5. **Limbic System**

6. **Thalamus**

7. **Hypothalamus**

8. **Cerebellum**
   - Superior cerebellar peduncle (SCP)

9. **Brainstem Nuclei**

10. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**

11. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

12. **Frontotemporal Neocortical Areas**

    - Superior frontal gyrus
    - Middle frontal gyrus
    - Inferior frontal gyrus
    - Lateral occipital cortex

13. **Amygdala**

14. **Nucleus Accumbens**

15. **Putamen**

16. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**

17. **Cingulate Cortex**

18. **Fourth Ventricle**

19. **Central Tegmental Tract**

20. **Medial Longitudinal Fasciculus**

21. **Central Gray of the Pons**

These regions are involved in diverse brain functions such as attention, sensory perception, synaptic plasticity, memory, cognitive flexibility, executive functioning, motor performance, and emotion regulation.
