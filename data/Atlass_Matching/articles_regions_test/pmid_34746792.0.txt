1. **Superior temporal region**

   - Right posterior superior temporal sulcus (pSTS)

   - Right posterior superior temporal gyrus

4. **Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)**

5. **Putamen**

6. **Caudate**

7. **Precentral gyrus**

8. **Supramarginal gyrus**

9. **Left superior medial frontal area**

10. **Middle cingulate gyrus**

11. **Striatum**

12. **Primary auditory cortex**

13. **Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)**

14. **Bilateral superior temporal regions**

15. **Superior temporal gyrus (STG)**

16. **Frontal region**

17. **Middle temporal gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotional processing, temporal pattern analysis, auditory processing, and the experience of chills in response to music.
