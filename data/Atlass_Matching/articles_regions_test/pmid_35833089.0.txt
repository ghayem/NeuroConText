## The brain regions:

1. **Brainstem**
    - Locus coeruleus

2. **Thalamus**

3. **Cerebellum**

4. **Precuneus**

5. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Calcarine gyrus
    - Lingual gyrus

6. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Inferior temporal lobe
    - Middle temporal lobe

7. **Brodmann Area 17**

8. **Visual Cortex**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: consciousness regulation, attention maintenance, and sensory information processing.
