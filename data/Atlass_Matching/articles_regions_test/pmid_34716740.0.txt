## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Visual Cortex**
    - Contralateral (left) middle occipital gyrus
    - Bilateral medial visual cortex
    - Bilateral V1
    - Contralateral lingual gyrus

2. **Posterior Parietal Cortex**
    - Bilateral posterior parietal cortex (V5, SPL)
    - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
    - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
    - Contralateral supramarginal gyrus
    - Ipsilateral (right) superior parietal lobule

3. **Premotor and Primary Motor Cortices**
    - Bilateral primary motor cortex (M1)
    - Bilateral premotor cortex
    - Contralateral middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
    - Contralateral inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
    - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
    - Contralateral ventral premotor cortex (PMv)

4. **Cerebellum**
    - Posterior cerebellum
    - Cerebellar Crus I
    - Cerebellar lobules V/VI
    - Medial cerebellum lobule V

5. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Putamen
    - Caudate

6. **Other Cortical Regions**
    - Bilateral middle cingulate cortex
    - Bilateral anterior cingulate cortex
    - Bilateral orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
    - Contralateral insula
    - Ipsilateral middle and frontal gyri
    - Contralateral frontal eye field

7. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Thalamus

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including visual processing, sensorimotor integration, motor planning, and execution, as well as higher-order cognitive processes such as attention, working memory, and executive functions.
