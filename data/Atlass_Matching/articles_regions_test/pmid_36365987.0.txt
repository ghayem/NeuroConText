## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**

2. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**
   - Brodmann Area 9 (BA 9)

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Brodmann Area 7 (BA 7)
   - Brodmann Area 40 (BA 40)

4. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Brodmann Area 44 (BA 44)

5. **Premotor Cortex**
   - Brodmann Area 6 (BA 6)

6. **Frontal Lobe**

7. **Motor Cortex**

8. **Visual Cortex**

9. **Cerebellum**

10. **Frontal Pole**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: executive function, planning, decision-making, goal-directed behavior, emotional expression, and motor and visual processing.
