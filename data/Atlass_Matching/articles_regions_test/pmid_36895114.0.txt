## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Ventral Premotor Cortex (PMv)**
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus

2. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

3. **Amygdala**
   - Bilateral amygdala

4. **Cerebellum**
   - Bilateral cerebellum

5. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Bilateral basal ganglia

6. **Brainstem**
   - Facial nerve nucleus (facial motor nucleus)

7. **Posterior Neocortical Regions**
   - Bilateral middle temporal gyrus

8. **Motor and Premotor Regions**
   - Left motor and premotor regions

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: facial expression processing, motor synchronization, social bonding, emotion recognition, and facial mimicry.
