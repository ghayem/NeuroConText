2. **Medial Frontal Cortex (mFC)**

3. **Habenula**
    - Right habenula

4. **Nucleus Accumbens**

5. **Insula**
    - Bilateral insula

6. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Bilateral occipital cortex

7. **Ventral Striatum**
    - Bilateral ventral striatum

8. **Inferior Parietal Lobe**
    - Left inferior parietal lobe

9. **Cerebellum**
    - Right cerebellum

10. **Lingual Gyrus**
    - Left lingual gyrus

11. **Lateral Occipital Cortex**
    - Left lateral occipital cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including error monitoring, reward processing, cognitive control, and emotional regulation.
