## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Insula**
    - Left insula (Insula_L)

2. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Superior parietal lobe (Parietal_sup_L)
    - Inferior parietal lobe (Parietal_Inf_L)

3. **Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)

4. **Posterior Parietal Cortex (PPC)**
    - Posterior parietal cortex (PPC)

5. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Default mode network (DMN)

6. **Hippocampus**
    - Hippocampus

7. **Ventral Parietal Lobe**
    - Ventral parietal lobe

8. **Lateral and Medial Parietal Frontal Cortex**
    - Lateral and medial parietal frontal cortex

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: memory, visceral sensations, affection, addiction, pain regulation, language, cardiac activity, perception, calculation, attention, episodic recall, spatial cognition, cognitive control processes, and executive functions.
