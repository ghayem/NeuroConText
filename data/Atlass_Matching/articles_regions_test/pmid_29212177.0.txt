## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Left insula**

2. **Hippocampal and parahippocampal cortex (HIPP/paraHIPP)**

3. **Putamen**

4. **Right anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)**

5. **Left orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)**

6. **Bilateral insula**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: pain processing, emotion, homeostasis, visceral sensation, motor function, and pain control.
