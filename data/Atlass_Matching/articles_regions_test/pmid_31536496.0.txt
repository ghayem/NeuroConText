Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Inferior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Left frontal cortex

2. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Inferior temporal gyrus
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus
   - Inferior temporal gyrus

3. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal lobule
   - Lateral parietal cortex
   - Precuneus

4. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Middle cingulate cortex

5. **Motor Cortex**
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA/preSMA)

6. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Visual cortex

7. **Subcortical Regions**
   - Thalamus

8. **Other Regions**
   - Auditory cortex
   - Planum temporale
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Medial orbitofrontal areas
   - Anterior and middle cingulate cortex
   - Dorsolateral and inferior frontal cortex
   - Retrosplenial cortex
   - Somato-sensory association cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including but not limited to:
- Language processing
- Memory
- Executive functions
- Attention and vigilance
- Perception
- Cognitive control functions
- Hemispheric asymmetry
- Higher cognitive functions
- Task processing and resting states
