Based on the article, the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned are:

1. **Frontolateral Regions**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Rostrolateral prefrontal cortex (rlPFC)

2. **Frontomedial Regions**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex/presupplementary motor area (dACC/pre-SMA)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (sgACC)

3. **Parietal Regions**
   - Parietal cortex
   - Precuneus (PCUN)
   - Posterior parietal cortex
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG)

4. **Temporal Regions**
   - Ventral striatum

5. **Other Regions**
   - Posterior medial prefrontal cortex (PFC)
   - Paracingulate cortex
   - Insula (bilateral)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus/insula (IFG/insula)
   - Middle frontal gyri
   - Superior frontal gyri (SFG)
   - Cingulo-opercular network
   - Frontoparietal network
   - Anterior prefrontal cortex
   - Middle cingulate gyrus
   - Hippocampus
   - Cerebellum
   - Ventricles
   - Precentral and postcentral gyri
   - Occipital regions

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: metacognition, confidence estimation, decision making, memory, perception, and cognitive control.
