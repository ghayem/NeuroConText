## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Periaqueductal Gray (PAG)**

2. **Brainstem**

3. **Cerebellum**

4. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (sgACC)
   - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex (rACC)
   - Anterior midcingulate cortex (aMCC)

5. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)

6. **Premotor Cortex (PMC)**

7. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

8. **Hippocampus**

9. **Striatum**

10. **Precuneus**

11. **Angular Gyrus (AG)**

12. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

13. **Cuneus**

14. **Retrosplenial Cortex**

15. **Lingual Gyrus**

16. **Supramarginal Gyrus**

17. **Superior Parietal Lobule**

18. **Temporal Pole**

19. **Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**

20. **Rostroventral Medulla (RVM)**

21. **Lateral Occipital Cortex (LOC)**

22. **Medulla**

23. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**

24. **Salience Network (SN)**

25. **White Matter (WM)**

26. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as pain perception, emotional reaction, executive functions, memory, and motor functions.
