Based on the neuroscientific article provided, the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned are:

1. **Cortical Midline Structures (CMSs)**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Precuneus
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)

2. **Dorsal Frontoparietal Network**
   - Frontal eye field (FEF)
   - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)

3. **Ventral Frontoparietal Network**
   - Ventral frontal gyrus (VFG)
   - Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Superior frontal gyrus/middle frontal gyrus (SFG/MFG)

4. **Limbic Lobe**
   - Anterior cingulate (part of the limbic lobe)

5. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Superior occipital gyrus (SOG)
   - Middle occipital gyrus (MOG)

6. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Precuneus/SPL
   - Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)
   - Paracentral lobule

7. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Frontal eye field (FEF)
   - Ventral frontal gyrus (VFG)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Superior frontal gyrus/middle frontal gyrus (SFG/MFG)

8. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Middle occipital gyrus (MOG)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including self-referential processing, attentional orienting, and the integration of self-referential information with attentional processes.
