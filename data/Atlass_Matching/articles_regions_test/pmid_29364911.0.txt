## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Inferior frontal cortex
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Anterior prefrontal cortex
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Frontal eye field

2. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Superior parietal lobule
   - Inferior parietal lobule
   - Parietal lobule
   - Intraparietal sulcus
   - Angular gyrus
   - Precuneus

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Inferior temporal cortex
   - Lateral occipital cortex

4. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Occipital pole (V1)
   - Lateral occipital cortex

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex

6. **Insula**
   - Anterior insula/frontal operculum

7. **Salience Network (SAL)**
   - Anterior prefrontal cortex
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex
   - Anterior insula/frontal operculum

8. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Left and right angular gyrus
   - Left and right precuneus
   - Left and right superior frontal gyrus

9. **Frontoparietal Network (FPN)**
   - Middle frontal gyrus/dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Parietal lobule (inferior and superior)

10. **Dorsal Attention Network (DAN)**
    - Intraparietal sulcus
    - Frontal eye field

11. **Visual Network (VIS)**
    - Occipital pole (V1)

12. **Lateral Ventricle**

13. **White Matter**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as attention, executive control, working memory, inhibition, and cognitive flexibility.
