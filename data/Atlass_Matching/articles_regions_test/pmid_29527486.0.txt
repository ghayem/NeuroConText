## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Subgenual cortex
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Dorsolateral PFC
   - Dorsomedial PFC
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Frontal medial cortex
   - Subgenual cortices
   - Frontal poles

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Dorsal ACC
   - Anterior and posterior cingulate gyrus

3. **Hippocampus**
   - Right hippocampal gray matter
   - Hippocampal formation

4. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Parahippocampal gyrus
   - Fusiform gyrus
   - Temporoparietal regions

5. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Angular gyrus
   - Precuneus
   - Temporoparietal regions

6. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Right hemisphere posterior cortical regions

7. **Subcortical Structures**
   - Amygdala
   - Ventral striatum
   - Cerebellum

8. **White Matter (WM)**

9. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

10. **Other Cortical Regions**
    - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

- These regions are involved in cognitive and executive functions, emotional processing, and other related neurophysiological impairments.
