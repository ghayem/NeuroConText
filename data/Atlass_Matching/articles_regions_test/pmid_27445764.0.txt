## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Hippocampus**

2. **Caudate**

3. **Frontal lobe**
    - Lateral prefrontal cortex
    - Medial pericalcarine

4. **Parietal lobe**
    - Precuneus

5. **Temporal lobe**

6. **Occipital lobe**
    - Cuneus
    - Lingual gyrus

7. **Rostral middle frontal cortex**

8. **Dorsal lateral prefrontal cortex**

## These regions are involved in these cognitive functions:
- Learning
- Memory
- Executive functions (e.g., attention, working memory)
- Visual processing
- Selective visual attention
- Higher-order visual abilities
