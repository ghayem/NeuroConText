## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Anterior insular cortex
   - Right anterior insula
   - Dorsal and anterior regions (dorsal DMN)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (MPFC)
   - Right ventrolateral prefrontal cortex [RPFC(R)]
   - Left ventrolateral prefrontal cortex [RPFC(L)]
   - Frontal eye field [FEF(L)]
   - Frontal eye field [FEF(R)]

2. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Paracentral lobule [LP(L)]
   - Paracentral lobule [LP(R)]
   - Intraparietal sulcus [IPS(L)]
   - Intraparietal sulcus [IPS(R)]
   - Posterior parietal cortex [PPC(L)]
   - Posterior parietal cortex [PPC(R)]

3. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Posterior and medial temporal regions (ventral DMN)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)

4. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Visual cortex
   - Medial occipital cortex
   - Lateral occipital cortex (left and right)

5. **Thalamus**

6. **Salience Network (SN)**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Right anterior insula
   - Left anterior insula
   - Left supramarginal gyrus [SMG(L)]
   - Right supramarginal gyrus [SMG(R)]

7. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Dorsal DMN
   - Ventral DMN

8. **Sensorimotor Network (SMN)**
   - Left and right lateral sensorimotor regions
   - Superior sensorimotor regions

9. **Visual Network (VN)**
   - Medial visual regions
   - Occipital visual regions
   - Left and right lateral visual regions

10. **Dorsal Attention Network (DAN)**
    - Left frontal eye field [FEF(L)]
    - Right frontal eye field [FEF(R)]
    - Left intraparietal sulcus [IPS(L)]
    - Right intraparietal sulcus [IPS(R)]

11. **Ventral Attention Network (VAN)**

12. **Frontal-Parietal Control Network (FPN)**
    - Left lateral prefrontal cortex [LPFC(L)]
    - Right lateral prefrontal cortex [LPFC(R)]
    - Left posterior parietal cortex [PPC(L)]
    - Right posterior parietal cortex [PPC(R)]

13. **Subcortical Network**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including sensory perception, visual information processing, emotional regulation, working memory, vigilant attention, executive control functions, introspective self-oriented processes, decision-making, and cognitive information processing.
