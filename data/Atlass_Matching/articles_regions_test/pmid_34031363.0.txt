## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Insula**

3. **Occipital**

4. **Frontal**

5. **Temporal**

6. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)
   - Posterior Cingulate

7. **Nucleus Accumbens**

8. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Bilateral Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex

9. **Middle Frontal Gyrus (MFG)**

10. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**

11. **Hippocampus**
   - Right Hippocampus/Amygdala

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including emotional processing, theory of mind, cognitive functions, impulsivity control, and emotional regulation.
