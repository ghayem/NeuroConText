1. **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)**

3. **Anterior Insula (AI)**

4. **Posterior Parietal Cortex (PPC)**

6. **Amygdala**

7. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

   - Pregenual ACC (pgACC)

8. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

9. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
   - Right IFG

10. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

11. **Pre-supplementary Motor Area**

12. **Ventral Tegmental Area (VTA)**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: decision-making, subjective valuation, reward processing, emotional processing, cognitive control, and executive functions.
