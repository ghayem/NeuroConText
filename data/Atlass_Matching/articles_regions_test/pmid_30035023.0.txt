### Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Lateral Occipito-Temporal Cortex (LOTC)**
   - Extrastriate body area (EBA)

2. **Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)**

3. **Inferior Parietal Lobule (IPL)**

4. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**

5. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
   - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Anterior cingulate gyrus (ACC)

6. **Inferior Occipital Gyrus (IOG)**
   - Left lingual gyrus
   - Right fusiform gyrus

7. **Middle Occipital Gyrus (MOG)**

8. **Cortical Midline Structure (CMS)**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

9. **Cerebellum**

10. **Lateral Frontoparietal Cortices**

11. **Upper Limb-Sensitive (ULS) Region**
    - Located within the LOTC

12. **Posterior Superior Temporal Cortex**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including self-body recognition, visual processing, social interaction, imitation, and self-representation.
