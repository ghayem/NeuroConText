## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
    - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
    - Precuneus
    - Anteromedial prefrontal cortex (aMPFC)
    - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
    - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
    - Posterior parietal cortex (PPC)
    - Premotor areas

2. **Salience Network (SN)**
    - Anterior insula
        - Right anterior insula
    - Posterior insula
        - Right posterior insula
    - Central opercular cortex
    - Midbrain

3. **Frontoparietal Control Network (FPCN)**
    - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
    - Posterior parietal cortex (PPC)

4. **Other Cortical Regions**
    - Middle occipital gyrus
    - Cuneus
    - Precentral gyrus/motor cortex
    - Lateral occipital cortex
    - Occipital pole
    - Occipital cortex
    - Prefrontal cortex (PFC)
        - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (dMPFC)

5. **Subcortical Regions**
    - Amygdala
    - Hippocampus

- These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including self-referentiality, emotional regulation, attentional control, and the integration of sensory, emotional, and cognitive information.
