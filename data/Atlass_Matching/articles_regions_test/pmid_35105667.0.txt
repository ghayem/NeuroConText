## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Frontal Region**
   - Right inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Left IFG
   - Right superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
   - Left SFG
   - Right middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Left MFG
   - Gyrus rectus
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)

2. **Temporal Region**
   - Right superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Left STG
   - Right middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
   - Left MTG
   - Right inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)
   - Left Heschl gyrus
   - Right Heschl gyrus
   - Right inferior occipital gyrus
   - Left inferior occipital gyrus
   - Right superior occipital gyrus
   - Right thalamus
   - Left thalamus

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Right paracentral lobule
   - Left postcentral gyrus
   - Right postcentral gyrus
   - Right precentral gyrus
   - Left precentral gyrus

4. **Limbic System**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Bilateral ACC
   - Median cingulate cortex
   - Midcingulate cortex
   - Amygdala

5. **Striatum**
   - Right striatum
   - Left striatum
   - Caudate nucleus
   - Putamen

6. **Insula**
   - Right insula
   - Left insula

7. **Cerebellum**
   - Left cerebellum

8. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Right cuneus cortex
   - Right fusiform gyrus

9. **Visual and Auditory Cortices**
   - Primary sensory cortex
   - Visual cortices
   - Auditory cortices

10. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Postcentral gyrus
    - Sensorimotor regions

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including perception, motor control, cognitive function, self-awareness, emotion, directed effort, mentalizing, and sensorimotor processing.
