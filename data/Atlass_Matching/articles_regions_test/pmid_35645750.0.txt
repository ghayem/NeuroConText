## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Anterior Insula (aINS)**
   - Mutually inhibited modules

2. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Submodule FS
   - Submodule D1
   - Submodule D2
   - Submodule R

3. **Primary Auditory Area**
   - Brodmann Area 41
   - Transverse temporal gyrus

4. **Medial Temporal Lobe (MTL)**
   - Entorhinal cortex (EC)

5. **Superior Temporal Cortex (ST)**
   - Superior temporal gyrus
   - Superior temporal sulcus
   - Rostral supratemporal plane

6. **Inferior Temporal Cortex (IT)**

7. **Visual Cortex**
   - V1/V2
   - V4

8. **Auditory Cortex**
   - A1
   - A2
   - ST (superior temporal cortex)
   - MTL (medial temporal lobe)

9. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Superior frontal gyrus

10. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Medial temporal lobe (MTL)
    - Superior temporal cortex (ST)

11. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Inferior parietal lobule

12. **Occipital Lobe**
    - V1/V2 (primary visual cortex)
    - V4 (visual association cortex)

13. **Thalamus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including attention, working memory, and sensory processing.
