## The brain regions:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex
   - Precuneus/posterior cingulate gyrus
   - Medial temporal lobe
   - Inferior parietal lobe
   - Lateral parietal gyri
   - Inferior/middle temporal gyri
   - Cerebellar areas
   - Brainstem regions
   - Posterior cingulate gyrus/precuneus
   - Right middle frontal gyrus
   - Bilateral medial prefrontal gyrus
   - Right parahippocampal gyrus/fusiform gyrus
   - Right precuneus

2. **White Matter (WM)**

3. **Gray Matter (GM)**
   - Gray matter/white matter (GM/WM) border
   - Gray matter/cerebrospinal fluid (GM/CSF) border

4. **Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF)**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: endogenous cognitive activity such as self-referential thoughts, (visual) memory retrieval, intrinsic memory, and unconstrained “top‐down” processing of endogenous or “intrinsic” memories and attention.
