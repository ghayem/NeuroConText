## The brain regions:

1. **Cerebellum**
   - Cerebellar grey matter
   - Cerebellar white matter

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Left frontal cortex
   - Prefrontal cortex

3. **Hippocampus**
   - Left hippocampal volume
   - Right hippocampal volume
   - Dentate gyrus

4. **Amygdala**
   - Nucleus accumbens
   - Basolateral amygdala

5. **Ventral Striatum**
   - Nucleus accumbens

6. **Thalamus**
   - Left thalamus

7. **Lingual Gyrus**
   - Right lingual gyrus

8. **Precuneus**
   - Right precuneus

9. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**

10. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

11. **Anterior Insula**

12. **Ventral Tegmental Area**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions and processes, including reward processing, emotional regulation, memory, and executive functions.
