## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
    - Ventrolateral PFC (VLPFC)
    - Dorsolateral PFC (DLPFC)

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

3. **Posterior Parietal Cortex (PPC)**

4. **Posterior Parietal Lobe (PPL)**

5. **Paracentral Lobules**
    - Left paracentral lobule (lLP)
    - Right paracentral lobule (rLP)

6. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (MPFC)**

7. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

8. **Frontal Eye Fields**
    - Left frontal eye field
    - Right frontal eye field

9. **Intraparietal Sulcus**
    - Left intraparietal sulcus
    - Right intraparietal sulcus (rIPS)

10. **Lateral Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Left lateral prefrontal cortex
    - Right lateral prefrontal cortex (rLPFC)

11. **Anterior Insula**
    - Left anterior insula
    - Right anterior insula

12. **Rostrolateral Prefrontal Cortices**
    - Left rostrolateral prefrontal cortex
    - Right rostrolateral prefrontal cortex

13. **Supramarginal Gyri**
    - Left supramarginal gyrus
    - Right supramarginal gyrus

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including working memory, attention, and executive control.
