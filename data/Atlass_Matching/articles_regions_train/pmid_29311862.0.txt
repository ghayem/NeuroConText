## The brain regions:

1. **Parahippocampal Cortex (PHC)**

2. **Retrosplenial Cortex (RSC)**
   - Brodmann Areas (BA 29 and 30)

3. **Lateral Occipital Cortex**

4. **Inferior Parietal Lobule**
   - Inferior Parietal Lobe (IPL)

5. **Frontal Cortex**

6. **Parietal Cortex**

7. **Fusiform Gyrus**

8. **Precuneus**

9. **Cerebellum**

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: object recognition, context processing, episodic memory, spatial processing, and autobiographical memory.
