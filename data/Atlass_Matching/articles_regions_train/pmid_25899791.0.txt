## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex**

3. **Fusiform Gyrus**
   - Fusiform face area (FFA)

4. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**

5. **Insula**

6. **Thalamus**

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: implicit responses, emotional processing, face recognition, and implicit racial bias.
