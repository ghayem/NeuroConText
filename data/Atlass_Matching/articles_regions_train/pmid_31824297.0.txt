## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Sensorimotor Cortex**
   - Contralateral sensorimotor cortex
   - Ipsilateral sensorimotor cortex

2. **Premotor Cortex (PMC)**
   - Lateral premotor area
   - Bilateral dorsal premotor area
   - Left premotor cortex (PMC.L)
   - Right premotor cortex (PMC.R)

3. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

4. **Primary Motor Cortex (M1)**
   - Left primary motor cortex (M1.L)
   - Right primary motor cortex (M1.R)

5. **Cerebellum**

6. **Basal Ganglia**

These regions are involved in motor functions, cognitive functions, and motor imagery.
