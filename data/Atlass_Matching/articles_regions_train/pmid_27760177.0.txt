## The brain regions:

1. **Middle Temporal Gyrus**
   - Left middle temporal gyrus (MTG)

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate

3. **Superior Parietal Lobule**
   - Superior parietal lobule
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)

4. **Inferior Parietal Lobule**
   - Right inferior parietal lobule

5. **Precuneus**
   - Right precuneus

6. **Occipito-temporal Gyrus**
   - Right occipito-temporal gyrus

7. **Posterior Cingulate Cortex**
   - Right posterior cingulate

8. **Angular Gyrus**
   - Right angular gyrus (ANG)

9. **Lateral Orbitofrontal Cortex**
   - Left lateral orbitofrontal

10. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex**
    - Medial prefrontal cortex

11. **Frontal Gyrus**
    - Left superior frontal gyrus

12. **Lingual Gyrus**
    - Lingual gyrus

13. **Inferior Frontal Lobe**
    - Left inferior frontal lobe

14. **Fusiform Gyrus**
    - Fusiform gyrus (FFG)

15. **Insula**
    - Right insula (INS)

16. **Median Cingulate**
    - Median cingulate and paracingulate gyri (DCG)

17. **Calcarine Fissure**
    - Calcarine fissure and surrounding cortex (CAL)

18. **Superior Parietal Gyrus**
    - Superior parietal gyrus (SPG)

19. **Temporal Pole**
    - Temporal pole: middle temporal gyrus (TPOmid)

20. **Supramarginal Gyrus**
    - Inferior parietal, but supramarginal and angular gyri (IPL)

These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: language, memory, executive functions, creativity, and semantic processing.
