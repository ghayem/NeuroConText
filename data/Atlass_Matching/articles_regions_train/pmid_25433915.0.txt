Sure, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Visual Cortex**
   - Primary visual cortex (V1)
   - Early visual cortex
   - Extrastriate cortex

2. **Motion Area**
   - Human motion area V5/MT+
   - Middle temporal area (MT)
   - Medial superior temporal area (MST)

3. **Subcortical Brain Regions**
   - Thalamus
   - Lateral geniculate nucleus
   - Pulvinar
   - Striatum
   - Superior colliculi

4. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Medial and lateral regions of the frontal pole

5. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal lobule
   - Posterior cingulate gyrus

6. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Insula

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions related to visual processing, motion perception, and higher-level cognitive functions.
