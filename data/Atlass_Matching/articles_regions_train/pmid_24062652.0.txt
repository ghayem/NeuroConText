## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Left Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
    - Pars triangularis
    - Pars orbitalis

2. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Temporal pole
    - Middle temporal gyrus
    - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
    - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)

3. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Angular gyrus
    - Parietal and premotor areas

4. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Medial superior frontal gyrus
    - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
    - Precentral gyrus

5. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Occipital brain regions

6. **Limbic System**
    - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

7. **Cerebellum**
    - Left cerebellum

8. **Other Regions**
    - Supramarginal gyrus
    - Fusiform gyrus
    - Temporo-parietal regions

- These regions are involved in these cognitive functions: language, memory, semantic processing, social/emotional processing, and motoric or semantic processes.
