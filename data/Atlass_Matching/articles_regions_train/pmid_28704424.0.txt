Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article, along with their associated cognitive functions:

1. **Frontal Cortex**
    - Prefrontal cortex (left and right)
        - Dorsolateral prefrontal areas (inferior, middle, and superior frontal gyri)
        - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex
        - Anterior prefrontal cortex (frontal pole, Brodmann area 10)
    - Motor areas (left and right)
    - Premotor cortex
    - Supplementary motor area (SMA)
    - Middle cingulate gyrus
    - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
    - Medial frontal areas
    - Orbitofrontal cortex

2. **Parietal Cortex**
    - Parietal lobule (left and right)
        - Inferior parietal lobule (angular gyrus, supramarginal gyrus, postcentral gyrus)
    - Precuneus
    - Posterior parietal cortex

3. **Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal gyrus (left and right)
    - Middle temporal gyrus (left and right)
    - Inferior temporal gyrus (left and right)
    - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)
    - Fusiform gyrus (left and right)
    - Medial temporal lobe
        - Parahippocampal gyrus (left and right)
        - Hippocampus

4. **Occipital Cortex**
    - Visual areas (left and right)
        - Primary visual cortex (V1)
        - Secondary visual cortex (V2)
        - Middle occipital gyrus (left and right)
        - Inferior occipital gyrus (left and right)
        - Cuneus (right)
        - Calcarine gyrus (left)
    - Occipito-temporal areas
        - Inferior occipital gyrus (left and right)
        - Fusiform gyri (left and right)
    - Extrastriate areas

5. **Subcortical Structures**
    - Caudate nucleus (left)
    - Pulvinar (right)
    - Cerebellum (left and right)

6. **Other Regions**
    - Insula
    - Middle cingulate cortex
    - Precentral gyrus
    - Temporo-parietal junction

**Associated Cognitive Functions:**
- Working memory (encoding, maintenance, retrieval)
- Executive functions (attentional control, attentional focusing)
- Visual processing (perceptual shape analysis, face processing)
- Verbal processing (phonological processing, articulatory rehearsal)
- Language networks
- Memory storage and retrieval
- Attentional refreshing and control
- Task switching and coordination
- Interference resolution and conflict monitoring
