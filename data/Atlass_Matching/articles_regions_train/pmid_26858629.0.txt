## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Posterior Medial Frontal Cortex (pMFC)**
   - Dorsal medial prefrontal cortex (dMPFC)
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
   - Pre-supplementary motor area (pre-SMA)

2. **Anterior Insula (AI)**

3. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**

4. **Lateral Orbitofrontal Cortex (LOFC)**

5. **Angular Gyrus (AG)**

- These regions are involved in cognitive dissonance, conflict monitoring, reinforcement learning, and cognitive control.
