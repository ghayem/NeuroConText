## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Anterior temporal lobe
    - Temporal pole
    - Parahippocampal gyrus
    - Temporoparietal cortices

2. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Inferior parietal cortex
    - Right parietal lobe

3. **Frontal Lobe**

4. **Frontotemporal Lobe**
    - Frontotemporal lobar degeneration (FTLD)
    - Frontotemporal atrophy
    - Right anterior temporal lobe
    - Left anterior temporal lobe
    - Temporal lobe variant FTLD

5. **Mesial Temporal Lobe**
    - Amygdala
    - Hippocampus
    - Entorhinal cortex

6. **Fusiform Gyrus**
    - Anterior fusiform gyrus

7. **Hippocampal Region**
    - Disproportionate symmetrical hippocampal atrophy

8. **Cerebral Regions**
    - Generalized cerebral atrophy

9. **Other Cortical Areas**
    - Right inferior parietal cortex
    - Right parahippocampal gyrus
    - Left inferior parietal cortex

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including voice processing, semantic knowledge, person recognition, memory, and executive functions.
