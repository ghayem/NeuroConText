## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned:

1. **Prefrontal brain areas**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex

2. **Motor cortex**
   - Primary motor cortex

3. **Supplementary motor area (SMA)**

4. **Frontal cortical regions**
   - Ventral premotor cortex (vPMC)
   - Left ventral premotor cortex (left vPMC)
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Inferior frontal cortex
   - Frontal cortex

5. **Cerebellum**
   - Cerebellar region VIIb

6. **Temporal cortex**
   - Left superior temporal gyrus
   - Inferior temporal cortex

7. **Parietal cortex**
   - Left interior parietal lobe

8. **Insula**
   - Right insula

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions and motor control, including movement initiation, planning, and execution, as well as emotional and cognitive processing.
