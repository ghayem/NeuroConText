Based on the article, the following brain regions and sub-regions are mentioned:

1. **Inferior Frontal Cortex (IFC)**
    - Bilateral inferior frontal cortices

2. **Posterior Superior Temporal Sulcus (pSTS)**
    - Bilateral posterior superior temporal sulci (pSTS)
    - Bilateral pSTS/planum temporale
    - Bilateral pSTS/posterior superior temporal gyrus (pSTG)

3. **Transverse Temporal Gyrus (TTG)**
    - Bilateral transverse temporal gyri (also known as Heschl's gyrus)

4. **Anterior Insula**
    - Left anterior insula
    - Right anterior insula

5. **Fusiform Gyrus**
    - Left fusiform gyrus

6. **Planum Temporale**
    - Bilateral planum temporale

7. **Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)**
    - Bilateral superior temporal gyri
    - Right posterior superior temporal gyrus (pSTG)

8. **Thalamus**

9. **Basal Ganglia**

10. **Cerebellum**

11. **Claustrum**

12. **Temporal Poles**
    - Anterior temporal poles

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including multisensory processing, auditory and visual perception, social cognition, and semantic knowledge representation.
