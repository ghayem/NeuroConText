## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Hippocampus**
2. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
3. **Amygdala**
4. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**
   - Left parahippocampal gyrus
5. **Cerebellum**
   - Right cerebellum
6. **Middle Frontal Gyrus (MFG)**
   - Left middle frontal gyrus
   - Right middle frontal gyrus
7. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG)**
   - Left inferior frontal gyrus (pars opercularis)
   - Left inferior frontal gyrus (pars triangularis)
8. **Insula**
   - Left insula

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as emotional regulation, memory, stress response, and executive functions.
