## Cognitive Functions:
These regions are involved in the following cognitive functions:
- **Arithmetic Operations**: Mental calculation and digit memory tasks.
- **Visual Imagery**: Mental abacus strategy.
- **Verbal Working Memory**: Language-related cognitive tasks.
- **Visuospatial Working Memory**: Mental abacus and digit memory tasks.
- **Motor Function**: Hand grip tasks and motor control.
- **Language Processing**: Verbal fluency tasks and speech production.
