Based on the provided neuroscientific article, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Default Mode Network (DMN)**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Left hippocampus (L-Hipp)
   - Right hippocampus (R-Hipp)
   - Thalamus (Thal)
   - Right inferior parietal (R-IP)
   - Left inferior parietal (L-IP)
   - Left inferior parietal-2 (L-IP(2))
   - Anterior cingulate gyrus (ACG)
   - Right middle frontal gyrus (R-MFG)
   - Left middle frontal gyrus (L-MFG)
   - Precuneus (Prec)
   - Cerebellum (Cer)

2. **Other Regions**
   - Left/Right insula
   - Left/Right dorsolateral prefrontal cortex
   - Left/Right supramarginal gyrus
   - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including but not limited to:
- Self-referential processing (DMN)
- Episodic memory (Hippocampus)
- Emotion regulation (Anterior cingulate gyrus)
- Language and speech (Supramarginal gyrus)
- Executive functions (Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex)
- Sensory and motor functions (Cerebellum)
