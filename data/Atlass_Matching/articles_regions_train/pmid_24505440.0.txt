Based on the neuroscientific article provided, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Orbital and medial prefrontal cortex (OMPFC)
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Orbital prefrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Dorsal regions of the superior frontal gyrus (BA 10)
   - Left frontal pole (BA 10)

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (sgACC) (BA 24, 32)
   - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC) (BA 24, 32)

3. **Striatum**
   - Ventral striatum
   - Nucleus accumbens
   - Caudate (bilateral)

4. **Motor Cortex**
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA) (BA 6)

5. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Ventral occipital cortex (BA 18, 19)

6. **Thalamus**
   - Right thalamus

7. **Hippocampus**
   - Right hippocampus
   - Parahippocampal gyrus

8. **Cerebellum**
   - Left superior cerebellum (left crus 1 and left lobule VI)

9. **Other Regions**
   - Ventral tegmental area (VTA)
   - Substantia nigra
   - Amygdala
   - Insula
   - Default mode network (DMN)
   - Salience network
   - Fronto-parietal network
   - Motor network
   - Visual network

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions and processes, including reward processing, decision-making, memory, emotion regulation, and motor control. They are also implicated in drug craving and addiction.
