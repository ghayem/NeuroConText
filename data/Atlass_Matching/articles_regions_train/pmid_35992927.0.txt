## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Trigeminal Cervical Complex (TCC)**
    - Rostral Ventromedial Medulla (RVM)

2. **Thalamus**

3. **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**

4. **Temporal Gyrus**
    - Superior temporal gyrus (TSG)
    - Middle temporal gyrus (TMG)

5. **Middle Cingulate Cortex (MCC)**

6. **Precuneus**

7. **Postcentral Gyrus**

8. **Insula**

9. **Frontal Middle Gyrus (FMG)**

10. **Frontal Superior Gyrus (FSG)**

11. **Orbitofrontal Gyrus (OFG)**

12. **Post Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

13. **Supplementary Motor Area (SMA)**

- These regions are involved in trigeminal spinal-thalamo-cortical pathways, default mode network (DMN), auditory network, and descending pain modulation system (DPMS).
