## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Auditory Pathway**
   - **Cochlear Nucleus (CN)**
   - **Inferior Colliculus (IC)**
   - **Medial Geniculate Body of the Thalamus (MGB)**
   - **Primary Auditory Cortex (PAC)**
     - **TE1.0**
     - **TE1.1**
     - **TE1.2**
   - **Auditory Association Cortex (AAC)**
     - **Superior Temporal Gyrus (BA 22)**

2. **Limbic Regions**
   - **Nucleus Accumbens**
   - **Amygdala**
   - **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)**

3. **Cerebellum**
   - **Vermis of the Cerebellum**
   - **Paraflocculus of the Cerebellum**

4. **Other Regions**
   - **Thalamic Reticular Nucleus (TRN)**
   - **Brainstem**
   - **Auditory Cortex**
   - **Temporo-parieto-occipital Junction (TPO)**

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as auditory processing, emotional evaluation, conscious perception, and motor control.
