Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Amygdala**
   - Right amygdala
   - Left amygdala

2. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Anteromedial temporal lobe
   - Inferior temporal lobe/gyrus fusiformis
   - Medial temporal gyri (posterior portion)

3. **Visual Cortex**

4. **Precentral Gyrus**
   - Right precentral gyrus

5. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
   - Right inferior frontal gyrus

6. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**
   - Medial orbitofrontal cortex

7. **Pallidum/Caudate**

8. **Fusiform Gyrus**

9. **Parahippocampal Cortex**

10. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus/Frontal Operculum**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including:
- Emotional salience detection and processing (amygdala)
- Face recognition (fusiform gyrus)
- Visual processing (visual cortex)
- Motor planning and execution (precentral gyrus)
- Language processing and production (inferior frontal gyrus)
- Decision-making and reward processing (orbitofrontal cortex)
- Memory and spatial navigation (parahippocampal cortex)
- Motor control and cognitive functions (pallidum/caudate)
