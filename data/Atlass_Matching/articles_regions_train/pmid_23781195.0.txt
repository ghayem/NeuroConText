## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Striatum**
   - Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)
   - Ventral caudate (vCaud)
   - Dorsal caudate (dCaud)
   - Ventral anterior putamen (vaPut)
   - Dorsal anterior putamen (daPut)
   - Posterior putamen (pPut)

2. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Orbitomedial prefrontal cortex (omPFC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Anterior cingulate cortex

3. **Midbrain**
   - Ventral tegmental area (VTA)
   - Substantia nigra (SN)

4. **Sensorimotor Cortices**
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Postcentral gyrus

5. **Ventral Visual Stream**
   - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG)

6. **Other Regions**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
   - Lateral prefrontal cortex (PFC)

## Cognitive Functions:

- **Striatum**: Involved in processing rewards and punishments, combining motor and affective information.
- **Frontal Cortex**: Involved in reward processing, decision-making, and emotional regulation.
- **Midbrain**: Involved in dopaminergic pathways, crucial for reward processing and motivation.
- **Sensorimotor Cortices**: Involved in motor planning and execution.
- **Ventral Visual Stream**: Involved in visual processing and object recognition.
