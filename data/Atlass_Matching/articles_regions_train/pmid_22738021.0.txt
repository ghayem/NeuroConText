Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Hippocampus**

3. **Hypothalamus**
   - Anterior hypothalamus

4. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC)
     - Ventral medial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)

5. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
     - Dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC)
     - Rostral anterior cingulate cortex (rACC)

6. **Brain-stem nuclei**
   - Periaqueductal gray

7. **Insula**

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including fear responses, inhibition and extinction of fear, stress responses, arousal, and emotion regulation.
