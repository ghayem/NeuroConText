## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Anterior Insula (AI)**

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

3. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (VLPFC)

4. **Ventral Striatum**

5. **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (VMPFC)**

6. **Retrosplenial Cortex**

7. **Right Insula**

8. **Left Ventral Striatum**

9. **Prefrontal Cortex**

10. **Right Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)**
    - Left Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC)

11. **Right Ventral Striatum**

12. **Left Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (VMPFC)**

13. **Right Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (VMPFC)**

14. **Superior Temporal Cortex**
    - Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)
    - Middle superior temporal cortex
    - Anterior portion of the pSTG/S

15. **Left Posterior Superior Temporal Cortex**
    - Superior temporal plane (STP)
    - Planum temporale (PT)
    - Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)
    - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as emotional processing, decision-making, reward processing, and social cognition.
