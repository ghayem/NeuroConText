Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC) (BA 46)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG) (BA 44)
   - Premotor cortex (PMC) (BA 6)

2. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex
   - Subgenual cingulate cortex
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC) (BA 23)

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Superior parietal lobule (SPL) (BA 7)
   - Supramarginal gyrus (SMG) (BA 40)
   - Precuneus (BA 7)

4. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Inferior temporal gyrus (ITG) (BA 20)
   - Fusiform gyrus (BA 37)

5. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Lingual gyrus (BA 19)

6. **Limbic System**
   - Amygdala
   - Parahippocampal gyrus (PHG) (BA 36)

7. **Insular Cortex**
   - Insula

8. **Subcortical Structures**
   - Thalamus

9. **Cerebellum**
   - Cerebellum posterior lobe (CPL)

10. **Other Networks**
    - Default mode network (DMN)
    - Cognitive control network
    - Affective network

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional functions, including but not limited to:
- Executive control and emotion regulation (DLPFC, SPL)
- Emotion recognition and processing (IFG, amygdala, insula)
- Motor preparation and control (PMC)
- Self-referential thought and memory retrieval (DMN, including PCC and precuneus)
- Visual recognition and processing (lingual gyrus, fusiform gyrus)
- Spatial processing and emotional regulation (precuneus, PHG)
- Language and memory processing (temporal lobe structures)
- Sensorimotor and cognitive processing (cerebellum)
