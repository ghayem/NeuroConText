## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions Mentioned in the Article:

1. **Frontoparietal Network**
   - Frontoparietal regions
   - Premotor cortex
   - Dorsal premotor areas (dPM)
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Precentral gyrus
   - Posterior parietal regions
   - Parietal opercula
   - Supplementary motor area

2. **Motor and Somatosensory Regions**
   - Primary motor cortex
   - Medial premotor cortex
   - Lateral premotor cortex
   - Somatosensory cortex
   - Vestibular cortex

3. **Visual and Visuo-Motor Regions**
   - Occipital lobe
   - Parietal lobe
   - Visual cortex
   - Visuo-motor areas

4. **Subcortical Regions**
   - Basal ganglia
   - Mesencephalic regions
   - Thalamus
   - Cerebellum

5. **Other Cortical Regions**
   - Insula
   - Middle cingulum
   - Paracentral lobule
   - Supramarginal gyrus

6. **Frontal Lobe Regions**
   - Prefrontal cortex
   - Median wall regions

These regions are involved in various cognitive and motor functions, including motor control, navigation, obstacle negotiation, gait motor control, and the adaptation of gait patterns to environmental cues.
