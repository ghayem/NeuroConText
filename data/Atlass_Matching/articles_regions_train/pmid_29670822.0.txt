## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Cortical Midline Structures (CMS)**
    - **Medial Prefrontal Cortex (mPFC)**
      - Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)
      - Dorsomedial Prefrontal Cortex (dmPFC)
    - **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
    - **Posterior Cingulate Cortex (PCC)**

2. **Left Postcentral Gyrus (PCG)**

3. **Right Orbitofrontal Cortex (OFC)**
    - Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)

4. **Right Lateral Prefrontal Cortex (lPFC)**

5. **Right Hippocampus**

6. **Right Amygdala**

7. **Left Hypothalamus**

8. **Left Pars Orbitalis**

9. **Right Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

10. **Left Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (dlPFC)**

11. **Right Middle Frontal Gyrus**

12. **Insula**

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions such as self-referential processing, emotion regulation, and socio-cognitive abilities.
