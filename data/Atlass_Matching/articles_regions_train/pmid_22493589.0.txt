1. **Somato-sensory Cortex**

2. **Visual Cortex**

4. **Reticular Thalamic Cells**

14. **Caudate Nuclei**

   - Temporo-parieto-occipital junction (TPO)

18. **Occipital Cortex**

19. **Temporal Cortex**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including sensory processing, sleep regulation, and higher-order cognitive processes such as attention and memory.
