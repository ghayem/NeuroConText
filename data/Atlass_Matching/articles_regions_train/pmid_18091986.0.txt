Sure, here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the article:

1. **Anterior Insula**
   - Bilateral anterior insula
   - Right middle insula
   - Posterior insula
   - Mid- and posterior insular cortices

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**
   - Anterior medial cingulate cortex (aMCC)
   - Dorsal and ventral aMCC
   - Subcallosal ACC
   - Perigenual ACC
   - Rostral aMCC
   - Ventral posterior MCC (vpMCC)
   - Dorsal anterior MCC (daMCC)

3. **Somatosensory Cortex**
   - Primary somatosensory cortex (SI)
   - Secondary somatosensory cortex (SII)
   - Posterior insula
   - Postcentral gyrus
   - Area 1 and Area 2
   - Area OP4

4. **Frontal Cortex**
   - Ventral premotor cortex
   - Dorsal premotor cortex
   - Inferior frontal gyri (pars opercularis, Area 44, pars triangularis, Area 45)
   - Medial frontal gyrus
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Medial orbitofrontal cortex (mOFC)
   - Right lateral prefrontal cortex

5. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal cortex
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Inferior parietal lobule
   - Intraparietal sulcus (Area hlP2)
   - Inferior frontal gyri (pars opercularis, Area 44)
   - Superior parietal lobe
   - Lateral precuneus
   - Posterior precuneus (pPRC)
   - Anterior precuneus (aPRC)
   - Temporo-parietal junction (TPJ)

6. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Anterior inferior temporal gyrus
   - Middle and anterior inferior temporal gyrus
   - Temporal poles
   - Superior temporal gyrus

7. **Occipital Cortex**
   - Right lingual gyrus
   - Visual cortex

8. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Striatum
   - Caudate nucleus
   - Putamen

9. **Thalamus**

10. **Cerebellum**

11. **Periaqueductal Grey**
    - Superior part of the periaqueductal grey

12. **Other Regions**
    - Hippocampus
    - Fusiform gyrus
    - Precuneus
    - Thalamus
    - Right pre- and postcentral gyrus (Areas 3 and 4)

These regions are involved in various cognitive and emotional processes, including empathy, pain perception, sensory discrimination, affective-motivational processing, and cognitive appraisal.
