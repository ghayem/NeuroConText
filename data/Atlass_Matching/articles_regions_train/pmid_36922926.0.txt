Certainly! Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Inferior temporal gyrus

2. **Occipital Lobe**
   - Middle occipital gyrus
   - Superior occipital gyrus

3. **Parietal Lobe**
   - Superior parietal gyrus
   - Postcentral gyrus
   - Precuneus

4. **Frontal Lobe**
   - Middle frontal gyrus
   - Superior frontal gyrus
   - Inferior frontal gyrus

5. **Cerebellum-Thalamus-Cortex Loop**
   - Cerebellum
   - Thalamus
   - Cortex

6. **Insula**

7. **Subcortical Regions**
   - Caudate
   - Putamen

8. **Other Regions**
   - Hippocampus
   - Perirhinal cortex
   - Parietal hippocampal cortex

- These regions are involved in cognitive functions including memory, attention, executive functions, visual-spatial and executive functions, naming, language, abstraction, delayed recall, orientation, and spatial memory.
