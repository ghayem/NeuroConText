### Cognitive Functions
These regions are involved in these cognitive functions:
- Memory formation and retrieval
- Associative memory processing
- Multimodal information integration
- Attentional control and processing
- Contextual and thematic memory schemata
- Recollection and updating of episodic representations
- Incidental learning and explicit memory
- Bottom-up and top-down attention processes
- Goal-directed behavior and interference resolution
- Emotional salience detection and affective learning
