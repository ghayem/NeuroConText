## Cognitive Functions:

These regions are involved in the following cognitive functions:
- Reward processing
- Reward anticipation
- Reward receipt
- Neural sensitivity to reward
- Loss anticipation
- Reward magnitude modulation
- Individual differences in neural response to reward
- Risk-taking behaviors
- Emotional regulation
