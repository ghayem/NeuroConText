1. **Auditory Cortex**
   - Auditory cortex of marmoset monkeys

2. **Right Hemisphere**
   - Middle Temporal Gyrus (MTG)
     - Posterior division of the Middle Temporal Gyrus
   - Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG)
     - Posterior division of the Superior Temporal Gyrus
   - Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)
   - Temporo-parietal junction (TPJ)

3. **Left Hemisphere**
   - Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)
   - Temporo-parietal junction (TPJ)

4. **Right Hemisphere Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)**
   - Selectivity for slow temporal cues (200–300 ms)

5. **Left Hemisphere Superior Temporal Sulcus (STS)**
   - Selectivity for rapid temporal cues (25–30 ms)

6. **Medial boundary of the STG and MTG**

These regions are involved in auditory processing, specifically the auditory steady-state response (ASSR), and the processing of temporal cues in speech and other auditory stimuli.
