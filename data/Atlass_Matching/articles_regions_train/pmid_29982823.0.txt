## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Reward Network**
    - **Dorsal Striatum**
        - Caudate
        - Putamen
    - **Ventral Striatum**
        - Nucleus accumbens (NAcc)
    - **Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortex (vmPFC)**
    - **Ventral Tegmental Area (VTA)**

2. **Visual Attention and Processing**
    - **Primary Visual Cortex**
    - **Occipital Cortex**
        - Lateral occipital cortex
        - Fusiform cortex
        - Temporooccipital cortex
    - **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
    - **Bilateral Intraparietal Sulcus**

3. **Emotional and Cognitive Processing**
    - **Thalamus**
    - **Bilateral Insula**
    - **Orbitofrontal Cortex**
    - **Hippocampus**
    - **Amygdala**
    - **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
    - **Paracingulate Cortex**
    - **Dorsal Anterior Cingulate Cortex (dACC)**

4. **Motor and Sensory Processing**
    - **Primary Motor Cortex**
    - **Supplementary Motor Cortex**
    - **Cerebellum**

5. **Default Mode Network**
    - **Precuneus**
    - **Bilateral Temporoparietal Junction**

6. **Salience Network**
    - **Anterior Insula**
    - **Dorsal Anterior Cingulate Cortex (dACC)**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including reward processing, social cognition, self-referential thought, visual attention, emotional processing, and decision-making.
