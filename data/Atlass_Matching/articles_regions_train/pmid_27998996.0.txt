## Brain Regions and Sub-regions:

1. **Amygdala**

2. **Ventral Striatum**

3. **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)**
   - Ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (VLPFC)
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (VMPFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Dorsomedial prefrontal cortex (DMPFC)
   - Superior frontal gyrus (SFG)
   - Medial frontal gyrus (medFG)
   - Middle frontal gyrus (MFG)
   - Inferior frontal gyrus (IFG)
   - Pregenual anterior cingulate cortex (pgACC)
   - Subgenual anterior cingulate cortex (sgACC)

4. **Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior temporal gyrus (STG)
   - Middle temporal gyrus (MTG)
   - Fusiform gyrus

5. **Parietal Cortex**
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
   - Precuneus

6. **Occipital Cortex**

7. **Insula**

8. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**

9. **Basal Ganglia**
   - Caudate nucleus

10. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

11. **Pre-central Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions such as emotion regulation, memory, attention, self-reflection, semantic memory, and language.
