## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Parietal Lobe**
    - Parietal areas
    - Superior parietal lobule (part of the precuneus)

2. **Frontal Lobe**
    - Frontal areas
    - Superior frontal gyrus
    - Left medial superior frontal gyrus
    - Right superior frontal gyrus

3. **Temporal Lobe**
    - Middle temporal gyrus (bilaterally)
    - Left middle temporal gyrus
    - Right middle temporal gyrus

4. **Occipital Lobe**
    - Visual areas (cortical thickness)

5. **Cingulate Gyrus**
    - Posterior/middle cingulate gyrus (bilaterally)

6. **Thalamus**

7. **Angular Gyrus**
    - Right angular gyrus
    - Left angular gyrus

8. **Amygdala**
    - Right amygdala

9. **Parahippocampal Gyrus**
    - Right parahippocampal gyrus

10. **Perirhinal Cortex**

11. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**

12. **Precentral Gyrus**
    - Medial segment of the precentral gyrus (bilaterally)

13. **Corpus Callosum**

14. **Anterior Commissure**

15. **Hypothalamic Regions**

16. **Precuneus**

17. **Supplementary Motor Cortex**

18. **Premotor Cortex**

19. **Default Mode Network**

20. **Posterior/Middle Cingulate Gyrus**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including mental rotation, spatial cognition, memory, and executive functions.
