Here are the brain regions and sub-regions mentioned in the neuroscientific article:

1. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Ventral lateral prefrontal cortex
   - Dorsal lateral prefrontal cortex
   - Right inferior frontal gyrus (rIFG)
   - Right middle frontal gyrus (rMFG)
   - Left frontal pole (lFP)
   - Mesial prefrontal cortex

2. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
   - Left anterior cingulate cortex (lACC)

3. **Posterior Parietal Cortex**
   - Right supramarginal gyrus (rSMG)

4. **Cognitive Control Network (CCN)**
   - Includes regions involved in cognitive control over emotional stimuli

5. **Fronto-limbic Network**

6. **Default Mode Network**

These regions are involved in cognitive control, particularly control over mood-congruent information, attentional control, behavioral inhibition, suppression of unwanted thoughts, attention shifting, and efforts to reappraise emotional stimuli.
