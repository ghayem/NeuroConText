### Brain Regions and Sub-Regions

1. **Peri-Sylvian Network**
   - This region is involved in language processing and is particularly affected in nfvPPA.

2. **Anterior Temporal Lobe Network**
   - This region is involved in semantic memory and is particularly affected in svPPA.

3. **Temporo-Parietal Junction**
   - This region is involved in auditory verbal working memory and is particularly affected in lvPPA.

4. **Heschl's Gyrus**
   - This region is involved in primary auditory processing.

5. **Superior Temporal Gyrus**
   - This region is involved in auditory processing and language comprehension.

6. **Posterior Superior Temporal Cortex**
   - Planum temporale
   - Posterior superior temporal gyrus and sulcus (pSTG/S)
   - This region is involved in phonemic processing.

7. **Medial Heschl's Gyrus**
   - This region is involved in auditory processing and is particularly affected in nfvPPA.

8. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex**
   - This region is involved in temporal pattern integration and speech production.

9. **Supplementary Motor Cortex**
   - This region is involved in motor planning and is particularly affected in nfvPPA.

10. **Caudate Nucleus**
    - This region is involved in sequence entropy processing and is particularly affected in svPPA.

11. **Orbitofrontal Cortex**
    - This region is involved in signal information content processing and is particularly affected in svPPA.

12. **Inferior Frontal Gyrus**
    - This region is involved in speech signal entropy processing and is particularly affected in svPPA.

13. **Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex**
    - This region is involved in phonemic structure processing and is particularly affected in lvPPA.

14. **Precuneus**
    - This region is involved in auditory stimulation and is particularly affected in healthy controls.

15. **Temporo-Parieto-Occipital Junction**
    - This region is involved in auditory stimulation and is particularly affected in nfvPPA.

16. **Posterior Inferior Temporal Sulcus**
    - This region is involved in auditory stimulation and is particularly affected in svPPA.

17. **Posterior Temporo-Parietal Cortices**
    - This region is involved in auditory stimulation and is particularly affected in healthy controls, nfvPPA, and svPPA.

18. **Frontotemporal Lobar Degeneration Spectrum**
    - This region is involved in non-Alzheimer proteinopathies and is particularly affected in nfvPPA and svPPA.

19. **Dorsal Striatum**
    - This region is involved in the analysis of auditory regularity and predictability.

20. **Planum Temporale**
    - This region is involved in phonemic processing and is particularly affected in lvPPA.

### Cognitive Functions
- **Language Processing**: Peri-Sylvian network, anterior temporal lobe network, temporo-parietal junction, superior temporal gyrus, posterior superior temporal cortex.
- **Memory**: Anterior temporal lobe network.
- **Executive Functions**: Anterior cingulate cortex, supplementary motor cortex, dorsolateral prefrontal cortex.
- **Auditory Processing**: Heschl's gyrus, medial Heschl's gyrus, superior temporal gyrus, posterior superior temporal cortex, precuneus, temporo-parieto-occipital junction, posterior inferior temporal sulcus, posterior temporo-parietal cortices.
- **Phonemic Processing**: Posterior superior temporal cortex, planum temporale.
- **Sequence Entropy Processing**: Caudate nucleus, orbitofrontal cortex, inferior frontal gyrus.
- **Temporal Pattern Integration**: Anterior cingulate cortex.
- **Motor Planning**: Supplementary motor cortex.
- **Speech Production**: Anterior cingulate cortex.
- **Auditory Regularity and Predictability**: Dorsal striatum.
- **Non-Alzheimer Proteinopathies**: Frontotemporal lobar degeneration spectrum.
