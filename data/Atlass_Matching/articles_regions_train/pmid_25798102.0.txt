## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Anterior Insula**
   - Bilateral anterior insula
   - Right dorsal anterior insula
   - Left ventral anterior insula

2. **Striatum**
   - Dorsal striatum
   - Ventral striatum
   - Nucleus accumbens
   - Caudate

3. **Prefrontal Cortex**
   - Ventromedial prefrontal cortex (VMPFC)
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)

4. **Cingulate Cortex**
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)

5. **Temporal Lobe**
   - Left superior temporal pole
   - Right superior temporal pole
   - Left superior temporal gyrus

6. **Periaqueductal Gray (PAG)**

7. **Superior Temporal Cortex**
   - Superior temporal pole

## Cognitive Functions:
- Emotion and interoceptive awareness
- Perception, experience, and anticipation of emotion
- Empathy
- Risk and uncertainty processing
- Social norm violations
- Theory of mind processing
- Fairness-related decision-making
- Punishment of unfairness
- Behavioral adaptation in social contexts
- Strategic decision-making
