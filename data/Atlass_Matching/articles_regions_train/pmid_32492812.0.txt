## Brain Regions and Sub-Regions:

1. **Hypothalamus**

2. **Anterior Insula**
   - Short insula gyri (left and right)
   - Sensorimotor cortex (connected with anterior insula)
   - Prefrontal cortex (connected with anterior insula)
   - Left temporoparietal junction (TPJ)

3. **Thalamus**
   - Ventroposterior medial nucleus
   - Connected regions:
     - Insular cortex
     - Frontal cortex
     - Occipital cortex
     - Mid and posterior insula
     - Temporal cortices
     - Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)
     - Precentral cortex
     - Prefrontal cortex

4. **Anterior Cingulate Cortex (ACC)**

5. **Salience Network**
   - Anterior insular cortex (key node)

6. **Ventral Attention Network**
   - Temporoparietal junction (TPJ) (key node)

7. **Basal Ganglia**

8. **Prefrontal Cortex**

9. **Temporoparietal Junction (TPJ)**

10. **Inferior Frontal Cortex**

11. **Midfrontal Cortex**

12. **Mid-insula**
   - Dorsal mid-insular cortex

13. **Sensorimotor Cortex**

14. **Frontal Cortex**

15. **Occipital Cortex**

16. **Temporal Cortex**

17. **Precentral Cortex**

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions, including sensory processing, homeostatic regulation, gustatory experiences, and the integration of interoceptive information.
