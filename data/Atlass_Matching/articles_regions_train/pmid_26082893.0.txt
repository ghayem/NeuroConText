13. **Thalamus**
    - Thalamus

14. **Globus Pallidus**
    - Medial globus pallidus

15. **Basal Ganglia**
    - Basal ganglia

16. **Mid-brain Regions**
    - Mid-brain regions

17. **Cortico-striatal Networks**
    - Cortico-striatal networks

18. **Frontocortical Behavioral Control Centers**
    - Frontocortical behavioral control centers

19. **Fluid-Attenuated Inversion Recovery (FLAIR)**
