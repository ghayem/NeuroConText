## Brain Regions and Sub-regions

1. **Default-mode network (DMN)**
   - Posterior cingulate cortex (PCC)
   - Ventral medial prefrontal cortex (vmPFC)
   - Inferior parietal lobule (IPL)
   - Hippocampal formation (HF+)
   - Lateral temporal cortex (LTC)
   - Retrosplenial cortex (Rsp)
   - Dorsal medial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC)

2. **Dorsal attention network (DAN)**
   - Frontal eye fields (FEF)
   - Intraparietal sulcus (IPS)
   - Superior parietal lobule (SPL)
   - Middle temporal motion complex (MT+)

3. **Frontoparietal network (FPN)**
   - Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC)
   - Anterior insula (AI)
   - Anterior inferior parietal lobule (aIPL)
   - Inferior frontal junction (IFJ)

4. **Ventral attention network (VAN)**
   - Temporoparietal junction (TPJ)
   - Ventral frontal cortex (VFC)
   - Anterior insula (AI)
   - Middle and anterior cingulate cortex (MCC/ACC)

5. **Somatomotor network (SMN)**
   - Primary motor cortex (M1)
   - Primary somatosensory cortex (S1)
   - Premotor cortex (PMC)
   - Supplementary motor area (SMA)

6. **Visual network (VIS)**
   - Primary visual cortex (V1)
   - Extrastriate cortex (V2, V3, V4, V5/MT)
   - Fusiform gyrus (FG)
   - Lateral occipital cortex (LOC)

7. **Limbic network (LIMB)**
   - Amygdala
   - Hippocampus
   - Orbitofrontal cortex (OFC)
   - Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)

8. **Subcortical regions**
   - Thalamus
   - Basal ganglia (including the caudate, putamen, and globus pallidus)
   - Hypothalamus
   - Brainstem

9. **Lateral parietal regions**
   - Ventral postcentral gyrus
   - Supramarginal gyrus
   - Angular gyrus

10. **Additional specific regions**
    - Ventral area 8A (8Av)
    - Area 7Am
    - Area 7PC
    - Area 6a
    - Area 6v
    - Area PFt
    - Area FEF
    - Area 43
    - Area FOP4
    - Area 5mv
    - Area 9-46v
    - Area IFSa
    - Area VIP
    - Area FST
    - Area VMV1
    - Area V4
    - Area V3A

These regions are involved in various cognitive functions including but not limited to:

- **DMN**: Self-referential thought, autobiographical memory, and mind-wandering.
- **DAN**: Voluntary allocation of attention and working memory.
- **FPN**: Cognitive control and decision-making.
- **VAN**: Stimulus-driven attention and salience detection.
- **SMN**: Motor control and somatosensory processing.
- **VIS**: Visual processing.
- **LIMB**: Emotion regulation and memory.
- **Subcortical regions**: Motor control, emotion, and homeostatic functions.
- **Lateral parietal regions**: Integration of sensory information and cognitive processing.
- **Additional specific regions**: Various roles in attention, motor control, and sensory processing.
